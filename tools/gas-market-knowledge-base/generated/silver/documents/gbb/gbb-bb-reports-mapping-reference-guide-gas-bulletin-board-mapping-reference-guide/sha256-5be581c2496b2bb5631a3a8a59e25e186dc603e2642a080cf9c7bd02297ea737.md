## 1.0 Final September 2018

This guide provides a mapping between the report format under the Gas Bulletin Board (BB) reports published by AEMO prior to 30 September 2018, and the report format under the updated rules from 30 September 2018.

The primary audience for this document is participants retrieving BB report data who would like to gain a greater understanding of the differences between the old and new reports.
