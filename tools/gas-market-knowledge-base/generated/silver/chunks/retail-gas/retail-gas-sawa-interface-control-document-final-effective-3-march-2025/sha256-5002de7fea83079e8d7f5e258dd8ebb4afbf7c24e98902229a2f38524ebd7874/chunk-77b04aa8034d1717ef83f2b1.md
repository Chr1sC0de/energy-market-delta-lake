This report is a MP specific report that shows the MIRNs won or lost, its commissioned status and the date of the change in status. The report will be generated each day and show a rolling 30 days of data for the avoidance of doubt this report only has entries for MIRNs that  have  changed  FRO  or  status  during  the  period  and  includes  changes  arising  from  a  meter  fix,  reconnection,  disconnection, abolishment, normal transfer and error correction.
