Although there is no ebXML message layer handshaking between the low volume participant and the GRMS, it should be noted that the low volume participant is still bound by the aseXML guidelines and should send aseXML transaction acknowledgements for every transaction they receive. Similarly, they will receive a transaction acknowledgement from the end recipient for every transaction they send.
