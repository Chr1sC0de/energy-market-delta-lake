At the transaction level, the GRMS does not perform any checks to ensure that transaction acknowledgements  are  received  within  any  particular timeframe. This is in line with the AEMO view that it is a decision of the individual business how it wishes to handle transaction acknowledgements.
At the message level, acknowledgements are monitored and retries occur as defined by the FRC Hub Specification.
