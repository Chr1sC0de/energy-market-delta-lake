A gate point for a sub-network means a point (which may be the same location as a physical gate point), which is designated as the gate point under clause 1.6 of the Retail Market Procedures (South Australia) for the sub-network from a pipeline or injection  faclity and  it  is  the  sum  of  all  ' physical gate points ' from that pipeline or injection facility on a sub-network .
