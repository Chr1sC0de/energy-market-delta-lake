Files containing transactions must be compressed to allow efficient use of bandwidth.  Files containing transactions sent to and from GRMS are to be compressed by the sender prior to sending.  Compression is to be carried out using the Zlib standard (RFC 1950), which is supported by winzip, pkzip etc. All Acknowledgement files are not to be compressed.
CSV files sent to and from the GRMS are limited to a 2MB size limit when unzipped .
