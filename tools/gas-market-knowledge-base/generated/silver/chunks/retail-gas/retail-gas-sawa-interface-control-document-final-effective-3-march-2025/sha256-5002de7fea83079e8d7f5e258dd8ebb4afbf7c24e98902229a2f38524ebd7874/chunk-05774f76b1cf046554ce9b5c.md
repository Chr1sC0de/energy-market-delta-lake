The data estimation entity is to notify participants of the calculated heating degree day for each HDD zone in the sub-network for the gas day.
This flow is used to notify participants about both the forecasted as well as the actual HDD value. Based on the timestamp of the flow, if can be derived, if the value was produced before the gas day, during the gas day or after the gas day.
