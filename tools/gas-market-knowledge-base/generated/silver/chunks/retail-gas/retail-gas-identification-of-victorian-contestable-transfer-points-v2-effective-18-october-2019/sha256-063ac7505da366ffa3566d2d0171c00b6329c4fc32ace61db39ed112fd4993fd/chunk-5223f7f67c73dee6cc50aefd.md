An initial allocation of numbers has occurred as meters have been registered with AEMO.  An initial block allocation has been made to Gas Distribution Businesses who will then allocate to individual delivery points.  Retailers will be informed of the relevant Meter Identification Registration Numbers for their end-users, by the Responsible Person for their sites. Please refer to the MIRN Structure document for details about the initial allocation.
It should be noted that the initial allocation of numbers (0001 to 3000) could have been allocated to a site that was originally a daily read site but has now been downgraded to a basic metered site
