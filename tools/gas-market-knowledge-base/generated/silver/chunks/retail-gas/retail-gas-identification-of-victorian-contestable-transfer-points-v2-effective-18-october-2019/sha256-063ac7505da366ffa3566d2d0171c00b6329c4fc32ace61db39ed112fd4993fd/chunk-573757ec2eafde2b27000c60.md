The Meter Identification Registration Number is designed to be allocated once and 'never' change. If a site is de-commissioned, then the MIRN will also be de-commissioned, but not re-allocated. This means that no embedded characters or meanings relating to site parameters will be used in allocating Meter Identification Registration Numbers, apart from the currently defined parameters (3 or 2 for Vic and NSW respectively, the differentiation between Physical and Logical, Custody Transfer and Daily Meters).
A Meter Identification Registration Number cannot be reassigned to another supply point, changed or edited and therefore there will be no re-issue of a Meter Identification Registration Number for any purpose.
All communications to and from registered end-users and Market Participants inclusive of financial responsible Market Participant accounts must include the Meter Identification Registration Number. All Market Participants must be aware that the Meter Identification Registration Number is the only identifier to be used for defining specific supply points.
Re-registrations of end-users involving status changes, communication and/or meter changes must include the Meter Identification Registration Number on all communications between affected parties.
