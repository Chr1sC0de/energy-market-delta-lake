AEMO, Definition = Australian Energy Market Operator. Business day, Definition = A day other than a Saturday, Sunday or public holiday in Melbourne.. Connection, Definition = The joining of a natural gas installation to the Network's gas pipeline to allow the flow of gas from the Network to the natural gas installation.. Delivery point, Definition = The point where natural gas leaves the Network before being supplied to the customer. The meter is the last part of the Network.. End-user, Definition = The person or legal entity using natural gas, usually the customer of the Retailer. In this document end-user and customer are used interchangeably.. Market Participant, Definition = An authorised Person who reticulates, or supplies gas within the contestable gas market.. Master Meter, Definition = A meter connected directly to a consumer service and supplying gas to subsidiary meters.. Meter, Definition = An instrument that measures the quantity of gas passing through it and includes associated equipment attached to the instrument to filter, control and regulate the flow of gas.. Meter Identification Registration Number (MIRN), Definition = A ten (10) digit alpha-numeric unique identifier used to identify a supply point used in the Victorian Gas Market..
