- 'n+1' MIRNs
- One Master Gas meter
- One Master Water meter
- Individual Hot water meters
- Individual gas meters
- Eg. Multiple dwelling with Individual Cooking & heating facilities, Centralised hot water system
Note: In this arrangement the take off point to the individual meters would be re-routed to the supply side of the Master Gas Meter, rather than leave it on the load side of the gas meter for multiple MIRNs to be available.  If individual meters are services from the load side, then the sub-meters would NOT be considered Transfer Points in the Victorian Market. Rather, the Master meter would be considered the transfer point, per the diagram below.
