2 July 2025, Market = QLD. 2 July 2025, AEMO/Market Participant = AEMO. 2 July 2025, Procedures and Clause = 6.3.2 of QLD Retail Market Procedures. 2 July 2025, Summary = Description Under clause 6.3.2 of the QLD Retail Market Procedures (RMP), AEMO must use its reasonable endeavours to publish on the Market Information Bulletin Board (MIBB) for Users only their aggregated consumption for each withdrawal zone and distribution region as provisional daily aggregated consumption reports by 3.00PM on the gas day after the gas day to which the aggregated consumption pertains. On 2 July 2025, Queensland daily balancing MIBB reports, INT 549 Daily Interval Meter Data and INT 588 Daily Aggregated Consumption, were published at 5.04pm, 2 hours and 4 minutes later than required under the QLD RMP. This caused a non-compliance with QLD RMP relating to timely provision of aggregated consumption data to Users. Cause The breach was discovered by a team member when checking all markets had been run for the day and the reports were then run upon discovery.
