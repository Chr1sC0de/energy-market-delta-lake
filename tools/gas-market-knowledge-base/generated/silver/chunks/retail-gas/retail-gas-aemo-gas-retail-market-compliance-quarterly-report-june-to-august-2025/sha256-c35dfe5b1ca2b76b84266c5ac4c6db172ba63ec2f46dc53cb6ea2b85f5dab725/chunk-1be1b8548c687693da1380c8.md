relates. On 14 July, EDD values were not published on the MIBB for gas days 11 to 13 July. This caused a non-compliance with VIC RMP relating to timely publication of EDD on MIBB. Cause The calculation of EDD is a fully automated process. Information is extracted from a file received from the Bureau of Meteorology (BOM) and published on the MIBB. Between 12 to 14 July (gas days 11 to 13 July), this process was unsuccessful due to missing sunshine hours data from the BOM. When EDD values are not published, an automated email alert should be sent to notify the relevant teams. However, on 14 July, EDD values for gas days 11 to 13 July were not calculated and email alert was not sent. Actions. 14 July 2025, Impact = Immaterial A EMO's non -compliance with clause 2.8.4(a) of VIC RMP on 14 July had no material impact on any other market participants, the market as a whole, or end use customers.
