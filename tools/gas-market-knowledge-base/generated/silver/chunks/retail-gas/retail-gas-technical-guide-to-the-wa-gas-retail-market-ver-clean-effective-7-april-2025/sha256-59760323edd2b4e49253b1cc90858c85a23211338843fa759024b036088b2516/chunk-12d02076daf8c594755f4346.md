Table 2 Content of the Retail Market Procedures
1. Interpretation and administration of the Procedures, Content = Deals with legal clarity, including: defining terms used in the Procedures; and identifying who the Procedures apply to.. 2. The databases, Content = Deals with management of databases, including: the 'AEMO registry', which is maintained by AEMO, and links Users (i.e. Retailers) with their customers; and the 'MIRN database', which is maintained by the Network Operator, and contains the meter information for all customers.. 3. MIRN transactions, Content = Deals with the transfer of customers between Users.. 4. Metering, Content = Sets out the requirements for metering equipment, meter reading, and management of metering data.. 5. Allocation, reconciliation and swing, Content = Sets out the processes for managing the daily allocation of gas usage to Users to enable settlement of gas supply and transmission contracts. Participants are urged to ensure that they have a detailed understanding of Chapter 5, which is the most complex part of market operations, and therefore represents the most risk to Participants.. 6. Compliance and interpretation, Content = Deals with Procedure breaches and interpretation of the Procedures, and with establishment of the Compliance Panel.. 7.
