- Gate point meters -for each gate point, allocations are based upon the pipeline injections or the physical gate point energy quantities as provided to AEMO by the Network Operator under clause 152;
- Interval meters -for each interval-metered delivery point, allocations are based upon the intervalmetered withdrawals ('IW') for each of the User's interval -metered delivery points, as provided to AEMO by the Network Operator under clause 158(1)(c); and
- Basic-meters -for each basic-metered delivery point, allocations are based upon the estimated basicmetered withdrawals ('EBW') for each of the User's basic -metered delivery points, as provided to AEMO by the Network Operator under clause 158(1)(a).
