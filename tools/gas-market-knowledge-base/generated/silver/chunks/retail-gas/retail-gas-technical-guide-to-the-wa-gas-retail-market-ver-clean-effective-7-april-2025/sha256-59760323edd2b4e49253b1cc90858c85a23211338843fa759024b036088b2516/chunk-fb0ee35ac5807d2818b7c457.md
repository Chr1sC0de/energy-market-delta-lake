Customer transfers are facilitated through the FRC Hub, whereby a customer can transfer from one User to another. To lodge a transfer request, the User must obtain the customer's consent and identify the delivery point identifier (i.e. MIRN) utilising the processes defined in the Procedures. Unless otherwise specified, references to 'clause' followed by a number are to a clause in the Procedures.
