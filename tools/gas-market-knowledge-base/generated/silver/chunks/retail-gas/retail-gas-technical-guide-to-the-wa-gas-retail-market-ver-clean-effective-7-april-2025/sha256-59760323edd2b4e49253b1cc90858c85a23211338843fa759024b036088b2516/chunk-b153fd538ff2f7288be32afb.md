The FRC Hub is a communication infrastructure operated by AEMO, and is the gateway through which gas retail market participants can deliver and receive structured B2B and B2M messages using defined protocols and formats.
The Hub T&Cs contains information for Participants who are connected to and use the FRC Hub infrastructure, which is administered by AEMO for the gas retail markets. 1
The Hub T&Cs provide an overview of the FRC Hub and specific information for gas retail market participants who wish connect to the FRC Hub to operate in the relevant markets. The Hub T&Cs contain a number of appendices with detailed instructions on the use of certain features of the FRC system.
