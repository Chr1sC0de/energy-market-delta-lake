Assume for gas day D that two Users are supplied with gas, with a total swing service amount of 6TJ for the gate point:
- User 1: UETW = 30TJ USS = 4TJ
absolute value of the USSE = 2;
USA1 =
2/5 X 4 = 1.6
USA2 =
4/5 X 2 = 1.6
This results in a total User specific swing service amount ('TUSA') = 3.2TJ.
The nonUser specific swing service amounts ('NUSA') for Users 1 and 2 are calculated as follows:
NUSA =
USS - USA
NUSA1 =
4 - 1.6 = 2.4
NUSA2 =
2 - 1.6 = 0.4
This results in a total nonUser specific swing service amount ('TNUSA') of 2.8TJ.
