Importantly, once the Self Certification process has been completed, the applicant must sign the Readiness Criteria Self Certification Checklist Form to verify that the applicant has successfully completed the Self Certification Process. A signed copy of this form must then be sent to AEMO.
On receipt of the signed Readiness Criteria Self Certification Checklist form, AEMO will notify all Participants of the pending activation of the new Participants GBOid and that the activation of this GBOid is required on the day specified on the notice, which must be at least 10 business days from the date of the notice being issued.
