Once the bid stack has been calculated, AEMO determines the market clearing price ('MCP') for total amount of swing service to be procured through applicable bid-stack. The MCP is the intersect point between the total amount of swing service to be procured on market and the price path.
Costs and swing service amounts are then allocated to the Users and Swing Service Providers in proportion to the User's contribution and the bid stack outcome. AEMO publishes the bid stack no later than five hours after the end of the gas day.
