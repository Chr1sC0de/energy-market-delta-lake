The GRMS provides the IT systems necessary to facilitate the competitive gas retail market in WA. The system was built on the foundation of the Procedures, and consists of the following three sub-systems:
- Delivery Point Registry ('DPR') entity;
- Balancing, Allocation and Reconciliation ('BAR'); and
- Gateways (CSV, ebXML, e-mail).
Figure 3 provides a diagrammatical representation of the GRMS and its various entities.
