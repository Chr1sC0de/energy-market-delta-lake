GRCF, Functions = The Gas Retail Consultative Forum (GRCF) is open to all interested organisations and stakeholders. Its role is to assist AEMO in making determinations on the matters under its consideration. It is also a forum for stakeholders to raise and address issues relating to the operation or functionality of the WA gas retail market. The GRCF functions and administrative processes are described in the ' Gas Retail Market Forum Terms of Reference' on AEMO's website.
ITDF, Functions = The IT Development Forum (ITDF). See the AEMO website for further information about this forum.
AEMO must undertake a different process to change the WA Gas Retail Market Agreement, including getting agreement from all signatories to the agreement and from the ERA.
