AEMO operates gas and electricity retail and wholesale markets in Australia. AEMO is a not-for-profit company that operates in accordance with the terms of its Constitution. The AEMO Constitution sets out, amongst other things, AEMO's objects, and the membership and workings of the AEMO Board, and is available on the AEMO website (www.aemo.com.au).
AEMO is owned 60% by Government and 40% by industry; and membership is open to any party that meets the eligibility criteria for 'Government Members' and 'Industry Members', as set out in clause 1.1 of the AEMO Constitution.
AEMO is governed by a Board of five to ten directors, all of whom are appointed by the COAG Energy Council (except the Managing Director who is appointed by the Board), and must include:
- the Chair,
- the Managing Director, and
- three to six directors with industry experience.
