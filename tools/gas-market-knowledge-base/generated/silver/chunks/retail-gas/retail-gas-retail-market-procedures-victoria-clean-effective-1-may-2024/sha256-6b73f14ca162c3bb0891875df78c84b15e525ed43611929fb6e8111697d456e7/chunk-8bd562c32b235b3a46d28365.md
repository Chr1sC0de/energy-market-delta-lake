- (d) A Retailer may at any time request a Distributor to  change a date in a meter reading  schedule where  that  change  pertains  to  a meter that  relates  to  a distribution supply point which is located in the distribution area of that Distributor and in respect of which the Retailer is the FRO .  However, the Distributor is not required to make the requested change.
