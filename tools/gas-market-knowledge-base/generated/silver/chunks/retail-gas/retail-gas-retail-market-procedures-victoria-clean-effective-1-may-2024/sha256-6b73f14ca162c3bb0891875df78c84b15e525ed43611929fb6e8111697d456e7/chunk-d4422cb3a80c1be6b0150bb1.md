- (b) Each Distributor must provide to each Retailer who is the FRO for a distribution supply point a schedule ( meter reading schedule ) setting out the date on which it proposes to read all its meters during each 12 month period commencing on either 1 January or 1 July. The meter reading schedule must provide for all such meters to be read at intervals of approximately one month (where the meters are on a monthly reading cycle) or two months (where the meters are on a bimonthly reading cycle), with the first reading to be undertaken approximately one month or two months (as the case may be) after the last reading undertaken prior to the commencement of the meter reading schedule .
- (c) A meter reading schedule must be provided under clause 2.2.1(b) in each year, not less than two months prior to the expiry of the then current meter reading schedule .
