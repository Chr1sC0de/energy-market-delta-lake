- (a) Where, pursuant to clause 4.7.1, AEMO registers the Market Participant who delivered the transfer request in the metering register as the FRO for a supply point ,  that  registration  will  be  deemed  to  take  effect  at  6.00am  on  the retrospective transfer date and will be deemed to cease to have effect at 6.00am on the day after the registration end date (if any).
- (b) AEMO must record the date on which such registration takes place, the date on which such registration is deemed to take effect and the period of registration in the metering register.
