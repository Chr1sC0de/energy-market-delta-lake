Each Distributor must provide any updates to the estimated data provided under clause 6.1.5  (b),  (c)  and  (d)  to  AEMO , the failed  Retailer and declared  host  Retailer. The updates must be provided as soon as it is practical to do so, but in any event by the 118 th business day after the end of the month in which the provisions of chapter 6 of these Procedures have been invoked.
