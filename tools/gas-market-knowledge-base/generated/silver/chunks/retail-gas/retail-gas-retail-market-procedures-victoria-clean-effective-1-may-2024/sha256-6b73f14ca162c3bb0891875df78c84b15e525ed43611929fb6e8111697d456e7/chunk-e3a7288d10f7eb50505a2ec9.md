- (a) If  a Distributor is  required  to  undertake  an estimated meter  reading for  the purposes  of  these Procedures ,  the Distributor must  undertake  that estimated meter reading in accordance with an approved estimation methodology .
- (b) Prior to requesting the approval by AEMO of an estimation methodology as an approved estimation methodology , the Distributor must consult with each Retailer who is the FRO for a distribution supply point which is located in the distribution area of that Distributor .
