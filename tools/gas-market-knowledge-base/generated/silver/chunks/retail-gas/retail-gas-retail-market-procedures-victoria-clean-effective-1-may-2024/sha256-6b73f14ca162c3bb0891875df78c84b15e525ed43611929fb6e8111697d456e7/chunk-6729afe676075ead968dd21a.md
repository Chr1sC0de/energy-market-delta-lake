Subject  to  clause  3.2.2(a),  any Market  Participant or AEMO  may  deliver  a MIRN discovery  request in  relation  to  a distribution  supply  point to  a Distributor .  A MIRN discovery  request must  include  at  least  the MIRN or  the  mandatory  components  of discovery address for the distribution supply point to which the MIRN discovery request relates.
