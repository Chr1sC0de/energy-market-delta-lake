- (a) If, pursuant to clause 3.3.2(d), a Market Participant or AEMO is notified that the discovery address or MIRN relating to the distribution supply point in respect of which a MIRN discovery request has been made cannot be found in the MIRN database of the relevant Distributor , the Market Participant or AEMO (as the case may be) may request that the Distributor assist in the location of that discovery address or MIRN in that MIRN database , in which case the Distributor must use its reasonable endeavours to provide that assistance:
- (i) where the request for assistance is made before midday on a day that is a business day - by 5.00 pm on that business day ; and
- (ii) where the request for assistance is made on or after midday on a day that is a business day, or is made on a day that is not a business day - by 5.00 pm on the first business day following the day on which the request is made.
