- (a) A Retailer may  request  a Distributor to  undertake  a special  read of  a meter relating to a distribution supply point which is located in the distribution area of the Distributor by delivering a special read request to the Distributor .  The special read request  must  nominate,  as  the  day  on  which  the special  read is  to  be
undertaken, a business day that is not less than two business days after the day on which the special read request is given to the Distributor .
- (b) The Distributor must use its reasonable endeavours to undertake the special read on the special read date .
