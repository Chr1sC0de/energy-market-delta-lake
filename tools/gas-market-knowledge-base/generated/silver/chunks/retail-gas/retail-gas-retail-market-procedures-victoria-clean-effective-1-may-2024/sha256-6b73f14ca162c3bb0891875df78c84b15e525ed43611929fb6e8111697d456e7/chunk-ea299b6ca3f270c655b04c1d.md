- (a) AEMO must maintain a register of network sections that each Market Participant is entitled to supply gas to supply points within that network section.
- (b) Market Participants must advise AEMO of network sections where they hold all necessary rights to supply gas .
- (c) AEMO may request a service provider to confirm that information provided under clause 4.5A.3(b) is correct
