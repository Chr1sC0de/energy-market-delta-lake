- (viii) where  the Distributor has  substituted  a meter  reading in  accordance  with clause 2.5, the Distributor must use its reasonable endeavours to provide the FRO with the following information:
- (A) the date to which the substituted meter reading pertains;
- (B) the substituted meter reading (identified as such) for that meter ; and
- (C) details  of  the approved substitution  methodology applied  to  obtain that substituted meter reading ,
by  5.00 pm  on  the  first business  day following  the  day  on  which  the substituted meter reading was calculated.
