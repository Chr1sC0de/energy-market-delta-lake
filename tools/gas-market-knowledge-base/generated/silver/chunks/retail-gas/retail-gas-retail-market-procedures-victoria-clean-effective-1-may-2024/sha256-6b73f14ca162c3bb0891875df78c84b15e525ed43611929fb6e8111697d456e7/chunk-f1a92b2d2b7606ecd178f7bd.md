Where:
- (a) a Market Participant delivers a transfer request in relation to a supply point to AEMO;
- (b) a transfer request in relation to that supply point has already been delivered to AEMO (whether by that or any other Market Participant ); and
- (c) the transfer request referred to in clause 4.1.5(b) (the existing transfer request ) is  being  processed  by AEMO  but  the Market  Participant who  delivered  the existing transfer request to AEMO has not yet been registered in the metering register as the FRO for the supply point to  which the existing transfer request relates,
then AEMO must not process the transfer request referred to  in  clause  4.1.5(a)  and must, by midnight on the first business day following  the  day  on  which  that transfer request was delivered to AEMO, deliver a notice stating this fact to the Market Participant who delivered that transfer request .
