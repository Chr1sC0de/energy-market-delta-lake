- (a) A Distributor must only undertake a substituted meter reading in accordance with, and in the circumstances specified in, an approved substitution methodology . The approved substitution methodology must specify the criteria which must be satisfied before that methodology may be applied.
- (b) Prior to requesting the approval by AEMO of a substitution methodology as an approved  substitution  methodology ,  the Distributor must  consult  with  each Retailer who is the FRO for  a distribution supply point which is located in the distribution area of that Distributor .
