Changes to Customer Details information is initiated by the User and sent to the Network Operator to maintain the most up to date Customer Contact Information. The Network Operator uses this information to support contact management in relation to planned works, emergency and fault calls.
The following table  shows the  Customer  Details  aseXML  transactions  and  the  corresponding transactions from the Table of Transactions.
aseXML Transaction, 1 = Table of Transactions. aseXML Transaction, 2 = Table of Transactions. Transaction Name, 1 = Ref No. Transaction Name, 2 = Transaction Type. CustomerDetailsNotification, 1 = 70. CustomerDetailsNotification, 2 = Amend Customer Details. CustomerDetailsRequest, 1 = 72. CustomerDetailsRequest, 2 = Customer Details Request
These business transactions will be mapped to the 'CUST' Transaction Group in aseXML.
