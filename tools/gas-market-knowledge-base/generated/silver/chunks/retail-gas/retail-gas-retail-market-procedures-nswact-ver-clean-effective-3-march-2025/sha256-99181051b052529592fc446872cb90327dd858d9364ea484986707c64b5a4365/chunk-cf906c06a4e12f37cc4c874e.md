= In relation to a Customer at a delivery point , whether the Customer is:. Customer characterisation, 1 = (a) 'metropolitan' or 'non-metropolitan', where metropolitan indicates the delivery point is on the NSW-Wilton or ACT-Canberra network section ; and. Customer characterisation, 1 = (b) 'residential' or 'business', where residential indicates the primary use of the consumed energy is for household purposes and business indicates the primary use of the consumed energy is for commercial purposes, as determined by the Customer's Retailer .. Customer no- change statement, 1 = A statement given with a transfer request in accordance with clause 6.2.1(c)(iv).. Customer-own read, 1 = A read of a meter undertaken by a Customer , details of which are provided by the Customer to the User or Network Operator for the delivery point to which the meter relates. For the purpose of these Procedures, except for a Customer-own read (Actual) , a Customer-own read is taken to be an estimated meter reading ..
