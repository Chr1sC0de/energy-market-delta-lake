- (a) A User may trade all or part of its cumulative imbalance in a network section on a nomination day with another User . A trade may be proposed by a User at any time on a day for the next nomination day .
- (b) Following a trade referred to in paragraph (a), the buying User and the selling User must notify AEMO of:
- (i) the identity of the buying User and selling User ;
- (ii) the relevant network section ;
- (iii) the relevant nomination day ; and
- (iv) the quantity of cumulative imbalance traded (in whole MJ ).
- (v) On receipt of a notification from a User under paragraph (b), AEMO must confirm that:
- (vi) the selling User 's provisional cumulative imbalance for the relevant nomination day is greater or equal to the quantity of cumulative imbalance that the User proposes to trade on that day;
- (vii) if the trade is notified by a selling User , it matches a trade notified by a buying User and vice versa; and
- (viii) the proposed trade would not increase either trading party's cumulative imbalance for the relevant nomination day .
