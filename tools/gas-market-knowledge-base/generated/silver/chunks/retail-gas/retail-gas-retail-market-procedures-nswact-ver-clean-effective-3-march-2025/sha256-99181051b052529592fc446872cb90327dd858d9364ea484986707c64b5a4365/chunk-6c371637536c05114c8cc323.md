A Network Operator must use reasonable endeavours to collect metering data for each of the delivery points in its network in accordance with the following timeframes:
- (a) for daily metered delivery points - at the start of each gas day ;
- (b) for non-daily metered delivery points with meters read monthly - 31 days plus or minus 2 business days ;
- (c) for non-daily metered delivery points with meters read every two months - 61 days plus or minus 1 business day ; and
- (d) for non-daily metered delivery points with meters read quarterly - 91 days plus or minus 2 business days .
