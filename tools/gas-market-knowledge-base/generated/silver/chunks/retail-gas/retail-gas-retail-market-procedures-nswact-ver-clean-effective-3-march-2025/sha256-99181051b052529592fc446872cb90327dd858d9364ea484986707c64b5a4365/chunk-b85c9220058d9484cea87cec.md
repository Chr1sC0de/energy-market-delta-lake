- (a) AEMO must ensure that at any time there is an industry approved methodology to determine the FCLP validity criteria .
- (b) At any time after the date of determination under paragraph (a) a Participant may request AEMO to consider whether one or more of the FCLP validity criteria continues to be met or is appropriate. AEMO must consider a request under this paragraph (b) as soon as practicable.
- (c) Subject to paragraph (d) AEMO may from time to time amend the FCLP validity criteria on 10 business days prior notice to Participants .
- (d) AEMO must not amend the FCLP validity criteria without the consent of the Network Operator for the ACTCanberra network section .
- (e) Subject to paragraph (f), AEMO may, on not less than 10 business days' notice to Participants , determine that clauses 8.6.7 and 8.6.8 will cease to be effective on a date determined by AEMO .
- (f) AEMO may only make a determination under paragraph (e) if in its reasonable opinion one or more of the FCLP validity criteria is no longer met.
