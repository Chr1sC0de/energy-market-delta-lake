Each Network Operator with a RoLR affected delivery point in its network must:
- (a) update its metering database by recording the relevant designated RoLR as the FRO for each RoLR affected delivery point to which clause 6.1.2 does not apply; and
- (b) provide AEMO with a report of the details of the MIRNs that have been updated in the Network Operator's metering database.
