- (a) A Network Operator must use reasonable endeavours to ensure that all meters at a non-daily metered delivery point are read on the same gas day .
- (b) The Network Operator must ensure that the applicable meter reading information for all meters relating to a delivery point is provided to the FRO for that delivery point at the same time.
- (c) For the purposes of the timeframes for providing meter reading information under this Chapter, where a delivery point includes a hot water meter , the information for all meters at the delivery point must be delivered within the timeframe applicable to the hot water meter .
