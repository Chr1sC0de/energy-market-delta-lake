- (a) By no later than 3 business days after 15 days after the end of the month, AEMO must have available information for each User for each network section and for each nomination day in the previous month:
- (i) total estimated withdrawals ; and
- (ii) total daily withdrawals.
- (b) The total estimated withdrawals and total daily withdrawals will be calculated using the most recent information available to the AEMO .
