- (a) A Retailer must not deliver a transfer request in relation to a delivery point to AEMO unless the Retailer has received the explicit informed consent of the Customer to the registration of that Retailer in the AEMO metering database as the FRO for that delivery point with effect from any time during the permitted prospective period in relation to the transfer request .
- (b) A Retailer who delivers a transfer request in relation to a delivery point to AEMO is taken to have represented to AEMO that the Retailer has received the explicit informed consent of the Customer to the registration of that Retailer in the AEMO metering database as the FRO for that delivery point with effect from any time during the permitted prospective period in relation to the transfer request .
