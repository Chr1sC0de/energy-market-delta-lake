- (a) In accordance with the certification process (Gas FRC Business to Business connectivity Testing and System Certification) maintained and published by AEMO , AEMO and each Retailer and Network Operator must be certified as compliant by AEMO prior to using the FRC HUB for transactions specified in the Gas Interface Protocol.
- (b) AEMO must maintain and publish FRC HUB Operational Terms and Conditions for the FRC HUB .
- (c) AEMO, prior to implementing changes to the published FRC HUB Operational Terms and Conditions for the FRC HUB , must:
- (i) provide Retailers and Network Operators with the proposed change to the FRC HUB Operational Terms and Conditions ; and
- (ii) allow a reasonable time to receive Retailer and Network Operator responses to the proposed change to the FRC HUB Operational Terms and Conditions .
- (d) AEMO and each Retailer and Network Operator must comply with the FRC HUB Operational Terms and Conditions , as published by AEMO from time to time.
- (e) A breach by a Retailer or Network Operator of the FRC HUB Operational Terms and Conditions, is taken to be a breach of these Procedures for the purposes of section 91MB of the Law .
