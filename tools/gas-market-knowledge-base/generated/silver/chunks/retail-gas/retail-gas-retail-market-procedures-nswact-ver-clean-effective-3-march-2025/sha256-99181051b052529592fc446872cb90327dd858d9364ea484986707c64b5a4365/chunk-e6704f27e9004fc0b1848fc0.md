- (a) By no later than 5 hours after the start of the gas day +1, AEMO must notify the Network Operator for the relevant network section of the total estimated withdrawal for each User in that network section for the gas day .
- (b) On each gas day that AEMO recalculates a User 's STTM distribution system allocation under clause 8.11.14, 8.11.15, or 8.11.16, AEMO must notify the Network Operator for the relevant network section of the total estimated withdrawal for each User in that network section for each gas day to which that recalculation applied.
