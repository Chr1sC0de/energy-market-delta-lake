- (a) By no later than 5 hours after the start of the gas day +1, AEMO must calculate, for each User in a network section :
- (i) that User 's percentage share of the net section load for the gas day ( apportionment percentage ); and
- (ii) that User 's share of the net section load for the gas day (in MJ ).
- (b) The sum of the percentages for all Users calculated under paragraph (a)(i) must be 100 percent.
