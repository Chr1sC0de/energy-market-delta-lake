- (a) Any Participant who has received a transfer error correction request notification under clause 11.2 may deliver to AEMO , at any time before the User who delivered the transfer error correction request is registered in the AEMO metering database as the FRO for the delivery point to which that transfer error correction request relates, a notice describing a potential problem in respect of that transfer error correction request .
- (b) A notice delivered by a person under paragraph (a) does not constitute an error correction objection notice .
