- (a) A Network Operator may withdraw an objection notice by delivering to AEMO a notice of withdrawal (an objection withdrawal notice ) at any time before midnight on the 20th business day after the day on which the Network Operator delivered the objection notice to AEMO .
- (b) A Network Operator must withdraw an objection notice by the time specified in paragraph (a) if, by midnight on the 19th business day after the day on which the Network Operator delivered the objection notice to AEMO , the prospective FRO holds all necessary rights to supply gas at the relevant delivery point .
- (c) An objection withdrawal notice must include the same information specified in clause 6.4.1(b).
