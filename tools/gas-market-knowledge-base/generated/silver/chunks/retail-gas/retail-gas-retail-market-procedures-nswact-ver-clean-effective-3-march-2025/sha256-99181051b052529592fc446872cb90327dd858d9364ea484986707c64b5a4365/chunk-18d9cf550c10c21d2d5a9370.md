- (d) Where an interval meter is downgraded to a basic meter by a Network Operator , the Network Operator must use its reasonable endeavours:
- (i) to provide to AEMO all the relevant details relating to the basic meter which are required for the purposes of updating the AEMO metering data base by 5.00 pm on the 2 nd business day after the day on which the meter is to be downgraded; and
- (ii) to include the relevant details relating to the basic meter in its metering data base by 5.00 pm on the 5 th business day after the day on which the meter was downgraded.
