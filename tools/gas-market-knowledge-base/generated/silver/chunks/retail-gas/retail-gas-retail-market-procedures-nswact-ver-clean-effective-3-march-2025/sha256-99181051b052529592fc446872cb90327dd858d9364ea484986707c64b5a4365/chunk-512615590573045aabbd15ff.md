- (a) A User may request a Network Operator to undertake a special read of a meter relating to a delivery point in that Network Operator's network by delivering a special read request to the Network Operator . The special read request must nominate, as the day on which the special read is to be undertaken, a business day that is not less than 2 business days after the day on which the special read request is given to the Network Operator .
- (b) The Network Operator must use its reasonable endeavours to undertake the special read on the nominated date.
