- @hrlyssm is the hourly Sunshine minutes
- @psm is possible minutes of sunshine during that hour based on sunrise/sunset times
- @percentsun is the inverse of cloud coverage from meters with the values set out below.
Cloud Coverage:
CLR = 0
SCT = 30
BKN = 70
OVC = 100
As an example between noon and 1pm, the possible sunshine minutes (@psm) would be 60. If the meter observation was BKN or Cloud Coverage 70, @percentsun would be 30.
In that hour, @hrlyssm = 18 (60 * 30 * 0.01)
@hrlyssm is then converted to hours by dividing by 60
Therefore sunshine hours for that hour = 0.3 (18/60)
