- (a) Provided that the requirements of clause 11.1 have been complied with in relation to a transfer error correction request , AEMO must, by midnight on the next business day after the day on which the transfer error correction request was delivered to it, deliver a notification of the transfer error correction request ( transfer error correction request notification) to:
- (i) the affected FRO ; and
- (ii) the Network Operator in whose network the delivery point is located.
- (b) If AEMO considers that the requirements of clause 11.1 have not been complied with, AEMO must notify the User who delivered the transfer request of the noncompliance and cease processing the transfer error correction request .
