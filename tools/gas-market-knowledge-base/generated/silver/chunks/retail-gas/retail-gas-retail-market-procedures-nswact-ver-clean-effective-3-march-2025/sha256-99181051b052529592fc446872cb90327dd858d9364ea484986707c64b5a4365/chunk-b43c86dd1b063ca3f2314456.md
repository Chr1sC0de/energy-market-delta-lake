Network, 1 = In relation to a Network Operator , the distribution network described in its applicable access arrangement.. Network Operator, 1 = An entity (also commonly referred to as a distributor) that participates in the retail gas market of New South Wales and the Australian Capital Territory in the registrable capacity of a 'network operator' under the Rules and has registered with AEMO under the Rules in that capacity.. network receipt point, 1 = A point at which gas enters a Network Operator's network .. network section, 1 = Note: For the avoidance of doubt in relation to the STTM network section, a network receipt point is a custody transfer point. Part of a Network Operator's network described as a 'network
arrangement does not identify any network sections, a pipeline forming part of the relevant network .
