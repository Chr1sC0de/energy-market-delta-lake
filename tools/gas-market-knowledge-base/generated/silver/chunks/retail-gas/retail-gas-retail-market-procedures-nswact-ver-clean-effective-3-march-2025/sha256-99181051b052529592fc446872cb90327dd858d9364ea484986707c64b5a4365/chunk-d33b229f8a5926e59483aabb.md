- (i) The effective degree day (EDD) for NSW is calculated as follows:
EDD = DD (temperature effect)
+ 0.0092 x DD x average wind (wind chill factor)
- 0.0628 x sunshine hours (warming effect of sunshine)
+ 5.0805 x Cos ((2π(day-198)) / 365)  (seasonal factor)
