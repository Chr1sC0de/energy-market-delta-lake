Where a notice has been delivered to AEMO by the Network Operator in respect of a delivery point , stating that the proposed transfer date nominated in the transfer error correction request is invalid, then AEMO must:
- (a) cease processing that transfer error correction request ; and
- (b) by midnight on the next business day , deliver a notice that the transfer error correction request has been terminated, to:
- (i) the User who delivered the transfer error correction request to AEMO ;
- (ii) the affected FRO ; and
- (iii) the relevant Network Operator.
