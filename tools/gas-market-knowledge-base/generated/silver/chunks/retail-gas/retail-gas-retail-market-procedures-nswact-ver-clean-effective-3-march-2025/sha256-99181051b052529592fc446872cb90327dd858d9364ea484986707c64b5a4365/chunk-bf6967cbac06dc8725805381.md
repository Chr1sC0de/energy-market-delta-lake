- (a) AEMO must undertake a review in accordance with this clause (' Review ') at least every two years.
- (b) A Review must constitute an examination in accordance with the standard for a review specified in Auditing Standard ASAE 3000 (Explanatory Framework for Standards on Audit and Audit Related Services) (as varied from time to time) prepared by the Auditing and Assurance Standards Board.
- (c) In undertaking a Review, AEMO must appoint a person (a ' Market Auditor ') who in AEMO 's reasonable opinion is independent and suitably qualified to conduct a Review.
