- (a) For the purposes of Jemena's applicable access arrangement , the withdrawal quantity for a User for all of its non-daily metered delivery points on a nomination day is the User 's total estimated withdrawal plus the User 's reconciliation adjustment amount for that nomination day .
- (b) On any day, if a notification under clause 8.10.2 is not issued, then Jemena may in relation to each User of its network , estimate a withdrawal quantity in relation to that User 's non-daily metered delivery points .
