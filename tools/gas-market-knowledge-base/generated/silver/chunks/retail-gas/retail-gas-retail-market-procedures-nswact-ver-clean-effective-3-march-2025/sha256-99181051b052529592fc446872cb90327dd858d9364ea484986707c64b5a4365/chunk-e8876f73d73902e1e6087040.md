On each weekly calculation day for a month, AEMO must recalculate each User 's STTM distribution system allocation , and each component of the User 's STTM distribution system allocation , for a network section for each gas day during the period that:
- (a) commences on (and includes) the gas day which begins on the 1 st day of that month; and
- (b) ends on (and includes) the gas day which begins on the day immediately before the weekly calculation day .
