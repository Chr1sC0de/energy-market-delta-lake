A transfer request notification must include the following information:
- (a) the MIRN for the delivery point to which the transfer request relates;
- (b) the proposed transfer date nominated in the transfer request ;
- (c) whether the transfer request contains a Customer no-change statement ; and
- (d) in the case of the transfer request notification delivered to the Network Operator for the delivery point , the name of the User who delivered the transfer request to AEMO .
