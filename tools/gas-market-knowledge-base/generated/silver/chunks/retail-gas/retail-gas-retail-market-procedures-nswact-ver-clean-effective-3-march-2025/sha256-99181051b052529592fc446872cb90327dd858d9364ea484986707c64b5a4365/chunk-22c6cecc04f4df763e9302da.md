- (a) AEMO must calculate, STTM distribution system allocation in accordance with rule 422 of the Rules and the STTM Procedures .
- (b)  For a User in an STTM network section for a gas day , the STTM distribution system allocation is the sum of:
- (i) the User's total daily withdrawals for that network section for that gas day ;
- (ii)  either the User's share of net section load for that network section for that gas day as calculated under clause 8.11.8 or, if recalculation under clause 8.11.13 is required, the User's total non-daily metered withdrawals calculated under clause 8.11.13(d);
- (iii) the User's SCLP for that network section for that gas day;
- (iv) the User's SUAG for that network section for that gas day ; and
- (v)  the User's adjustment amount for that network section for that gas day , if that adjustment amount has been calculated under clause 8.11.13(g).
