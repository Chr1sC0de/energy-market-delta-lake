Each Network Operator must provide any updates to estimated meter reading data provided under clause 7.2.6 to AEMO, the failed Retailer and designated RoLR as soon as practicable, but in any event by the 185 th business day after the end of the month in which the provisions of Chapter 7 of these Procedures have been invoked.
