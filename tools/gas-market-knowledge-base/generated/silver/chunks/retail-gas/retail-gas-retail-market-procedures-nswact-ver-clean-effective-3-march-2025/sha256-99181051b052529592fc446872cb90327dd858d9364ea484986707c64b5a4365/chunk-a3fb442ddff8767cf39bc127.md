- (a) If no objections are received from Users within the period specified in clause 8.7.3(a), then as soon as reasonably practicable after the completing its assessment under clause 8.7.4 AEMO must determine whether the proposed new nominations timetable should come into effect in the relevant network section , having regard to the impacts on AEMO identified in that assessment.
- (b) As soon as practicable but in any event no later than 3 business days after the determination under paragraph (a) AEMO will notify the relevant Network Operator and each User of:
- (i) whether or not the new nominations timetable will come into effect; and
- (ii) if the new nominations timetable will come into effect, the date it will come into effect. The effective date specified in the notice must be no earlier than 5 business days after the date of the notice provided under this paragraph (b).
