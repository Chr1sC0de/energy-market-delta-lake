AEMO must, by midnight on the next business day after the day on which a transfer error correction withdrawal notice is delivered to it, deliver notice of the withdrawal of the transfer error correction request , together with a notice that AEMO will not further process that transfer error correction request , to:
- (a) where the proposed transfer date nominated in the transfer error correction request is an error correction transfer date , the affected FRO ; and
- (b) the Network Operator in whose network that delivery point is located.
