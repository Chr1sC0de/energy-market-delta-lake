- (a) A User must use its reasonable endeavours to maintain a cumulative imbalance for each network section of zero.
- (b) If a User 's cumulative imbalance exceeds the limit in clause 8.8.6(b) as a result of the transfer to the User of some or all of another User 's cumulative imbalance under clause 8.8.5 then the User will not be in breach of paragraph (a) provided that it uses its reasonable endeavours to bring its cumulative imbalance within the limit in clause 8.8.6(b) within a reasonable period of time after the transfer under clause 8.8.5.
