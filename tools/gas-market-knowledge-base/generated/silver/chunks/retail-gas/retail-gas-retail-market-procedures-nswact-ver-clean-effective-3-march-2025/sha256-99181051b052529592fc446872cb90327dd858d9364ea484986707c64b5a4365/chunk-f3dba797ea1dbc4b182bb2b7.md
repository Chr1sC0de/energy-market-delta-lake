Nothing in this clause 6.9 prescribes the mechanism by which the transfer of affected delivery points to the transferee must be effected in AEMO's metering database and AEMO may in its discretion determine the manner in which the transfer is to be effected, provided that the transfer mechanism used:
- (a) is cost effective and efficient;
- (b) has been approved by the Network Operator for each of the affected delivery points ;
- (c) is consistent with these Procedures; and
- (d) has results specified in clause 6.9.7(a)(ii).
