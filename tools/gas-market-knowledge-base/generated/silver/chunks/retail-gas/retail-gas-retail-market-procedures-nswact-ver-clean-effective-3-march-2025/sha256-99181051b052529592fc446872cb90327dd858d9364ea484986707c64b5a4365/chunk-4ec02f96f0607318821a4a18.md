A Network Operator must undertake an estimated meter reading in the circumstances required under clause 3.5 of these Procedures.
On each gas day AEMO must use its reasonable endeavours to calculate a New South Wales 'effective degree day' or ' EDD ' value and a Australian Capital Territory EDD value for use in the calculation of Type 1 estimations prescribed in paragraph (b), and publish those EDDs by midnight on the next business day after the day to which the EDD applies.
The EDD calculations will be the EDD (NSW or ACT), calculated in accordance with clause A1.3, applicable to the network section to which the meter is connected.
