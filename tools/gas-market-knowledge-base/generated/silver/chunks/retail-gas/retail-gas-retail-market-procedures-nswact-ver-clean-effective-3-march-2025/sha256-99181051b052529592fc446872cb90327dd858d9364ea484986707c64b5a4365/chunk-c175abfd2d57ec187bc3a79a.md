- (a) On nomination day +1, AEMO must determine the reconciliation amount for a non-daily metered delivery point for the nomination day in accordance with the following formula:
RAi = EWi - DWLi
Where:
i = each gas day in the sculpting period;
RAi = reconciliation amount for a non-daily metered delivery point for gas day i in the sculpting period;
EWi = the estimated withdrawal for the gas day ; and DWLi = the distributed withdrawal for the gas day .
- (b) The reconciliation amount on nomination day +1 will be zero for those non-daily metered delivery points for which no meter reading was received for the nomination day .
