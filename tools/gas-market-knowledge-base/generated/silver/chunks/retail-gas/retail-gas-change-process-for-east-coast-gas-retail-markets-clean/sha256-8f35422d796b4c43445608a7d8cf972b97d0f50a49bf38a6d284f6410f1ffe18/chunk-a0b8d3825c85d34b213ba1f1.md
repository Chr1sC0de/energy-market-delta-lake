Each year, AEMO tables a proposed prioritisation strategy and a list of initiatives from the Issue / Change Register with the GRCF for consideration. These are a list of initiatives that AEMO has identified as candidates to be considered for consultation in the following calendar year.
The annual review commences in the fourth quarter of each year. When developing the list of initiatives to progress, AEMO undertakes a review of all gas initiatives on the Issue / Change Register. AEMO will consider any potential impact of known major projects 5  on resources. Initiatives that have fixed NGR timelines are given priority.
Once the GRCF finalises and endorses the list of initiatives, it is placed under version control. Any updates to this list will be referred to the GRCF prior to any changes being accepted.
