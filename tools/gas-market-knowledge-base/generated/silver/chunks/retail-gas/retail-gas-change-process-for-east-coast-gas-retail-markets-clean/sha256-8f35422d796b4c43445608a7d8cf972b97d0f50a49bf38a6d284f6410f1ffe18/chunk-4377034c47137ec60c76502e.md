AEMO may prepare an industry implementation plan that describes the industry cut-over plan. The plan would provide details of the events relating to, timing of, and those responsible for the activities associated with the implementation of the industry changes. The document would cover a number of key activities such as timing requirements to switch off systems, clearing remaining transactions from gateways (both outgoing and incoming), shutting down gateways, manual procedures to be invoked while the system outage is occurring, and commencement of changes.
