GMI to AEMO and the proponent, advising specifically with relevance to the matters under clause 4(c) of the Approved Process and to whether the change upholds the NGO. GRCF may propose alternative solution(s) to the issue, comparing. PPC (Note: PPC stage commences from lodgement date and can run up to 40 business days. Generally, 30 business days allowed), AEMO Action = AEMO records GRCF outcomes. AEMO maintains change register. If determining whether the change upholds the NGO becomes problematic, AEMO will work with the GRCF on an efficient and effective methodology that will assist AEMO in making an NGO determination. AEMO decides whether an extension of time is required. If so, AEMO publishes a notification of extension on the AEMO website and advises the GRCF.. PPC (Note: PPC stage commences from lodgement date and can run up to 40 business days. Generally, 30 business days allowed), Proponent / Participant / GRCF Action = the any proposed solution(s) and a 'do nothing' solution. A 'do nothing' solution is always an option. Where this option is assessed as the most efficient outcome, the process ends..
