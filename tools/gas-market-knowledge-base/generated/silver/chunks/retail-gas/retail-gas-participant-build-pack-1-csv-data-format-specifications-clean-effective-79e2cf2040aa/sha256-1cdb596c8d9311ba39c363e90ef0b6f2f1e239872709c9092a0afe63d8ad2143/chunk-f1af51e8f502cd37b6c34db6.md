This transaction is used to advise the Retailer of the annual listing of proposed reading schedule. It is initiated by the Distributor and is passed to the Retailer and the frequency is once a year. The data is expected to be sorted in date order.
An example of a CSV file is below:
Scheduled_Reading_Day_Number,Meter_Read_Frequency,Date_of_Future_Read
```
12,B,2002-01-17 12,B,2002-03-19 12,B,2002-05-20 12,B,2002-07-18 12,B,2002-09-16 12,B,2002-11-14
```
