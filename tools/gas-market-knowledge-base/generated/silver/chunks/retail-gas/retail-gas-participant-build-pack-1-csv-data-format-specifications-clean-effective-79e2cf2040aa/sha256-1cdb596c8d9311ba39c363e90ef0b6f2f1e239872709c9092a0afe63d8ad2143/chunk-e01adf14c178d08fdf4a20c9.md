The complete MIRN listing password exchange process is as follows:
- Retailer to nominate an email address to send password. These details are sent to DB's.
- Password to change each 90 days consistent with AEMO's MSATS Systems & AEMO's MIBB.
- DB's to email password details to retailer prior to it being used.
