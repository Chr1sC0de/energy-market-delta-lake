Upon receipt of a reconnection confirmation notice which is not valid, AEMO must immediately :
- (a) reject the reconnection confirmation notice ; and
- (b) notify the network operator that lodged the reconnection confirmation notice that the reconnection confirmation notice has been rejected and provide the reason why the reconnection confirmation notice is not valid.
{Note: A network operator wishing to reinitiate a reconnection confirmation notice that has been rejected must lodge a new reconnection confirmation notice .}
