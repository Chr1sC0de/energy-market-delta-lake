- (1) A self-contracting user may request AEMO , or AEMO's nominee, to:
2. (a) lodge a transfer request on its behalf in order for the self-contracting user to transfer to itself; and
3. (b) accept notices that  are  required  to  be in  aseXML  format  under  these procedures on behalf of the self-contracting user in relation to the transfer request referred to in clause 13(1)(a).
- (2) Upon receiving a request under clause 13(1), AEMO , or AEMO's nominee, must lodge a transfer request and accept notices in aseXML format on behalf of a selfcontracting user on such terms and conditions as AEMO determines.
