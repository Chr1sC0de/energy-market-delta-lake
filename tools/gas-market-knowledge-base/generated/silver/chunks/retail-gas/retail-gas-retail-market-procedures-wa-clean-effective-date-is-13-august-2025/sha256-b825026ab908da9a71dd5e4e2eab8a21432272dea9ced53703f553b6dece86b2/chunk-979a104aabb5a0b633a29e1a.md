{Note:  The objective of clause 189 (3) is to prevent gaming by a user by the user generating swing. The objective of clause 189(6) is to ensure that clause 189 (3) does not prevent a user from taking action which is for the overall benefit of the sub-network as a whole in extraordinary circumstances. For example, a user should be able to ensure that an adequate amount of gas is supplied into a subnetwork from an alternative pipeline where the capacity of its original pipeline for injecting gas into the sub-network is restricted because of sudden equipment failure or physical constraints within the subnetwork .}
