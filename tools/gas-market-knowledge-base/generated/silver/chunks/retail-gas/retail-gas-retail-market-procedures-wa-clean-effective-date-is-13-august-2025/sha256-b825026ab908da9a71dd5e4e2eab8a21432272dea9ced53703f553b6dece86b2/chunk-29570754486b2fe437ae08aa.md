- (1) If, due to a transfer error or otherwise, the wrong user is recorded in the AEMO registry as the current user , then AEMO and the affected users must cooperate to correct this error by either:
2. (a) a user lodging an error correction notice under clause 32(2); or
- (b) a user lodging a new transfer request in  respect of the delivery point and entering  into  an  agreement  under  clause  77(2),  but  to  avoid  doubt  the correcting transfer must have only prospective effect.
- (2) Subject to participants' obligations under clause 32 to lodge an error correction notice in  respect  of  an  incorrect transfer  request , participants may  enter  into agreements if they cannot meet the requirements under clause 33 to lodge a valid error correction notice , to address or correct transfers which should have occurred but did not, or which occurred but should not have, or were otherwise in error (in this clause collectively 'transfer errors' ).
{Note:  The purpose of clause 77(2) is to permit 'off-market' correcting transactions.  For example, if the transfer day is in error.}
