A data change notice is valid only if:
- (a) the delivery point exists in the AEMO registry ; and
- (b) the delivery point's MIRN status is commissioned or decommissioned ; and
- (c) there is not, in relation to the delivery point:
- (i) an open data change transaction for the same item of AEMO standing data with  an  effective  date  on  or  after  the  effective  date  of  the data change notice ; or
- (ii) an open transaction that is not a data change transaction ; and
- (d) it  is  lodged by the network operator who has an active GBO identification ; and
- (e) the proposed amendment only relates to items 20(1)(f), 20(1)(g), 20(1)(h), 20(1)(k) or 20(1)(l) of the AEMO registry ; and
- (f) the proposed amendment relates to item 20(1)(h) of the AEMO registry , that the proposed gas zone exists in the AEMO registry ; and
