Within 5 business days after making a determination made under clause 341(2), the compliance panel may correct the determination if the determination contains:
- (a) a clerical mistake; or
- (b) an accidental slip or omission; or
- (c) a material miscalculation of figures or a material mistake in the description of any person, thing or matter referred to in the determination; or
- (d) a defect of form.
