Upon receipt of an error correction objection withdrawal notice which is not valid, AEMO must immediately :
- (a) reject the error correction objection withdrawal notice ; and
- (b) notify the participant that  lodged  the error  correction  objection  withdrawal notice that the error correction objection withdrawal notice has been rejected and provide the reason why the error correction objection withdrawal notice is not valid.
