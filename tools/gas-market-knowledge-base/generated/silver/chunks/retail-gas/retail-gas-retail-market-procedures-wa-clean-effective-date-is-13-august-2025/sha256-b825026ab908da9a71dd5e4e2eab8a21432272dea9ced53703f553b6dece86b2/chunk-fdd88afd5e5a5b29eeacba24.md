The functions of the compliance panel are to hear and make determinations on matters referred to it by AEMO or a participant regarding:
- (a) alleged breaches of the procedures ; or
- (b) the interpretation of the procedures ; or
- (c) any other matter that can be referred to the compliance panel under these procedures .
