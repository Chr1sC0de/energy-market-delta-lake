Upon receipt of a valid error correction objection , AEMO must:
- (a) forthwith accept the error correction objection ; and
- (b) promptly notify the  relevant participants that  the error correction objection has been accepted, which notice must provide at least:
- (i) details  of  the error  correction  notice to  which  the error  correction objection relates; and
- (ii) the process time of the error correction objection .
