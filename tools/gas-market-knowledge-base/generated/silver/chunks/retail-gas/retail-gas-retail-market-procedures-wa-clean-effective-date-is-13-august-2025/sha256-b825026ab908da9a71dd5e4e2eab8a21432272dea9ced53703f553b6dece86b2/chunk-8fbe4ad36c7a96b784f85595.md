Under section 11ZOC of the Energy Coordination Act 1994 (WA), where there is more than one gas distribution operator or retail gas operator on a gas distribution system, each gas distribution operator and retail gas operator must be bound by agreement to comply  with  the  relevant  provisions  of  an  'approved  retail  market  scheme'  for  that distribution system.
The WA  Gas  Retail  Market  Agreement is  the  agreement  between  gas  distribution operators and retail gas operators for the AEMO Scheme for the WA gas retail market, and requires AEMO and the Scheme participants to comply with these procedures .
In  addition,  gas  transmission  operators  and  persons  prescribed  under  regulations pursuant section 11ZOD of the Energy Coordination Act 1994 (WA) must comply with the relevant provisions of the 'retail market rules' made as part of an approved retail market scheme for a distribution system.  These procedures are the 'retail market rules' made as part of the approved retail market scheme.
