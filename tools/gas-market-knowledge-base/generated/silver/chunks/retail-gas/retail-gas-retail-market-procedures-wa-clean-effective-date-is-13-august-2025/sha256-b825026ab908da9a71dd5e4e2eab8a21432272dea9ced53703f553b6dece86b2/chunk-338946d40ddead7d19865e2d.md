For each user for each gate point for each gas day , AEMO must apply ( 'applied request' ) the user's validated procurement requests in the priority order specified by  the user ,  until  all  of  the user's  total  (pre-procurement)  swing  service is exhausted:
- (a) first, against the user's non-user-specific amount of swing service calculated under clause 266;
and, if the user's validated procurement requests exhaust the user's non-userspecific amount of swing service :
- (b) then,  against  the user's user-specific  amount  of swing  service calculated under clause 263.
