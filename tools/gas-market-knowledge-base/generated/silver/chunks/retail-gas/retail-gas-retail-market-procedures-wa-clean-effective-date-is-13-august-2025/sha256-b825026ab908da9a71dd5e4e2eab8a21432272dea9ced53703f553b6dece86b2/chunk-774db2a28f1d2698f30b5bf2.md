- (1) Subject to this Part 1.3, a notice or  other  communication connected with these procedures has no legal effect unless it is in writing and either sent in the format required under clause 9, or if no format is specified, given as follows:
2. (a) sent  by  electronic  mail  transmission  or  any  other  method  of  electronic communication  to  the  appropriate  nominated  electronic  address  of  the addressee; or
3. (b) sent by facsimile to the nominated facsimile number of the addressee; or
4. (c) delivered by hand to the nominated office of the addressee; or
5. (d) sent by post to the nominated postal address of the addressee.
