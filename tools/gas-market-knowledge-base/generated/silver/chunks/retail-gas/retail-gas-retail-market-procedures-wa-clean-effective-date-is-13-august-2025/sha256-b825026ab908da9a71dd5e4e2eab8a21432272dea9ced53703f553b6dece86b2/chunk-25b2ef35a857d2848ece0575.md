- (1) AEMO , a participant, pipeline operator, prescribed person or interested person may propose the making of procedures .
- (2) A proposal for the making of procedures must be in the format approved and published by AEMO ( proposed procedure change ).
- (3) The proposed procedure change must include:
4. (a) a draft of the proposed procedures ;
5. (b) a description of, and an explanation of the reasons for, the proposed procedures ;
6. (c) except where AEMO makes a proposed procedure change , the GBO identification or the identity (as applicable) of the person lodging the proposal ;
7. (d) the anticipated impact of the proposed change to the procedures on AEMO, participants , pipeline operators , prescribed persons, interested persons and customers ; and

AEMO
- (e) the proposed time frame for implementation of the proposed change to the procedures .
- (4) Within 2 business days after receiving a proposed procedure change , AEMO must notify the proponent confirming that the proposed procedure change was received.
