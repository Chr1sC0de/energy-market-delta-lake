- (1) Before lodging a transfer request with AEMO , an incoming user must obtain the transferring customer's explicit informed consent to the lodgement.
{Note:  This consent could be obtained at the same time, and on the same form, as consent under clause 72, and may include consent for the purposes of clause 82(a).}
- (2) By  lodging  a transfer  request with AEMO ,  the incoming  user represents  and warrants to AEMO that the incoming user has complied with clause 79(1).
{Note:  Under clause 376A(2), a breach of this warranty will expose the incoming user to liability for more than just direct damage.}
{Note: For the avoidance of doubt, Explicit Informed Consent is required for a 'move in' as defined under clause 78.}
