A network  operator must  as  a reasonable  and  prudent  person ensure  that  an interval meter remains:
- (a) capable  of  being  read  by  means  of telemetry in  order  to  satisfy  the requirements of these procedures ; and
- (b) at all times capable of aggregating the flow of gas across time, and recording that flow for each hour .
