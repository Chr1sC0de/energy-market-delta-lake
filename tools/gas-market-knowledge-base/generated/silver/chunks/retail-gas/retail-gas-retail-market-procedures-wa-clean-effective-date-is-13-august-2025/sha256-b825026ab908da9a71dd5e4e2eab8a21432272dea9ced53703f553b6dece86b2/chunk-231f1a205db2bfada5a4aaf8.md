AEMO must maintain all  data  collected,  received,  generated  or  sent  to  any person by AEMO under this Chapter 5 and any data that is the result of AEMO's latest final calculations for a gas day :
- (a) in a format that identifies:
- (i) the time and date the data was collected, received, generated or sent by AEMO ; and
- (ii) the person from whom AEMO collected or received the data, or to whom AEMO sent the data, or if AEMO generated the data, AEMO is identified as having generated the data, and
- (b) for at least 2 years, in a format that is accessible within 2 business days to enable the repeated performance of calculations AEMO is  responsible for performing under this Chapter 5 for any of and up to the previous 425 gas days ; and
- (i) at least another 5 years after that, in a format which is accessible within 5 business days .
