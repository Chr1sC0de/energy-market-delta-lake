- (1) For each sub-network for each gas day , each user must procure nominations from one or more related shippers or swing service providers (as applicable) within the time  frames  required  by  the related shipper's or swing  service  provider's transmission contract which are sufficient to satisfy the user's obligations under Part 5.2.
- (2) Nothing  in  clause  186(1)  prevents  a shipper's or swing  service  provider's nomination from being made in aggregate, to address the requirements of more than one user .
