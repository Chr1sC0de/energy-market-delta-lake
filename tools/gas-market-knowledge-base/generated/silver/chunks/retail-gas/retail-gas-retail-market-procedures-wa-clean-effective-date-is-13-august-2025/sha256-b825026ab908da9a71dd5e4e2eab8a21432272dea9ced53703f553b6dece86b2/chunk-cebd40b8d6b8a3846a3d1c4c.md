- (5) Membership to the gas retail  consultative forum is  to  be  open  to participants, pipeline  operators,  prescribed  persons and interested  persons and  will  be administered on such terms of reference as are required for its roles under this consultation process and the membership under this clause (5).
- (6) If  a  person  provides  information  about  the costs or  benefits  of  a proposed procedure change that the person identifies as confidential, AEMO must keep that information confidential provided that AEMO may use such information for the  purposes  of  the impact  and  implementation  report or  any  decision  under these procedures but may only disclose that information in a manner that does not identify the person to whom the information relates.
