- (a) In this clause 4:
- (i) words and expressions which are not defined in these terms but which have a defined meaning in the GST Law have the same meaning as in the GST Law ; and
- (ii) 'GST Law' has the meaning given in the A New Tax System (Goods and Services Tax) Act 1999 (Cth).
- (b) Unless  otherwise  expressly  stated,  all  prices  or  other  sums  payable  or consideration to be provided under these terms are exclusive of GST.
- (c) If GST is payable by:
- (i) a supplier; or
- (ii) by the representative Scheme participant for a GST group of which the supplier is a Scheme participant ,
on any supply made under these terms , the recipient of the supply must pay to the supplier an amount equal to the GST payable on the supply.
