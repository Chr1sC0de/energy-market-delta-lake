- (1) In this clause 311 the term 'conflict of interest' includes, but is not limited to:
2. (a) the holding of any office; or
3. (b) the entering into, or giving effect to, any contract, arrangement, understanding or relationship,
by a compliance panel member whereby, directly or indirectly, duties or interests are or might be created for the compliance panel member which conflict, or might reasonably be expected to conflict, with any one or more of the following:
- (c) the compliance panel member's duties in making a determination under this Chapter 6;
- (d) the interests of AEMO ; or
- (e) the  interests  of  one  or  more participants, pipeline  operator  or  prescribed person .
- (2) AEMO must  not  appoint  a  person  as  a compliance  panel member,  if AEMO becomes aware of an actual conflict of interest in the compliance panel member that might reasonably be expected to materially adversely affect the compliance panel member's independence and impartiality or the performance of his or her duties.
