Anticipated annual consumption is determined in accordance with the following table by having regard to the size of the meter installed at the delivery point:
- For an AL6 meter:, Anticipated Annual Consumption = 30 MJ/d. - For an AL12 meter:, Anticipated Annual Consumption = 250 MJ/d. - Other Basic Meters:, Anticipated Annual Consumption = Based on the nominal annual usage for that particular consumer
