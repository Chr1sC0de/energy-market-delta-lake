In this Part 5.3, 'user's gas injections' for a sub-network for a gas day means, as appropriate, either:
- (a) before  the  end  of  the gas day ,  the user's  total  nomination  amount under clause 198 minus the sum of the user's swing service repayment quantities for repayment on the gas day calculated under clause 299; or
- (b) after the end of the gas day , the user's estimated total withdrawals calculated under  clause  228  minus  the  sum  of  the user's  swing  service  repayment quantities for repayment on the gas day calculated under clause 299.
