- (3A)  A user may only specify the name of a swing service provider under clause 267(3)(f)(ii) if that swing service provider :
- (a) is a swing service provider on the same pipeline as the swing service provider from whom the user procured the swing service ; and
- (b) is listed in the shipper register as a swing service provider for the user lodging the procurement instruction in  respect  of  the pipeline and sub-network to which the procurement instruction relates ; and
- (c) its listing on the shipper register for the procuring user and the sub-network is effective for the gas day to which the procurement request relates.
