Upon receipt of a valid transfer withdrawal notice , AEMO must:
- (a) forthwith accept the transfer withdrawal notice ; and
- (b) forthwith cancel the requested transfer ; and
- (c) immediately  notify the current  user ,  the incoming  user and  the network operator that the transfer withdrawal notice has been accepted and that the requested transfer has been cancelled .
