- If, before or during a gas day :
- (a) a user's related shipper renominates for the gas day ; or
- (b) a user becomes aware that its related shipper's nomination for the gas day is to be adjusted under its transmission contract , or that the pipeline operator does not plan to inject gas in accordance with the shipper's or swing service providers (as applicable) nomination for the gas day,
in a manner which will cause a change to the user's pipeline nomination amount , then:
- (c) the user must immediately notify AEMO of  the  revised user's  pipeline nomination amount for the relevant pipeline and sub-network , and
- (d) the user may give AEMO a revised allocation instruction under clause 189 for the sub-network .
