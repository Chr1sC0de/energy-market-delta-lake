{Note:  The purpose of this Part 6.3 which imposes a requirement that matters be referred to AEMO before being referred to the compliance panel or the Economic Regulation Authority , is:
- (a) to provide a speedy mechanism to resolve minor matters without activating the compliance panel or requiring an investigation by the Economic Regulation Authority ; and
- (b) to impose a filter which discourages the referral of vexatious or frivolous claims to  the compliance panel or the Economic Regulation Authority .}
