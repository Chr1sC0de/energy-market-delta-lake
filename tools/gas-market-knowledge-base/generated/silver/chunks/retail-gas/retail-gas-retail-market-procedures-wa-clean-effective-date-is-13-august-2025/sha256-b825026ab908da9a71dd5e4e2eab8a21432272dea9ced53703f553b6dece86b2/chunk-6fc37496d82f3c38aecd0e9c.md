- (6) AEMO must record a person's GBO identification as 'suspended' in the AEMO registry :
9. (a) if the person is a Scheme participant, upon the person ceasing to be a party to the WA Gas Retail Market Agreement ; and
10. (b) if the person is a party to an agreement entered into under these procedures which  provides  for  the  person's GBO  identification to  be  recorded  as 'suspended' - in accordance with the terms of the agreement.
- (7) The  consequences  of AEMO recording a person's GBO  identification as 'suspended' in the AEMO registry are:
12. (a) the  person  must  continue  to  comply  with  its  obligations  under  these procedures ; and
13. (b) for a user - without limiting clause 22(7)(a), the user is not entitled to exercise any rights granted to it under Chapter 2, Chapter 3, or Chapter 9; and
14. (c) for  a swing  service  provider -  without  limiting  clause  22(7)(a),  the swing service provider is not entitled to exercise its rights under clause 281;
