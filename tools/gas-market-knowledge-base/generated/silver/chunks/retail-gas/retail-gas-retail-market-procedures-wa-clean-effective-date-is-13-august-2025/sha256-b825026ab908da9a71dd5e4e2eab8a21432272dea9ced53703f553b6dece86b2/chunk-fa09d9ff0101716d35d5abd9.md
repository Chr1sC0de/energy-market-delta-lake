- (1) Each person required, or who has agreed, to comply with some or all of these procedures (as applicable), must participate in the readiness testing process and must satisfy the readiness criteria prior to the date they are required to comply with these procedures .
- (2) Prior  to AEMO issuing  a GBO  identification under  clause  22(2), AEMO must determine as a reasonable and prudent person whether a person referred to under clause 21B(1) has satisfied the readiness criteria ,  and  if  so,  issue  a readiness certificate to the person.
