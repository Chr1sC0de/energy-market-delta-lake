Upon  receipt  of  an error  correction  notice which  is  not  valid, AEMO must immediately :
- (a) reject the error correction notice ; and
- (b) notify the participant that  lodged  the error  correction  notice that  the error correction notice has been rejected and provide the reason why the error correction notice is not valid.
{Note: A previous user or a network operator wishing to reinitiate an error correction notice that has been rejected must lodge a new error correction notice .}
