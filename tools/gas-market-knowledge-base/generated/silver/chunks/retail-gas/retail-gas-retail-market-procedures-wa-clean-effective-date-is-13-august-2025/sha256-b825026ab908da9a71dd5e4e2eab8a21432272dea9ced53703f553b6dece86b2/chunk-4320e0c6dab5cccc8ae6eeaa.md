- (1) AEMO may  only  make procedures if AEMO is  satisfied  that  the procedures are consistent with the following objectives:
2. (a) to ensure that the retail gas market operates and is governed in a manner that is:
3. (i) open and competitive; and
4. (ii) efficient; and
5. (iii) fair to participants and their customers ; and
6. (b) to ensure compliance with all applicable laws ; and
7. (c) to ensure consultation with all participants , pipeline operators , prescribed persons and interested persons as is reasonably required to meet the objectives set out in this procedure .
