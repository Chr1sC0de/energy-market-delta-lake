- (1) AEMO must establish a gas retail consultative forum in respect of the WA gas retail market.
{Note: for the avoidance of doubt, the gas retail consultative forum under clause 381(1) may be the result of combining the gas retail consultative forum in respect of the WA gas retail market with consultative forums in respect of other gas retail markets}
- (2) The gas retail consultative forum will examine the proposed procedure change and may nominate specialist working groups for this purpose.
- (3) The gas retail consultative forum is to advise AEMO and is to assist AEMO in making a determination on the following matters by the date in clause (4):
3. (a) its  analysis  of  the costs and  benefits  (either  tangible  or  intangible)  of making the requested procedures;
4. (b) material issues and  risks for the implementation  of  the proposed procedure change ;
