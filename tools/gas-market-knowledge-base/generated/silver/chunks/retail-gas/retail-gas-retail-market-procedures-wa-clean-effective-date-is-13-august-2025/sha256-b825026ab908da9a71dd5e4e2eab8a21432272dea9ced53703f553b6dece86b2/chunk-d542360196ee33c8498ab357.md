Upon  receipt  of  an error  correction  objection which  is  not  valid, AEMO must immediately :
- (a) reject the error correction objection ; and
- (b) notify the participant that lodged the error correction objection that the error correction objection has been rejected and provide the reason why the error correction objection is not valid.
