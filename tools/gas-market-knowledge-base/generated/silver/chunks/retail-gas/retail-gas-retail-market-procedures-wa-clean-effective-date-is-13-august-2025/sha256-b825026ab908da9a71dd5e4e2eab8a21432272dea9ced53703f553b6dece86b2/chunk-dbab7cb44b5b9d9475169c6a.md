For each user for each gate point for each gas day , AEMO must determine the user's total swing service cost as follows:
$$UC - USAC + NUSAC$$
where:
UC
=
the user's total swing service cost for the gate point for the gas day ;
USAC
=
the user's user-specific swing service cost for the gate point for the gas day calculated under clause 292; and
NUSAC
=
the user's non-user-specific swing service cost for the gate point for the gas day calculated under clause 293.
