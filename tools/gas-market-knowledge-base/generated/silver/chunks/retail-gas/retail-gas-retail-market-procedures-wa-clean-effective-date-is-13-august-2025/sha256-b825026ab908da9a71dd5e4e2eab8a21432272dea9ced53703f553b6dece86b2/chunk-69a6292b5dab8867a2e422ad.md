- (a) A clause  192(2)  notice creates  a  legally binding  contract  ( 'deemed contract' ) on the terms set out in this agreement between the shipper and the contracting user under which:
- (b) ( shipper deemed to have injected ) the shipper is deemed to have injected the apportioned injections for the contracted day for the contracted charge ; and
- (c) ( user  must  accept  and  pay )  the user is  deemed  to  have  accepted  the apportioned injections for the contracted day , and must pay the shipper the contracted charges .
