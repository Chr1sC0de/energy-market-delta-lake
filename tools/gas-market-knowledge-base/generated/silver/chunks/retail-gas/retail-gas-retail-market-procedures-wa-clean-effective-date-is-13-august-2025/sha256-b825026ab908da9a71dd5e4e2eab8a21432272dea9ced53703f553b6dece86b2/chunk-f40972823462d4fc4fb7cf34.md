Nothing in these procedures :
- (a) prevents a participant, pipeline operator or prescribed person from charging for  doing  a  thing, if  the  person  is  entitled  at law or  under  an Access Arrangement or a contract other than these procedures to charge for doing that thing; and
- (b) allows  a participant,  pipeline  operator or prescribed  person to  charge  for doing  a  thing  (including  providing  information  or  taking  any  action)  under these procedures , unless otherwise stated in these procedures .
