- (i) Where a swing service contract under this deed requires a person to pay or reimburse an expense or outgoing of another person, the amount to be paid or reimbursed by the first person will be the sum of:
- (i) the amount of the expense or outgoing less any input tax credits in respect of the expense or outgoing to which the other person, or to which the representative Scheme participant for a GST group of which the other person is a Scheme participant , is entitled; and
- (ii) if the payment or reimbursement is subject to GST, an amount equal to that GST.
