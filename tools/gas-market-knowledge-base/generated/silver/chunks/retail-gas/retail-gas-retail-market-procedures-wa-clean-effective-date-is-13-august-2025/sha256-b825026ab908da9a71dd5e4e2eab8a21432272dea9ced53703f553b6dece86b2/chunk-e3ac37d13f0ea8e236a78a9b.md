- (1) For each allocation made under clause 295, AEMO must, within 5 hours after the end of the gas day , give a contract note to the user and the swing service provider , specifying:
2. (a) the name of the swing service provider ;
3. (b) the name of the user ;
4. (c) the gate point to which the allocation relates;
5. (d) the FSS to  be  paid  to  the swing service provider by the user determined under clause 295(1);
6. (e) whether  the  allocated swing  service is park  swing  service or loan  swing service ;
7. (f) the SSA provided to the user by the swing service provider determined under clause 295(2), in megajoules;
8. (g) the gas day in respect of which the allocation under clause 295 was made.
