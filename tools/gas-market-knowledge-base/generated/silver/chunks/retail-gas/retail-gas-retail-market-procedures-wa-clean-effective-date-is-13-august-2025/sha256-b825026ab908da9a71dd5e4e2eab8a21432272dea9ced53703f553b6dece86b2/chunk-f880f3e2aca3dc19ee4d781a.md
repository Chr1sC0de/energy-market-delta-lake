{Note: These procedures assume that all activities relating to meter reading , and meter reading data , for delivery points are undertaken by the network operator .    This procedure is included to facilitate changes should that assumption no longer be correct.}
- (1) If a person other than a network operator is to undertake activities relating to meter reading , or metering data , for delivery points , then AEMO , the network operator and all affected participants must work cooperatively to agree either or both of:
2. (a) suitable procedures to accommodate the fact; or
3. (b) suitable changes to these procedures for submission under Chapter 9.
- (2) Nothing in this clause 150 permits a person to do anything it is not permitted by law or a contract other than these procedures to do .
