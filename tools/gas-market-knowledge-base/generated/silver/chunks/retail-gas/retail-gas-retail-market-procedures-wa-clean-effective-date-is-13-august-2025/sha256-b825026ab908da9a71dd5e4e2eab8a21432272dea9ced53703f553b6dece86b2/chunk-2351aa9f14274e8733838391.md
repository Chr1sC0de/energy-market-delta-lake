On each gas day for  each user for  each sub-network ,  for  the  purposes  of  this Chapter 5, the gas injected or repaid (as applicable) into the sub-network by or on behalf of the user is allocated as follows:
- (a) first, the gas is deemed to be the user's swing service repayment quantities for repayment on  the gas day calculated under clause 299 until all of the user's swing service repayment quantities have been repaid ;
- (b) thereafter,  the  gas  is  deemed  to  be  the user's  reconciliation  adjustment amount for injection on the gas day calculated under clause 243 until the full reconciliation adjustment amount has been injected ; and
- (c) thereafter,  the  remainder  of  the  gas  is  available  to  satisfy  the user's withdrawals from the sub-network on the gas day .
