Upon receipt of both a valid permanent removal confirmation notice lodged under clause 127(2)(c) and valid metering data in accordance with clause 127(2)(e), AEMO must:
- (a) forthwith accept the permanent removal confirmation notice ; and
- (b) promptly notify the network operator that the permanent removal confirmation notice has been accepted.
{Note:  Upon accepting a valid permanent removal confirmation notice under clause 132 and receiving  valid metering data in  accordance  with  clause  127(2)(e), AEMO must update the AEMO registry under clause 51(d) by changing the MIRN status to deregistered and provide the user and network operator with the AEMO standing data for the delivery point under clause 53.}
