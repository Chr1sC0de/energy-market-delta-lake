- (a) Unless  this  deed  or  the users specify  otherwise,  whenever  the SSP is permitted or required to give a notice or other communication under this deed to the users , it must do so by giving it to AEMO on the users' behalf, and not directly to the users .
- (b) Clause  6.1(a)  does  not  apply  if  due  to  the  insolvency  of AEMO or  other extraordinary circumstance it would prevent the SSP from communicating effectively with the users .
- (c) To avoid doubt a tax invoice under clause 8 is to be given to the user named in the tax invoice , and not to AEMO the users .
