- (2) For each user for each gate point for each gas day , the user's SRQ for a swing service provider is determined as follows:
3. (a) where a swing service provider must repay swing service on behalf of a user as a result of an applied request under clause 270 -
4. (i) the SRQ is the amount of swing service applied in the applied request , or the proportion of the amount applied as specified in the procurement request under clause 267(3)(f); and
5. (ii) the SRQ must be repaid by the swing service provider specified in the procurement  request under  clause  267(3)(f)(i)  or 267(3)(f)(ii),  as applicable;
