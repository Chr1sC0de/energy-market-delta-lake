- (ii) 5 business days after receiving the permanent removal request . {Note:  The reason that there may not be a meter installed at the delivery point previously have been removed in the course of a disconnection.}
- (2) As soon as practicable after a delivery point has been permanently removed under clause 125(1) or clause 127(1)(c), and in any event within 5 business days , the network operator must:
- (a) calculate the actual value for the delivery point using the information obtained under clause 127(1)(c); and
- (b) change the MIRN status to deregistered ; and
- (c) notify the user that the delivery point has been permanently removed ; and
- (d) lodge a permanent removal confirmation notice with AEMO ; and
- (e) provide AEMO and the user with the metering data under clause 158 from, as applicable:
- (i) the meter reading undertaken under clause 127(1)(c); or
- (ii) if there was no meter installed at the delivery point , the meter reading undertaken under clause 107(2) when the meter was removed.
