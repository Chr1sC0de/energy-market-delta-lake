A referral must be in writing and must specify at least the following information:
- (a) the identity of the person lodging the referral with the compliance panel ; and
- (b) such  information as AEMO has  regarding  the matter, including  any preliminary view formed by AEMO; and
- (c) if the referral is an appeal under clause 139(5) or clause 331 - the grounds of appeal; and
- (d) if the referral is under clause 332(b), the decision sought; and
- (e) If the referral is under clause 332(b), the orders sought.
