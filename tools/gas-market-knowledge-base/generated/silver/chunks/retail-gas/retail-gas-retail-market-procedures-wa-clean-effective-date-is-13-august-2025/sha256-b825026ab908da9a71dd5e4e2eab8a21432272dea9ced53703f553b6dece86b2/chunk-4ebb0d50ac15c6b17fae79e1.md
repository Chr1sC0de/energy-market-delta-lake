Upon  receipt  of  a transfer  withdrawal  notice which  is  not  valid, AEMO must immediately :
- (a) reject the transfer withdrawal notice ; and
- (b) notify the participant that  lodged  the transfer  withdrawal  notice that  the transfer withdrawal notice has been rejected and provide the reason why the transfer withdrawal notice is not valid.
