Upon receipt of a disconnection notice which is not valid, subject to clause 105(5), the network operator must immediately :
- (a) reject the disconnection notice ; and
- (b) notify the participant that lodged the disconnection notice that the disconnection  notice has  been  rejected  and  provide  the  reason  why  the disconnection notice is not valid.
