AEMO
Upon receipt of a valid error correction withdrawal notice , AEMO must:
- (a) forthwith accept the error correction withdrawal notice ; and
- (b) forthwith cancel the error correction transaction ; and
- (c) promptly notify the affected participants that the error correction withdrawal notice has been accepted and that the error correction transaction has been cancelled .
