- (a) Subject to clause 3.5, if a compensating person fails by the relevant due date to make full payment of the net amount shown on an invoice then, without prejudice to the recipient user's other rights, the compensating person must pay interest on the unpaid amount, calculated daily at the prescribed interest rate from the due date until payment.
- (b) This clause does not limit clause 300A(12).
