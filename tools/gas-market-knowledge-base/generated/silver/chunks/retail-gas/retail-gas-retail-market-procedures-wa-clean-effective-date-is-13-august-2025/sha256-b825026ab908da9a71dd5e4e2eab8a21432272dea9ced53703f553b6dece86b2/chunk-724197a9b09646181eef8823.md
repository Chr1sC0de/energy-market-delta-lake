- (8) Where these procedures require  a  person  to  pay  or  reimburse  an  expense  or outgoing of another person, the amount to be paid or reimbursed by the first person will be the sum of:
2. (a) the amount of the expense or outgoing less any input tax credits in respect of  the  expense  or  outgoing  to  which  the  other  person,  or  to  which  the representative Scheme participant for a GST group of which the other person is a Scheme participant , is entitled; and
3. (b) if the payment or reimbursement is subject to GST, an amount equal to that GST.
