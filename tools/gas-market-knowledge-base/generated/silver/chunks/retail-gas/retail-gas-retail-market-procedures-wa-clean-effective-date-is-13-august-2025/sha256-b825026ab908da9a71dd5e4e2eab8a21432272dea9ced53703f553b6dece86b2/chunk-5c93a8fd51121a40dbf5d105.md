- (1) Subject to clause 79 and clause 80(2), an incoming user may lodge a transfer request with AEMO on any day.
- (2) An incoming user that is a self-contracting user may only lodge a transfer request in respect of a delivery point at which it is the customer .
- (3) By lodging a transfer request with AEMO , the self-contracting user represents and warrants to AEMO that the self-contracting user is the customer for the delivery point to which the transfer request relates.
{Note:  Under clause 376A(2), a breach of this warranty will expose the self-contracting user to liability for more than just direct damage.}
