- (a) The shipper may issue an invoice in the form of a tax invoice to a user for the contracted charges forthwith after the end of the contracted day .
- (b) The shipper may from time to time issue a tax invoice in respect of part only of  the contracted  charges ,  even  if  one  or  more  other  components  of  the contracted charges have not yet been determined, and may subsequently issue another tax invoice or tax invoices for one or more other components of the contracted charges , and may keep doing so from time to time for so long as any element of the contracted charges remains uninvoiced.
