Upon receipt of a transfer objection withdrawal notice which is not valid, AEMO must immediately :
- (a) reject the transfer objection withdrawal notice ; and
- (b) notify the participant that lodged the transfer objection withdrawal notice that the transfer objection withdrawal notice has been rejected and provide the reason why the transfer objection withdrawal notice is not valid.
