If in the compliance panel's reasonable opinion:
- (a) a  referral  lodged  under  clause  139(5)  or  clause  331  does  not  satisfy  the requirements set out in clause 333 (as applicable); or
- (b) the grounds of appeal are not sufficient having regard to the nature of the decision the appeal is in relation to and the reasons given for that decision,
then the compliance panel may decline to accept the referral within 20 business days of receipt of the referral .
{Note:  If participant wishes to re-lodge the appeal, it may do so by lodging a further referral within the time frame indicated in clause 331(1).}
