- (b) the network operator must lodge a data change notice under clause 27(1)(a) in respect of each delivery point affected by the network operator's proposed change to the AEMO standing data .
{Note:  The next procedure dealing with multiple data change transactions is clause 31.}
- (3) A data change notice must specify at least the following information:
2. (a) the MIRN ; and
3. (b) the GBO  identification of  the network  operator lodging  the data  change notice ; and
4. (c) the proposed amendment to the AEMO standing data ; and
5. (d) the reason for the proposed amendment; and
6. (e) the earliest date that the proposed amendment to the AEMO standing data can be registered in the AEMO registry .
