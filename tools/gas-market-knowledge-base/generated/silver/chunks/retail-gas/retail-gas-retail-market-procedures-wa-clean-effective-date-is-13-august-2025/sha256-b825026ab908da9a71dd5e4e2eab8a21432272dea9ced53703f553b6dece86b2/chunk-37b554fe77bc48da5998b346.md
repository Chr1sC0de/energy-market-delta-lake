If AEMO :
- (a) receives a valid transfer objection ; and
- (b) does not receive a valid transfer objection withdrawal notice within the time period specified under clause 91(1),
then AEMO must:
- (c) before the start of the next business day , cancel the requested transfer ; then
- (d) promptly , notify the incoming user , the current user and the network operator that the requested transfer has been cancelled .
{Note: An incoming user wishing to reinitiate a requested transfer that has been cancelled must lodge a new transfer request .}
