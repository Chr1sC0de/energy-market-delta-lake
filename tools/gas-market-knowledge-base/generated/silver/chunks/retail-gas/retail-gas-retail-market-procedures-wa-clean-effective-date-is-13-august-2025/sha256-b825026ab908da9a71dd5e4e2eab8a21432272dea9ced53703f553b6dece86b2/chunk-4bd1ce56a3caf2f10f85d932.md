Upon receipt of a new connection confirmation notice which is not valid, AEMO must immediately :
- (a) reject the new connection confirmation notice ; and
| Doc Ref:  | 13 August 2025
- (b) notify the network  operator that  lodged  the new  connection  confirmation notice that the new connection confirmation notice has been rejected and provide the reason why the new connection confirmation notice is not valid.
{Note: A network operator wishing to reinitiate a new connection confirmation notice that has been rejected must lodge a new new connection confirmation notice .}
