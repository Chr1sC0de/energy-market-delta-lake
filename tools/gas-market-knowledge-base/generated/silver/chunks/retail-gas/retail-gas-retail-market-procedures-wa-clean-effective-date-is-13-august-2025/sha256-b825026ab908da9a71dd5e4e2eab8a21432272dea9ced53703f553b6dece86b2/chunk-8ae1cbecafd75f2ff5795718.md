A  person  required  by  this  Part  7.2  to  appoint  an auditor must  ensure  that  the auditor 's report of a negative assurance audit under this Part 7.2 at least:
- (a) provides reasonable detail regarding the auditor 's investigations  and methodology; and
- (b) details any material restrictions or deficiencies in the auditor 's access to or use of relevant documents or records; and
- (c) without  limiting  clause  358(b),  details  the  circumstances  of  any  noncompliance  by  a participant or AEMO with  clause  357,  in  respect  of  the negative assurance audit ; and
- (d) complies with the deed of undertaking under clause 356(1); and
- (e) makes all disclosures required under clause 354(2)(a); and
- (f) either:
- (i) states that the negative  assurance  audit did not disclose  noncompliance; or
- (ii) provides details of each breach, non-compliance or other circumstance which prevents a statement under clause 358(f)(i) being made.
