To minimise the number of data fields required in the AEMO registry and the network operators'  databases the  concepts  of  licence  area, sub-network and heating  value zones are all coded using a single 5 digit gas zone code, as follows:
{Note:  The following code is split into two components:
- (a) AB  -  which  is  held  in  the  existing  two  digit  transmission  zone  and  identifies  the network operator , licence and Access Arrangement coverage; and
- (b) CCD - which is held in the existing three digit heating value zone and identifies the sub-network and gas zone within the sub-network .}
