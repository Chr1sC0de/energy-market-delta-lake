- If, in relation to a delivery point , any of the following information changes:
- (a) the MIRN status ; and
- (b) the meter number ; and
- (c) the meter type ; and
- (d) the index type ; and
- (e) the gas zone code ; and
- (f) the pressure correction factor ; and
- (g) for a basic meter - the number of dials,
then,  the network  operator must  provide  at  least  the  updated  item  of MIRN standing data or meter standing data (as applicable) to the user , before providing the metering data under clause 158.
