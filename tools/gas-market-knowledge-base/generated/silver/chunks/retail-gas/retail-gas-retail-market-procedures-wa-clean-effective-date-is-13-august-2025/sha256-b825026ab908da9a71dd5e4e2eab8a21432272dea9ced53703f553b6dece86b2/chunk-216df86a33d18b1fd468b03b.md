- (1) Before close of business at the expiry of 2 full business days after the process time notified  under  clause  85(1)(c)(v),  if  the requested  transfer is  not  a move in ,  a network operator may lodge a transfer objection with AEMO on the ground that the incoming user has not entered into a haulage contract in respect of the delivery point and its metering equipment with the network operator .
- (2) A transfer  objection under  clause  86(1)  must  specify  at  least  the  following information:
3. (a) details of the transfer request to which the transfer objection relates; and
and
- (b) the GBO identification of the network operator lodging the transfer objection.
