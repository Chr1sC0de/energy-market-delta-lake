Upon receipt of a transfer request which is not valid, AEMO must immediately :
- (a) reject the transfer request ; and
- (b) notify the incoming  user that  the transfer  request has  been  rejected  and provide the reason why the transfer request is not valid.
{Note: An incoming user wishing to reinitiate a requested transfer that has been rejected must lodge a new transfer request .}
