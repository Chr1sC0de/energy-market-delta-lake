- (1) Upon receipt of a valid disconnection notice , subject to clause 105(5), a network operator must:
2. (a) forthwith accept the disconnection notice ; and
3. (b) immediately notify the user that the disconnection notice has been accepted.
- (2) Subject to clause 108(3), a network operator must (subject to law ) at the same time, within  2 business  days after  receiving  a  valid disconnection  notice , disconnect and undertake a meter reading of, and obtain the meter reading data for, the delivery point.

7. (b) change the MIRN status in its MIRN database to decommissioned ; and
