A user remains recorded in the AEMO registry as the current user until such time as:
- (a) the MIRN is deregistered ; or
- (b) a transfer occurs in respect of the delivery point ; or
- (c) AEMO accepts an error correction transaction in respect of the delivery point
- .
{Note:  This procedure meets the requirement of section 11WL of the Energy Coordination Act 1994 (WA).}
