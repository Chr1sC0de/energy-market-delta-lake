Upon receipt of a valid error correction objection withdrawal notice , AEMO must:
- (a) forthwith accept the error correction objection withdrawal notice ; and
- (b) forthwith cancel the corresponding error correction objection ; and
- (c) promptly notify the  relevant participants that  the error correction objection withdrawal notice has been accepted and the corresponding error correction objection has been withdrawn, which notice must provide at least details of the error  correction  objection to  which  the error correction  objection withdrawal notice relates.
