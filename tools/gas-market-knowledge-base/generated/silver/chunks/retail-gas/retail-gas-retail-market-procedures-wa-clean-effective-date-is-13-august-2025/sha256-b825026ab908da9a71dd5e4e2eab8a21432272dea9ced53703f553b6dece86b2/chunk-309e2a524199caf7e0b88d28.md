- (1) The compliance  panel chairperson  must  ensure  that  each compliance  panel proceeding is minuted.
- (2) Within 5 business days after a compliance panel proceeding , the compliance panel chairperson  must  circulate  the  draft  minutes  of  the proceeding to  the  other members of the compliance panel for consideration and approval.
- (3) AEMO must ensure that the minutes of a compliance panel proceeding are made available to, and accessible by each compliance panel member for 7 years after the date of the proceeding .
