- (1) There is no clause 138(1).
- (2) The network operator must,  at  least  once  every  6 months, assess each basicmetered  delivery  point in  each  of  its sub-networks to  determine  whether  the quantity of gas delivered to the delivery point in the year immediately preceding the day of assessment exceeded 10 TJ, and if so the network operator must notify the user of its assessment and of the quantity of gas delivered during the year in
respect of those basic-metered delivery points where the quantity of gas delivered to the delivery point exceeded 10 TJ.
