If  the  position  of compliance panel chairperson is vacant, all compliance panel proceedings are suspended until:
- (a) AEMO appoints a compliance panel chairperson under clause 309; or
- (b) such time as AEMO determines.
{Note: Compliance panel proceedings are suspended if there is no chairperson with a legal background because of the nature of compliance panel proceedings .}
