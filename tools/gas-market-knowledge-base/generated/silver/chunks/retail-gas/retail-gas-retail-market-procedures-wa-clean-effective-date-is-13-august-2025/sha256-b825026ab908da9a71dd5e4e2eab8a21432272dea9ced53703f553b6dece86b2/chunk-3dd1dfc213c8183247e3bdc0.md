3. (a) the law , Access Arrangement or licence condition will prevail to the extent of the inconsistency; and
4. (b) by virtue of clause 7(1), each of AEMO , a participant , a pipeline operator and a prescribed person (as  applicable) is excused from performing its obligations under these procedures to the extent of the inconsistency.
