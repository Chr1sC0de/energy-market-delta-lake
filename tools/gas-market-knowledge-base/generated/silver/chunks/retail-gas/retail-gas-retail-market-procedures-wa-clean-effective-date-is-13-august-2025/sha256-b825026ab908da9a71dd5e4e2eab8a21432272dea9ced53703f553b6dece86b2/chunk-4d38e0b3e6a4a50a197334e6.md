- (1) For each gas day ,  for  each gas zone in a network operator's sub-network ,  the network operator must publish the daily flow weighted average heating value data used for billing purposes for delivery points in the gas zone .
- (2) A publication under clause 171(1) must be made:
3. (a) available in electronic form that can be remotely accessed for downloading by a participant ; and
4. (b) by noon on the next business day .
- (3) Data  published  under  clause  171(1)  must  remain  accessible  under  clause 171(2)(a) for at least 12 months after the gas day .
- (4) Heating value data for  a gas zone is  not  commercially sensitive or confidential information .
