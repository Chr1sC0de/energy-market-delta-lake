- (1) AEMO may, by notice published on its website, extend a time limit fixed by or under this chapter if:
2. (a) the  relevant proposed  procedure  change raises  questions  of  such complexity or difficulty that an extension of the time limit is justified; or
3. (b) a material change of circumstances occurs justifying the extension of the time limit.
- (2) A notice published under subclause (1) must state the reasons for the extension.
