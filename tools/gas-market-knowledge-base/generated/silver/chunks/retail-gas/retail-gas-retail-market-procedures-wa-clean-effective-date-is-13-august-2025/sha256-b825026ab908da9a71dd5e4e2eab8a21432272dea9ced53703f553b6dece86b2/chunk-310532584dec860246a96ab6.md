For each sub-network , AEMO may, after consultation with the network operator , determine  as  a reasonable  and  prudent  person one  or  more miscellaneous reconciliation amounts (each an 'MRA' ) for gas day D for any one or more users in the sub-network or for the sub-network .
{Note:  In most cases if a user is allocated a miscellaneous reconciliation amount , another user will be allocated an equal and opposite miscellaneous reconciliation amount .}
