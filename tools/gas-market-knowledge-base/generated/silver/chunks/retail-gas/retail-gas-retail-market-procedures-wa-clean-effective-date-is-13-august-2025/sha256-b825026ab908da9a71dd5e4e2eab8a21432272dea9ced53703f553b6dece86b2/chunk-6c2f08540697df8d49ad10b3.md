- (1) If AEMO is notified under clause 175 that a sub-network will be operating with one pressure control pipeline and one flow profile control pipeline , then:
2. (a) within  15 business days of AEMO receiving  the notice under  clause  175, AEMO must provide  to  the pipeline  operator of  the  flow  profile  control pipeline :
3. (i) the set of profiles referred to in clause 200(1); and
4. (ii) the set of principles referred to in clause 200(2)(b), which must be applied by the pipeline operator of the flow profile control pipeline in the selection of a profile for the operation of the flow profile control pipeline for the sub-network for each gas day ;
