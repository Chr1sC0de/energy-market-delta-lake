- (1) A network  operator must,  in  accordance  with  this  clause  62,  ensure  that  the information stored in its MIRN database and its MSD database is accurate .
- (2) Clause 62(1) does not require a network operator to undertake any investigation in respect of the items referred to in clauses 60(1)(i) and 60(1)(j), but this clause 62 (2) does not limit the network operator's obligations under clause 61(3) if it is notified under clause 61(2) of a necessary change to either of those items.
- (3) Upon  updating  its MIRN  database and MSD  database ,  except  as  a  result  of undertaking  a disconnection under  clause  105(1)(c)  or a reconnection under clause 117(1)(c), a network operator must promptly provide at least the updated item of MIRN standing data or meter standing data (as applicable) for a delivery point to the current user .
