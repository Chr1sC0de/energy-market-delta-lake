2. (a) forthwith accept the transfer request ; and
3. (b) immediately  notify the incoming  user that  the transfer  request has  been accepted which notice must provide at least the following details from the transfer request :
4. (i) the unique identifier assigned by AEMO to the transfer request ; and
5. (ii) the process time of the transfer request ; and
