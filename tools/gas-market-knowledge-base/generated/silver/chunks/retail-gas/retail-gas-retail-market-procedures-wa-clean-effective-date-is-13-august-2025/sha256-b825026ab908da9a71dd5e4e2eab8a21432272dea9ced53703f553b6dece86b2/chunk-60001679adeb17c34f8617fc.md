If AEMO :
- (a) receives a notice under clause 325(1); or
- (b) requires an interpretation of the proper meaning of a procedure
AEMO must before making a decision under clause 329(1) give a notice to each participant, pipeline operator, or prescribed person or AEMO affected specifying:
- (c) if the matter relates to an alleged breach of the procedures by a participant, pipeline operator, prescribed person or AEMO :
- (i) the GBO identification of the participant, pipeline operator, prescribed person or AEMO that is alleged to have breached the procedures ; and
- (ii) the  identity  of  each  person  that  is  involved  with  or  affected  by  the alleged breach of the procedures ; and
- (iii) the details of the alleged breach of the procedures ; or
- (d) if the matter relates to an interpretation of the procedures :
- (i) the procedure that requires interpretation and the reason why; and
- (ii) the circumstances in which, the interpretation is required.
