AEMO must:
- (a) upon accepting an error correction transaction in relation to:
- (i) an incorrect new connection confirmation notice; or
- (ii) an incorrect permanent removal confirmation notice,
for a delivery point under clause 35(a) ⎯ forthwith correct the relevant item of AEMO standing data in the AEMO registry , to take effect from the start of the gas day specified in clause 47(b) or 47(c) (as applicable); and
- (b) upon completing an error  correction  transaction in  relation  to  an  incorrect transfer for a delivery point under clause 46 ⎯ forthwith correct the relevant
AEMO
item of AEMO standing data in the AEMO registry ,  to  take effect from the start of the gas day specified in clause 47(a).
