- (1) A network  operator must maintain or  archive  previous metering  data for  each delivery point in its GDS :
2. (a) in a readily accessible format for at least two years; and
3. (b) after that for at least a further 5 years in a format which is accessible within a reasonable period of time.
- (2) The network operator must maintain or archive previous meter reading data for each delivery point in its GDS with an interval meter for at least 7 years in a format which is accessible within 5 business days.
