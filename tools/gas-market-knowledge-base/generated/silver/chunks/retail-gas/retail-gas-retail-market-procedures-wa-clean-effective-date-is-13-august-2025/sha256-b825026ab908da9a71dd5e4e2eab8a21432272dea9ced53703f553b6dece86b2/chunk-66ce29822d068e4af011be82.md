A range of checks are conducted on hourly data comparing Vun (volume uncorrected), Vcr (volume corrected for pressure and temperature), pressure and temperature values with site specific upper and lower limits.
Also included are checks to ensure the integrity of the incoming data e.g. Vcr > 0 when Vun = 0. Vcr is calculated based on the hourly Vun, pressure and temperature and compared against the Vcr value  supplied by  the  logging device.
A further inclusion is a device health check, we establish that there are no device status alarms and that battery voltage is at a satisfactory level.
