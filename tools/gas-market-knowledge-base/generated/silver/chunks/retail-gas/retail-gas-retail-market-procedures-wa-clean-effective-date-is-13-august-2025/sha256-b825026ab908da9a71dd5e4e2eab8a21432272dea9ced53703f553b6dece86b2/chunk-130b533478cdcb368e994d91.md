Upon receipt of a reconnection notice which is not valid, subject to clause 117(5), the network operator must immediately :
- (a) reject the reconnection notice ; and
- (b) notify the participant that lodged the reconnection notice that the reconnection  notice has  been  rejected  and  provide  the  reason  why  the reconnection notice is not valid.
