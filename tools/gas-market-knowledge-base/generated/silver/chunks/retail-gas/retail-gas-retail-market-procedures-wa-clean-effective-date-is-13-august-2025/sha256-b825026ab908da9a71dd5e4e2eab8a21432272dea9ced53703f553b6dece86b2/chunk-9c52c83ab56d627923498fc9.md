AEMO must upon accepting a data change transaction in relation to a delivery point under clause 30, amend the relevant item of AEMO standing data in the AEMO registry to take effect from the start of the gas day specified under clause 27(3)(e) ( 'effective date' ), on the earlier of:
- (a) forthwith, if the effective date is retrospective; or
- (b) on  the  start  of  the gas  day of  the effective  date ,  if  the effective  date is prospective.
{Note:  This clause 49 does not apply to multiple data change transactions that are dealt with as a bulk transaction under clause 31(2).}
