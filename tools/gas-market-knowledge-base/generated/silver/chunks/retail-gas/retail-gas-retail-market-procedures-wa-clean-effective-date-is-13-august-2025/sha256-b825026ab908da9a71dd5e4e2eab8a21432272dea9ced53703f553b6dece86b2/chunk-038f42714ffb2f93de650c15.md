Upon receipt of metering data under clause 158 which is not valid, AEMO must immediately :
- (a) reject the metering data ; and
- (b) notify the network operator that lodged the metering data that it has been rejected and provide the reason why the metering data is not valid.
{Note:  A network  operator must  re-send  the metering  data to AEMO to  comply  with  its obligations under clause 158.}
