- (1) If there is more than one physical interconnection between a given sub-network and a pipeline , then for the purposes of this Chapter 5, the several physical points of interconnection are treated as being aggregated into a single (notional) gate point between the pipeline and the sub-network .
- (2) If there is only one physical interconnection between a given sub-network and a pipeline , then  for  the  purposes  of  this Chapter  5,  that  physical  point  of interconnection is treated as the gate point .
