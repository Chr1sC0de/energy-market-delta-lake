To avoid doubt, clause 178 may require a user to procure the injection into the sub-network of a negative amount of gas on a gas day .
{Note:  Any negative injection may be resolved between the user and  its related shipper or swing service providers (as applicable), between the shipper or swing service providers (as applicable) and the pipeline operator or by an arrangement with another user .}
