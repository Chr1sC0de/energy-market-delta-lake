A permanent removal confirmation notice is valid only if:
- (a) the delivery point exists within the AEMO registry ; and
- (b) it  is  lodged by the network operator who has an active GBO identification ; and
- (c) the MIRN status is commissioned or decommissioned ;
- (d) the date on which the notice is received is on or after the date on which the delivery point was permanently removed ; and
- (e) the date the delivery point was permanently removed did not occur more than 425 days before the date the permanent removal confirmation notice was lodged.
