the user's  total  basic-meter  reconciliation  amount for the sub-network for gas day D ;
 D i SBRA =
the user's  delta  summed  basic-meter  reconciliation amount for  the sub-network for historical  gas  day  i calculated under clause 234 for gas day D ; and
