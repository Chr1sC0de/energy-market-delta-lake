- (1) The expedited process is applicable if AEMO considers that :
2. (a) the procedures are urgently necessary:
3. (i) to ensure the proper operation of the retail gas market; or
4. (ii) to ensure an adequate supply of natural gas; or
5. (iii) to ensure an appropriate response to an emergency; or
6. (b) the procedures are non-substantial (i.e. a change to the procedures that is being considered has a non-substantial impact if it has no effect on the operations  of AEMO,  participants,  interested  persons or prescribed persons and  merely  corrects  typographical  errors,  grammatical  errors, cross-referencing errors or other similar trivial defects in the procedures ).
- (2) The expedited process is not applicable if  the  proposed procedures relate  to clause 362A.
- (3) If the expedited process is applicable to a proposed procedure change , AEMO must, within 10 business days after completing an impact and implementation report , publish on its website a consultation notice :
