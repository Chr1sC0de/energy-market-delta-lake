Upon receipt of a transfer objection which is not valid, AEMO must immediately :
- (a) reject the transfer objection ; and
- (b) notify the participant that  lodged  the transfer  objection that  the transfer objection has  been  rejected  and  provide  the  reason  why  the transfer objection is not valid.
