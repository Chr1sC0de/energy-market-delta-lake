For  a new  connection of  a basic-metered  delivery  point under  clause  65,  the network operator must provide the user with at least the following metering data :
- (a) MIRN ; and
- (b) meter type ;
- (c) date of current meter reading ; and
{Note:  This may be the date on which the deemed meter reading is deemed to have occurred by clause 148.}
- (d) current index reading ; and
- (e) pressure correction factor ; and
- (f) next scheduled meter reading date.
