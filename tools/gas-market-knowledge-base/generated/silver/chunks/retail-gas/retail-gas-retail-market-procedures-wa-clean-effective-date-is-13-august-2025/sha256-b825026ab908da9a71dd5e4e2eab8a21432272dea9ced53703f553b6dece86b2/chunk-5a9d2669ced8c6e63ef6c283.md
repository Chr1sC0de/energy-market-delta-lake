- (2) Before accepting a valid disconnection confirmation notice under clause 116(1), if:
2. (a) in  respect  of  the  same delivery  point there  is  an  open reconnection confirmation notice when AEMO receives a valid disconnection confirmation notice and valid metering data as referred to under clause 116(1); and
3. (b) the date of reconnection in the reconnection confirmation notice is the same date as the date of disconnection in the disconnection confirmation notice ,
then, upon receiving valid metering data in accordance with clause 107(3)(e) (as applicable), AEMO must:
- (a) cancel both the open reconnection confirmation notice and the disconnection confirmation notice ; and
- (b) notify the network operator that the reconnection confirmation notice and the disconnection confirmation notice have been cancelled .
