Upon receipt of a bid book for a gate point for a gas day under clause 281(1) in which every bid is valid, then AEMO must forthwith:
- (a) remove from both bid  stacks for  the gate  point for  the gas day each bid currently in the bid stack from the swing service provider ; and
- (b) accept each bid from the bid book into the appropriate bid stack for the gate point for the gas day under clause 285.
