For  each  occasion  on  which  these procedures require  a network  operator to provide AEMO with metering  data ,  it  must  provide  at  least  the  following  (as applicable):
- (a) the MIRN ; and
- (b) date of the previous meter reading ; and
{Note:  For an interval meter , the date of the previous meter reading will be the previous gas day .}
- (c) date of current meter reading ; and
- (d) energy value type ; and
- (e) for a basic-metered delivery point ⎯ the energy value ; and
- (f) for an interval-metered delivery point :
- (i) for each hour in the gas day , the energy value ; and
- (ii) the energy value .
