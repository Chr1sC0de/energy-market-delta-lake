- (1) AEMO may, in order to recover the costs of establishing itself and operating as the market administrator of these procedures , determine the amount of the registration fee , service fee and market share charge in accordance with the following cost recovery principles:
2. (a) in recovery of its costs , the charges imposed by AEMO on users , who are Scheme participants , shall not be such as to create a material barrier to entry for new entrants, small users , or self-contracting users ;
3. (b) fees  and  terms  and  conditions  should  be  simple  to  understand  and implement;
4. (c) to  the  extent  that  is  administratively  feasible,  the  fee  set  should  provide efficient market signals;
5. (d) the costs are to be clearly identifiable;
6. (e) costs may be recovered in such a way as to ensure account is taken of the time value of money;
7. (f) AEMO is to have discretion to charge a lower registration fee and service fee for small users and self-contracting users ;
