- (1) A network operator :
2. (a) may reconnect a delivery point when required to, or if not prevented, by law or a contract other than these procedures ; and
3. (b) must (subject to law ) reconnect a delivery point if required to under clause 119; and
4. (c) must (subject to law ), in response to a deemed request under clause 82(a), if a transfer has been marked as pending by AEMO under clause 100 for a disconnected delivery point , reconnect the delivery point either:
5. (i) on the earliest transfer day nominated in the transfer request for  the delivery point , if the network operator receives notification under clause 100(2)(d)  that  the transfer has  been  marked  as pending at  least  2 business days before earliest transfer day ; or
6. (ii) otherwise, within 2 business days after the network operator receives notification under clause 100(2)(d) that the transfer has been marked as pending .
- (2) A user may at any time lodge a reconnection notice with a network operator .
