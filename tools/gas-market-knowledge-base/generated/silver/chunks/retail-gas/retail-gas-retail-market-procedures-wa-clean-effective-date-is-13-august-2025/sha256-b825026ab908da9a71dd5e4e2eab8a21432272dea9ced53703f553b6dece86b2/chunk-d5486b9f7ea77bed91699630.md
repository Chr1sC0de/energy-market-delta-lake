Upon receipt of a data change notice which is not valid, AEMO must immediately :
- (a) reject the data change notice ; and
- (b) notify the network operator that lodged the data change notice that the data change  notice has  been  rejected and  provide  the  reason  why  the data change notice is not valid.
{Note: A network operator wishing to reinitiate a data change notice that has been rejected must lodge a new data change notice .}
