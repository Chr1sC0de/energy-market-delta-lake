- (a) AEMO will  perform  all  tasks  ( 'market  tasks' )  specified  for  it  under  the procedures in calculating and allocating swing service and issuing a contract note , including:
- (i) receiving bids and assessing their validity;
- (ii) administering the bid stack ;
- (iii) calculating marginal clearing prices and swing service costs; and
- (iv) allocating swing service between users and swing service providers .
- (b) Having performed the market tasks for a gas day , AEMO will in accordance with the procedures give the SSP and a user a contract note .
