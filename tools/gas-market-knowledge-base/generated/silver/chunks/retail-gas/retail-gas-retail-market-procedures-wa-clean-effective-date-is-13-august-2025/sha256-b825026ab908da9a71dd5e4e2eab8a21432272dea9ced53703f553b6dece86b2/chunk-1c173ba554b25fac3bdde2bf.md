Upon receipt of a bid book under clause 281(1) which contains a bid which is not valid, AEMO must immediately :
- (a) reject the bid book ; and
- (b) notify the person that lodged the bid book that the bid book has been rejected and provide the reason why the relevant bid or bids are not valid.
