- (1) Upon installing a meter at a new connection , a network operator must, if it has not done so already:
2. (a) assign a MIRN to the delivery point within the range and format as allocated to the network operator by AEMO under clause 63; and
3. (b) determine a MIRN checksum for the MIRN in accordance with the standard algorithm as set out in Appendix 3.
- (2) A network operator :
5. (a) must not assign the same MIRN to more than one delivery point ; and
6. (b) must not re-use a MIRN that  related  to  a  previously deregistered delivery point .
