MCP(ANUSA)
has the meaning given in clause 288.
market responsive flow control pipeline
means a pipeline for which it is intended that the injections of gas on a day follow a pipeline profile forecast provided by a third party.
market share
with respect to a user , who is a Scheme participant , at a particular time, means the market share of the user determined on the basis of the percentage of commissioned and decommissioned delivery points for which the user is recorded in the AEMO registry as the current user in Western Australia as compared to the total number of commissioned and decommissioned delivery points recorded in the AEMO registry for Western Australia on the last day of each month.
market share charge
means a variable fee set by AEMO , having regard to the principles set out in clause 362A(1) and being based upon market share , imposed upon each user , who is a Scheme participant .
MCP(TSS(BS))
has the meaning given in clause 287(1).
meter
means a device used to directly measure the mass or volume of gas passing through it and includes the
AEMO
associated equipment attached to the device to filter, control or regulate the flow of gas.
