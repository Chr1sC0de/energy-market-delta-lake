- (1) AEMO must  operate  the AEMO information  system and  date  and  time  stamp transactions under these procedures , including the process time , on the basis of market standard time, which is Greenwich Mean Time plus 10 hours.
- (2) A reference in these procedures :
3. (a) as to 'gas day':
4. (i) a reference in these procedures to a day or date is a reference to the gas day commencing on the day or date referred to, and ending on the following day or date; and
5. (ii) references to months, quarters and years are to be given a corresponding meaning; and
6. (iii) in reckoning periods of months, quarters and years, the 8 hour offset between months, quarters and years reckoned under clause 6A(2)(a)(ii) and calendar months, quarters and years, is to be disregarded; and
7. (b) to '0000 hours' on a day is to midnight at the start of the day; and
8. (c) to '2400 hours' on a day is to midnight at the end of the day; and
