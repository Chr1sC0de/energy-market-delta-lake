ATCO Gas Australia uses this estimation methodology where the Type 1 or Type 2A Estimation Methodologies could not otherwise be used (e.g. for a newly commission supply point).  In this case an Average Daily Consumption figure for the supply point is used to derive an estimate.  The Average Daily Consumption figure is an energy value (in MJ) stored against the individual supply point.  It is updated on an ongoing basis whenever a verified meter read is received.  For new sites, the Average Daily Consumption is defaulted, depending upon the meter type:
- For an AL6 meter:
30 MJ/d
- For an AL12 meter:
250 MJ/d
- Other Basic Meters:
Based on a nominal annual usage for that particular consumer
Where  consumption  is to be estimated, multiply the Average  Daily Consumption by the number of days in the Estimation period.
