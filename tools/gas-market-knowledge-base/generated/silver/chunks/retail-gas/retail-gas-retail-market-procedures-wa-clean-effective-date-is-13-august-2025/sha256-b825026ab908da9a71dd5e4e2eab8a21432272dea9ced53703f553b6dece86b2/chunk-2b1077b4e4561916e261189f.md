- (1) A swing service provider who is party to a SSPUD may lodge a bid book with AEMO for a gate point for a gas day .
- (2) A bid book must specify the gas day to which it applies, and may be lodged with AEMO no earlier than one year before, and no later than 30 minutes before, the start of the gas day .
{Note:  A swing service provider who wishes to lodge a 'standing bid book ' for, say, a year in advance can achieve this result by lodging 365 separate daily bid books in advance.}
