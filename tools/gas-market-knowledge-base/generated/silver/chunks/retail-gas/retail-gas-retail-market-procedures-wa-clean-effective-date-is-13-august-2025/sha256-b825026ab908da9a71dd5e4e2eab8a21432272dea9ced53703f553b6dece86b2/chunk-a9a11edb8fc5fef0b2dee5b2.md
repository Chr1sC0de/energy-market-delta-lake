- (1) On receipt of a proposed procedure change from a proponent , AEMO must undertake an initial assessment of the proposed procedure change to confirm that:
2. (a) it complies with the requirements of clause 379 (2) and (3) ; and
3. (b) it relates to a matter about which the procedures may be made under the Energy Coordination Act 1994 (WA); and
4. (c) there is no other reason for AEMO to reject the proposed procedure change.
- (2) If a proposed procedure change complies with the requirements of clause 379 (2) and (3) and has not been rejected by AEMO under clause 382 (3), or if AEMO itself formulates a proposed procedure change , AEMO must:
6. (a) consider whether, in accordance with clause 385, the time limit for the preparation of an impact and implementation report ought to be extended; and
7. (b) publish the proposed procedure change on AEMO' s website.
