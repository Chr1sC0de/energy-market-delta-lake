- (1) The compliance panel has the power to:
2. (a) request  a participant,  pipeline  operator,  prescribed  person or AEMO to provide information relevant to a matter being considered by the compliance panel , and the participant , pipeline operator, prescribed person or AEMO (as the case may be) must comply with the request to the extent permitted by law ; and
3. (b) make determinations upon matters referred under clause 329(1)(a), clause 329(1)(b), 329(1)(d) or clause 331; and
4. (c) make orders in accordance with clause 343; and
5. (d) in order to assist it in fulfilling its functions under these procedures :
6. (i) appoint  a  person  performing  the  functions  of AEMO ,  any  officer  or employee of that person or such other person as the compliance panel thinks  fit,  to  act  as  agent  of  the compliance  panel in  carrying  out investigations or other functions; or
7. (ii) employ,  or  otherwise  seek  advice  or  assistance  from  an  external investigator, auditor, accountant, lawyer or other expert, or such other person as the compliance panel thinks fit,
