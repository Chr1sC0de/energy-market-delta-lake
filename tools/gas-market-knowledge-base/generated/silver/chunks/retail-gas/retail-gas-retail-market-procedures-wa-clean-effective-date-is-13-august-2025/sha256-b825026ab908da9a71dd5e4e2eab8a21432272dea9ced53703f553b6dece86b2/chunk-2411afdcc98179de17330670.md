- (1) A network operator must calculate an actual value if:
2. (a) the network operator has obtained meter reading data for the delivery point since the previous meter reading of the delivery point ; and
3. (b) the network operator is able to verify the meter reading data under clause 153; and
4. (c) the network  operator does  not  otherwise  suspect  an  error  in  the meter reading data , the heating value or other associated data.
- (2) An 'actual value' is a value for the total energy quantity of gas delivered at a delivery point (in megajoules) during the metering period , which is calculated by the network operator using meter reading data actually obtained from the meter or meters at the delivery point .
