If:
- (a) AEMO receives a valid transfer request ; and
- (b) the requested transfer is a move in ,
then AEMO must:
- (c) forthwith mark the move in as pending ; and
- (d) immediately notify the incoming  user ,  the current  user and  the network operator that the move in is pending .
