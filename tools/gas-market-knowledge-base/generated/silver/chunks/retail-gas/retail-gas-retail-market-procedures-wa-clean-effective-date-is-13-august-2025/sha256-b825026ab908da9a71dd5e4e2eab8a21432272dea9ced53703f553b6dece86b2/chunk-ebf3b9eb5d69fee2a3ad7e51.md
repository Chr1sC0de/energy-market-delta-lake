- (1) Subject to clause 116(2), upon receipt of both a valid disconnection confirmation notice lodged under clause 107(3)(d) and valid metering data in accordance with clause 107(3)(e) (as applicable), AEMO must:
2. (a) forthwith accept the disconnection confirmation notice ; and
3. (b) promptly  notify the network  operator that  the disconnection  confirmation notice has been accepted.
{Note:    Upon  accepting  a  valid disconnection  confirmation  notice under  clause  116(1)  and receiving valid metering data in accordance with clause 107(3)(e)  (as applicable), AEMO must update the AEMO registry under clause 51(b) by changing the MIRN status to decommissioned and provide the user and network operator with the AEMO standing data for the delivery point under clause 53.}
