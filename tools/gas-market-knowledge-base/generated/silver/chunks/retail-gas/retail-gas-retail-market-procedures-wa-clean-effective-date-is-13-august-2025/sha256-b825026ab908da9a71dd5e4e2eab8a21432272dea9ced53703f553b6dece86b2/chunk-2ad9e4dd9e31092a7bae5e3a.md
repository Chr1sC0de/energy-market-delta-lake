- (a) For the purposes of this deed the procedures which apply are the procedures as amended from time to time (including after the date of this deed) under:
- (i) the procedures (as amended from time to time); and
- (ii) the WA Gas Retail Market Agreement (as amended from time to time); and
- (iii) applicable laws .
- (b) Whenever  the procedures are  amended  in  a  manner  which  materially impacts upon the SSP 's rights or obligations under this deed, the users must provide the SSP with  a  copy of  the amended procedures ,  but  a  failure  to comply with this clause 3.3(b) does not affect the operation of clause 3.3(a).
