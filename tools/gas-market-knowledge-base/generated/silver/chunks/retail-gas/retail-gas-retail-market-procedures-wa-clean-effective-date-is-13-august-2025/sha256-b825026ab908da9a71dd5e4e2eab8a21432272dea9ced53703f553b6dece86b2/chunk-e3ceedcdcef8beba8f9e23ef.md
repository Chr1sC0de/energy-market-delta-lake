For each gate point for each gas day , AEMO must calculate the total amount of swing service to be procured through the applicable bid stack as follows: TSS(BS) = TANUSA + TAUSA where: TSS(BS) = the  total  amount  of swing  service to  be  procured through the applicable bid stack for the gate point for the gas day ; TANUSA = the total of all users' adjusted non-user-specific amounts of swing service for the gate point for the gas day calculated under clause 273; and TAUSA = the total of all users' adjusted user-specific amounts of swing  service for  the gate  point for  the gas  day calculated under clause 275.
