- (1) Within  40 business  days after  notification  of  a  determination  by AEMO under clause  139(3)(b)  or  clause  329(1)(b)(i),  a participant,  pipeline  operator,  or prescribed person may appeal AEMO's determination to the compliance panel by giving notice to AEMO .
{Note:  The compliance panel may make an order that the participant must pay the compliance panel's costs under clause 343(1)(d) or other parties' costs under clause 343(1)(e) as part of any determination made by the compliance panel .}
{While pipeline operators and prescribed persons are not subject to decisions by AEMO, they may still appeal an AEMO decision to the compliance panel on matter of non-compliance by a participant.}
- (2) Within 5 business days after notification under clause 331(1), AEMO must refer the matter to the compliance panel .
