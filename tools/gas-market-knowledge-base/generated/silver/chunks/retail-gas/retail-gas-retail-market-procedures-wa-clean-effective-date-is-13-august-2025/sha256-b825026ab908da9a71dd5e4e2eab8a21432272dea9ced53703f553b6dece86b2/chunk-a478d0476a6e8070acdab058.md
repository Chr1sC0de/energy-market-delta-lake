- (1) A notice sent by email is neither given nor received under these procedures until the  person  sending  the  email  ( 'sender' )  receives  an automated  response message for  the  email  from  the  person  to  whom  the  email  was  addressed ( 'addressee' ).
- (3) It is the sender's responsibility for each attempted email to:
3. (a) verify that it receives an automated response message ; and
4. (b) if  it  does  not  receive an automated response message ,  arrange either for retransmission  of  the  email, or  for  communication  of  the  information contained in the email by an alternative medium.
