A user must  procure  from  each  of  its related  shippers for  each gate  point ,  a 'standing nomination' to the pipeline operator which, for the purposes of these procedures ,  is  the user's  related  shipper's default nomination in  circumstances where  the user's  related  shipper fails to nominate in  accordance  with  its transmission contract .
