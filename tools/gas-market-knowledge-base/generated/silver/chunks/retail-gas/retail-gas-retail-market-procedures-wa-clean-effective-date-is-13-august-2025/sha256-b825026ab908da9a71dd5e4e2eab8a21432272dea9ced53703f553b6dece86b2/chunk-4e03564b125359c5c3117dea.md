- (1) In this clause 377B:
2. (a) 'active in the market' :
3. (i) for a user for a sub-network - means that a user is the current user for one or more delivery points in the sub-network ; and
4. (ii) for a shipper - means that the shipper is listed in the shipper register ;
5. (iii) for a swing service provider - means that the swing service provider is a party to a SSPUD .
6. (b) 'exit the market' means cease compliance with these procedures .
- (2) A user may not exit the market in respect of a sub-network :
8. (a) while it is active in the market in the sub-network; and
