- (1) Within 40 business days of formulating, or receiving from some other proponent , a proposed procedure change for the making of procedures , AEMO must prepare an impact and implementation report.
- (2) The impact and implementation report must include the following in relation to the proposed procedure change :
3. (a) the proponent and the date of the submission;
4. (b) the basis and rationale for the proposed procedures ;
5. (c) the  details  of  the  change  to  the  existing procedures including  a  draft identifying the amendments with marked changes;
