- (1) A network operator must provide, install, operate and maintain a meter at each delivery point within its GDS in accordance with all applicable laws .
- (2) Upon installation of a meter at a delivery point under clause 134(1), the network operator must provide the user with the meter standing data and MIRN standing data for the delivery point before providing the metering data under clause 158.
