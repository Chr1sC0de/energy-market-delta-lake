- (1) For each sub-network for each gas day , within 3.5 hours after the end of the gas day , the network operator must advise AEMO of its estimate of UAFG (which may later be revised under clause 223(2)) ( 'EUAFG' ), the name of each user who is a supplier of UAFG for the sub-network and the quantity of the UAFG estimated to be supplied by each supplier.
- (2) The amount of UAFG supplied on a gas day by a user which was notified under clause 229(1) is the user's UAFG ( 'UUAFG' ) for the gas day .
- (3) The network operator's estimate of UAFG under clause 229(1) must:
4. (a) take into account historical levels of UAFG ; and
5. (b) be a number that results in the net system load calculated by AEMO under clause 223(1) being zero or a positive number.
