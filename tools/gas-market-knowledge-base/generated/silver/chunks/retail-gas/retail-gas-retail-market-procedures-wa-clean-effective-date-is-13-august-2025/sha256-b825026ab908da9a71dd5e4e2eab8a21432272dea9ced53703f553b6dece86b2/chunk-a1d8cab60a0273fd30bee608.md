A user must ensure that for each sub-network for each gas day it procures:
- (a) the repayment into the sub-network of the user's swing service repayment quantities for the sub-network for the gas day ; and
- (b) the injection into the sub-network of an amount of gas equal to its good faith estimate as a reasonable and prudent person of  its  likely user's  required withdrawals for the sub-network for the gas day .
{Note:  The user's required withdrawals is  defined in clause 2 as meaning the sum of UIW, UEBW, UUAFG and URAA for a user for a gas day less any part of the URAA that relates to a gate point adjustment amount .}
