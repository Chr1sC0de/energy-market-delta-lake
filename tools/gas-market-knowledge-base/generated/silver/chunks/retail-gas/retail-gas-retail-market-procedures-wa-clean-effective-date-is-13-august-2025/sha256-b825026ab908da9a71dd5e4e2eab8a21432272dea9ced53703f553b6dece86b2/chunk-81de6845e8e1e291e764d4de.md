| Doc Ref:  | 13 August 2025
MIRN database, 1 = means a database maintained by each network operator under clause 58 containing the MIRN standing data and information regarding each delivery point that is located in the network operator's GDS .. MIRN discovery request, 1 = means a notice under clause 74(2) from a user to a network operator , requesting the network operator to provide the MIRN standing data for a delivery point .. MIRN standing data, 1 = in relation to a delivery point, means the information set out in clauses 58(a) to 58(g) for the delivery point .. MIRN status, 1 = means commissioned , decommissioned or deregistered (as applicable).
Commissioned, Meter status = commissioned and not disconnected or permanently removed (including after the delivery point has been reconnected). Decommissioned, Meter status = disconnected (temporary). Deregistered, Meter status = permanently removed
