Instigated by internal monitoring at AEMO a notice will be generated towards Organisations assigned to a Role on the request. The notice will be generated if the Change Request:
- has passed the end of its Objection Period, and has no active objections (an active objection is one that has not been withdrawn), or
- is related to a prospective move-in transfer.
