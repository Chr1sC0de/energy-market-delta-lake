Trigger, 261 NON-PROVISION OF ENERGY DATA = An internal process at AEMO will trigger this transaction. The process at regular intervals will check for presence of actual energy reads from every 2 nd tier meter associated with the Distributor.. Pre-conditions, 261 NON-PROVISION OF ENERGY DATA = Meter data is missing.. Post-conditions, 261 NON-PROVISION OF ENERGY DATA = The Distributor is notified of missing energy data.. Transaction acknowledgment specific event codes, 261 NON-PROVISION OF ENERGY DATA = No errors are expected in response to this transaction. If an error is received the sending application should invoke its default error escalation procedures.
The Missing Data Notification is realised with MeterDataMissingNotification  aseXML transaction. The transaction will carry an embedded CSV file with a list of meters that missing data.
