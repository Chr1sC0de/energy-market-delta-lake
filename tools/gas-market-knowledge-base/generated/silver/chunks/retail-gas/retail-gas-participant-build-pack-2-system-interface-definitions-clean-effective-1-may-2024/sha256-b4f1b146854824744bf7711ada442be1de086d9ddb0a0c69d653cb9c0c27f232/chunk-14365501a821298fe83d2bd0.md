Trigger, 94 AEMO METER FIX NOTIFICATION = Distributor receives details from the contract fitter and the Supply Point is in the 2 nd tier.. Pre-conditions, 94 AEMO METER FIX NOTIFICATION = None.. Post-conditions, 94 AEMO METER FIX NOTIFICATION = AEMO received meter fix notification.. Transaction acknowledgment specific event codes, 94 AEMO METER FIX NOTIFICATION = 3404, 3408, 3409, 3410, 3411, 3413, 3415, 3416
This transaction reports on a meter fix or installation. It is implemented with GasMeterNotification/MeterFix aseXML transaction. For DTS supply points, this transaction is only sent to AEMO when a site is a new second tier site and the meter is fixed or fixed and plugged. This transaction is used by AEMO to establish a record in its Meter Register and to assign the FRO for that supply point.
