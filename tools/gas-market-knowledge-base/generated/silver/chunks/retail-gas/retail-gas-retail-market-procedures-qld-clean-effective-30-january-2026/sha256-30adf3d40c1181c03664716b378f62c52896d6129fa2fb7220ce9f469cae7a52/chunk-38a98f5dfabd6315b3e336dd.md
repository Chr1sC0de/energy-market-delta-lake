- (a) Any person who has received a transfer request notification in relation to a transfer request pursuant to clause 4.2 may deliver to AEMO , at any time before that User is registered by AEMO in the AEMO meter register as the FRO for the distribution supply point to which that transfer request relates, a notice describing a potential problem in respect of that transfer request .
- (b) A notice delivered by a person pursuant to clause 4.4.1(a) does not constitute an objection notice for the purposes of clause 4.3.1.
