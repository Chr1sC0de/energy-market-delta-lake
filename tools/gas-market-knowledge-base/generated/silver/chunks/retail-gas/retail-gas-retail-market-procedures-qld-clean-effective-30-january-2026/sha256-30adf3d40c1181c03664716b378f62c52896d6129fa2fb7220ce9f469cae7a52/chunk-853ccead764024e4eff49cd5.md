For each MIRN that the designated RoLR has become the FRO , the Distributor must:
- (a) calculate an estimated meter reading for the RoLR transfer date and provide it to AEMO, the designated RoLR and the failed Retailer; and
- (b) calculate the energy data information for the RoLR transfer date as described in clause 2.6.2(a) and provide to AEMO and the failed Retailer; and
- (c) provide the designated RoLR the current information set out in clause 3.1.1(a) to 3.1.1(m);
- (d) and provide the information in accordance with the Gas Interface Protocol as soon as practicable but no later than 7 days after the RoLR transfer date .
