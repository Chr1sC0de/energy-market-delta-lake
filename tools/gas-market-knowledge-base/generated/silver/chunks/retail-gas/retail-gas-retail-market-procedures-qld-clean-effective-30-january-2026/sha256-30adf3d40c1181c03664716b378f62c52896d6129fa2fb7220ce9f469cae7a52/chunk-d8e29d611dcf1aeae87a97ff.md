- (a) AEMO must  create,  maintain  and  administer  a metering  database containing information for each metering installation required by AEMO for the purposes of balancing or the calculation of STTM distribution system allocations (as the case may be). 3
- (b) AEMO must,  on  or  before  the  start  of  each gas  day ,  publish  on  the market information  bulletin  board for  affected Participants only,  details  of  every MIRN required  by AEMO for  the  purposes  of balancing or  the  calculation  of STTM distribution system allocations (as the case may be) on that gas day .
- (c) AEMO may appoint an agent from time to time to create, maintain or administer the metering database .
- (d) AEMO must use its reasonable endeavours to procure that the metering database is accessible by all affected Participants at all reasonable times and:
- (i) in the case of data 16 months old or less, within four hours of receiving a written request from an affected Participant ; and
- (ii) in the case of data more than 16 months old, within two business days of receiving a written request from an affected Participant .
