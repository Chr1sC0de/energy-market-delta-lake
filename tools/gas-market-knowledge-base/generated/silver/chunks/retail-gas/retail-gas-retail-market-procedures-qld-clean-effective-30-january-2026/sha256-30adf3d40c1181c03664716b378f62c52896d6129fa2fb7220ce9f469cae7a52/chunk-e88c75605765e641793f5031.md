- 2.4.4 A Type 4 Basic Meter Estimation is based on the following hierarchy:
- (a) Use the same period last year, if:
- (i) the site has more than 12 months of history,
- (ii) there is an actual meter read that applies to the same period last year; and
- (iii) the meter reading is for a period greater than or equal to 26 days:
