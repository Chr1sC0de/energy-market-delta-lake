References in these Procedures to a " withdrawal zone and distribution region " are to be construed as references to that withdrawal zone in so far as it pertains to that distribution region .  Accordingly, where a withdrawal zone extends over more than one distribution region then,  for  the  purposes  of balancing and  the  calculation  of distribution  system allocations ,  the  relevant  provision  is  to  be  applied  separately  to  each  part  of  the withdrawal  zone that  pertains  to  a  different distribution  region .    References  in  these Procedures to  an  " STTM  withdrawal  zone and distribution  region "  or  a  " non-STTM withdrawal zone and distribution region " are to be construed in a corresponding way.
