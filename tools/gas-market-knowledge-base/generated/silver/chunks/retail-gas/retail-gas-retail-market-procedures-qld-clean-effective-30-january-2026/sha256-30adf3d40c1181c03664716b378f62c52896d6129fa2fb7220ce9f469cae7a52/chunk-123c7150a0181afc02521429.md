- (a) Within  25 business  days after  receiving  the  data  referred  to  in clause 11.6.2 (d), AEMO must calculate the annual reconciliation quantity for  each withdrawal  zone and distribution  region in accordance  with  this  clause  based  on  the  data  provided  by Distributors.
- (b) AEMO will apply the following formula to calculate the reconciliation quantity .
Reconciliation quantity = Total Injection -Total consumption
- (c) Within 10 business days after calculating the annual reconciliation quantity under clause 11.6.3 (b) which will include an indicator to signify  whether the  annual reconciliation quantity is  in  surplus  or shortfall, AEMO must  provide  the reconciliation  quantity ,  to  the Distributor, UAFG provider and the host retailer.
