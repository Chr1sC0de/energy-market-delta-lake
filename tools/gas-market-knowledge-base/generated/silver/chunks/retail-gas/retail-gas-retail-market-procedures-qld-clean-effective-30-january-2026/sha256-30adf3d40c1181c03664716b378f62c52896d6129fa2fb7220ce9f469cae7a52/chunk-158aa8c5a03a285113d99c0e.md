- (a) For the purposes of clauses 6.3.1(a)(i) and 6.3.1(a)(ii), AEMO must determine the sum of consumed energy for a gas day for interval meters located in any withdrawal zone and distribution region by:
- (i) summing the consumed energy for that gas day for  those interval meters ; and
- (ii) determining the consumed energy for each such interval meter by using the most  recent meter  readings provided  by  the Distributor to AEMO under clause 5.5.1 or 5.5.1A (as the case may be).
- (b) If any meter reading for an interval meter that AEMO is required to use under clause 6.3.B1(a)(ii) is not available at the time AEMO must determine the sum of the consumed energy under clause 6.3.B1(a)(i), then AEMO must use an estimated meter reading that is determined by it using the methodology set out in clause 2.5 of Attachment 2.
