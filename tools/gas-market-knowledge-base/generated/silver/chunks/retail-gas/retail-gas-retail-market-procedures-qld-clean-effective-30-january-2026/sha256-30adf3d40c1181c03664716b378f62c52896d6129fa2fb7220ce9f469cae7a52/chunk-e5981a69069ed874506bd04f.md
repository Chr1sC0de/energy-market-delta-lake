Provided that clauses 4.1.1 to 4.1.3 and 4.1.5 have been complied with in relation to the transfer request , AEMO must, by midnight on the first business day following the day on which the transfer request was delivered to it, deliver notice of the transfer request (a transfer request notification ) to:
- (a) where  the proposed  transfer  date nominated  in  the transfer  request is  a prospective transfer date , the FRO for the distribution supply point to  which the transfer request relates;
- (b) where  the proposed  transfer  date nominated  in  the transfer  request is  a retrospective  transfer  date ,  the retrospectively  affected  FRO in  relation  to  the transfer request ; and
- (c) the Distributor in whose distribution region that distribution supply point is located.
