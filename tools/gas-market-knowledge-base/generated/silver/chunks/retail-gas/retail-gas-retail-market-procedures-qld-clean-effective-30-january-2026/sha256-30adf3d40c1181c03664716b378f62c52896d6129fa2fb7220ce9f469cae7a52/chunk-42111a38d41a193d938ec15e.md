- (a) Subject  to  clause 6.10.1  (c), AEMO must  determine  each STTM  distribution system allocation for a User for an STTM withdrawal zone and distribution region for a gas day as follows:
DSAd, DR, WDZ = ACd, DR, WDZ
Where:
DSAd, DR, WDZ  = the STTM distribution system allocation for the User for withdrawal zone WDZ and distribution region DR for gas day d; and
ACd, DR, WDZ = the aggregated  consumption for  the User for withdrawal  zone WDZ and distribution region DR for gas day d.
