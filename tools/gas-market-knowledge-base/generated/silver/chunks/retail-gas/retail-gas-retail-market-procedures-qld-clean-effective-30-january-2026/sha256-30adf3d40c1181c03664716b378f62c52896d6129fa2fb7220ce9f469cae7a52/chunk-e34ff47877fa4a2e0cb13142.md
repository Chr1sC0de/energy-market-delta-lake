- (a) For the purposes of clause 6.3.1(a)(ii), AEMO must determine the net energy entering a withdrawal zone and distribution region on a gas day as:
- (i) the sum of all gas entering that withdrawal zone and distribution region at custody transfer points connected to that withdrawal zone and distribution region on that gas day ; less
- (ii) the sum of all gas leaving that withdrawal zone and distribution region at custody transfer points connected to that withdrawal zone and distribution region on that gas day ,
using the appropriate meter readings as determined in accordance with paragraphs (b) to (e).
