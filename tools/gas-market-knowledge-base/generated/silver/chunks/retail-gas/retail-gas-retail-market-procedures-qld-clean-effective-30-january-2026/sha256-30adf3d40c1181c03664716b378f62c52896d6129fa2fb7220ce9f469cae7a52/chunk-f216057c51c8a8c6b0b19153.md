- (a) A User or AEMO , as the case may be, must pay interest on any unpaid moneys due and payable by it under this chapter 7.
- (b) The  rate  of  interest  payable  under  clause  7.12(a)  is  the  default interest  rate calculated as simple interest on a daily basis from the date payment was due, up to and including the date on which payment is made, with interest compounding on monthly rates on the last day of each month whilst the unpaid moneys remain outstanding.
