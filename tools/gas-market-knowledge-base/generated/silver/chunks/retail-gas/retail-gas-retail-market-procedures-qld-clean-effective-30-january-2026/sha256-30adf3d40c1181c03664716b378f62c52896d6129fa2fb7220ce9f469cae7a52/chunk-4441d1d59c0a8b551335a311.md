- (b) A Retailer may  at  any  time  request  a Distributor to  change  a  date  in  a meter reading  schedule where  that  change  pertains  to  a meter that  relates  to  a distribution supply point which is located in the distribution region of that Distributor and in respect of which the Retailer is the FRO .  However, the Distributor is not required to make the requested change.
- (c) A Distributor must notify the Retailer who is the FRO for a distribution supply point in the distribution region of that Distributor of any changes the Distributor proposes to make to a date in a meter reading schedule ,  in respect of the reading of the meter relating to that distribution supply point , as far as practicable at least three months prior to that change being made and the Distributor must consult with that Retailer prior to making that change.
- (d) A Distributor must use its reasonable endeavours to read meters in accordance with  the  applicable meter  reading  schedule or  as  otherwise  agreed  with  the Retailer who  is  the FRO for  the distribution  supply  point to  which  the  relevant meters relate.
