This clause 6.2.1 applies only in respect of non-STTM withdrawal zones .
- (a) Each User must procure that gas can be injected on their behalf into each nonSTTM withdrawal zone and distribution region in which they have Customers or have submitted transfer requests .
- (b) On receipt of a written request from AEMO , each User must within five business days provide details of their arrangements under clause 6.2.1(a) in sufficient detail to  show  that  they  have  existing  arrangements  for  firm  delivery  of gas at  an adequate capacity to meet the Retailer's peak Customer load that can be injected on  their  behalf  into  each non-STTM withdrawal zone and distribution  region in which they have Customers or have submitted transfer requests.
