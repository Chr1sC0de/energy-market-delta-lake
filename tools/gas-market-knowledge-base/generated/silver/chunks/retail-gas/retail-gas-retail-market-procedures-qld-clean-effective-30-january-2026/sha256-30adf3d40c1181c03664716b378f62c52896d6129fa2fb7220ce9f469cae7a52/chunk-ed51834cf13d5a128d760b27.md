- (a) AEMO must allocate to each Distributor a set of numbers which are available for use as MIRNs for distribution supply points which are located in the distribution region of that Distributor .
- (b) AEMO may from time to time allocate a further set of numbers to a Distributor for the purpose set out in clause 3.5.1(a).
- (c) Each  number  allocated  by AEMO pursuant  to  clauses 3.5.1(a)  or  (b)  must  be unique and must not be allocated by AEMO to any other Distributor.
- (d) Each Distributor must assign a number, selected from the numbers allocated to it by AEMO pursuant to clauses 3.5.1(a) and (b), to each distribution supply point which  is  located  in  the distribution  region of  that Distributor. The  assignment referred to in this clause must be effected by the relevant Distributor recording that number in its MIRN database as the MIRN for the relevant distribution supply point .
