Where a notice has been delivered to AEMO by  the Distributor in  whose distribution region that distribution supply point is  located, stating that the proposed transfer date nominated in the transfer request is invalid, then AEMO must:
- (a) cease processing that transfer request ; and
- (b) upon  cessation  of  processing  the transfer  request ,  by  midnight  on  the  next business day , deliver a notice that the transfer request has been terminated, to:
- (i) the User who delivered the transfer request to AEMO ;
- (ii) the retrospectively affected FRO in relation to the transfer request ; and
- (iii) the Distributor in  whose distribution region that distribution supply point is located.
