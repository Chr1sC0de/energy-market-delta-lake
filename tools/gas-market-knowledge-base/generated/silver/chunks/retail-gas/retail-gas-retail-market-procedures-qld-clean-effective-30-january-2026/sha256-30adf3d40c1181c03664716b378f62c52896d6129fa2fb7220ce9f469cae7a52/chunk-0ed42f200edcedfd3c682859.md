- (a) Where  a User wishes  to  dispute  a settlement  amount stated  in  a settlement statement or the supporting data for a billing period , it must do so only in respect of the most recently issued settlement statement for that relevant billing period .
- (b) If a dispute arises between a User and AEMO during the period between the issue of a final statement and the issue of a revised statement in accordance with clause 7.8 concerning either:
- (i) the settlement  amount stated  in  a final  statement provided  under  clause 7.4(a) to be payable by or to it a User ; or
- (ii) the supporting data provided in accordance with clause 7.4(b),
they  must  each  use  reasonable  endeavours  to  resolve  the  dispute  within  nine months after the end of the billing period.
