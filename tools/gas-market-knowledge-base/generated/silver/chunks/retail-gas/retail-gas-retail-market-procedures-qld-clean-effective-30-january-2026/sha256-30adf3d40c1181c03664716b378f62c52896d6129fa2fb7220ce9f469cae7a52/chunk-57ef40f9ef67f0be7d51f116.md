- (a) AEMO must  maintain  a  procedure  under  which  notifications  regarding interval meters are made.
- (b) Each Distributor must notify AEMO within the timeframes set out in the procedure under clause 5.2.3(a) of:
- (i) all new interval meters that are installed;
- (ii) all parameter changes to existing interval meters ;
- (iii) all interval meters that are de-energised or re-energised ; and
- (iv) all interval meters that are downgraded to basic meters 1 .
