- (c) Unless  expressly  provided  otherwise,  the  provisions  of  Chapter 5  of  these Procedures do not apply to basic meters .
- (d) These Procedures apply in respect of distribution supply points in  a distribution region .
- (e) Nothing in these Procedures has the effect of enabling a Customer to transfer to a new Retailer on a date prior to the date on which that Customer is able to do so in accordance with the Gas Supply Act .
