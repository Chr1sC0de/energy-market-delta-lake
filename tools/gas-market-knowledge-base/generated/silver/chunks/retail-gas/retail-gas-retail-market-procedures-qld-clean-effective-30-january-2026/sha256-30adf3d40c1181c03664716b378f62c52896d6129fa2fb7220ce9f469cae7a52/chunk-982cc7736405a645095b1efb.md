- (a) Each Distributor must install an interval meter at each distribution supply point in their distribution region that is a basic meter where:
- (i) a User is  planning  to  lodge  a transfer  request in  respect  of  a distribution supply point that is a basic meter with an energy consumption greater than the basic meter limit and has requested that the meter is upgraded to an interval meter ; or
- (ii) the projected energy consumption at a new distribution supply point would exceed the projected basic meter limit; or
- (iii) the FRO of the distribution supply point has requested the installation of an interval meter at their expense.
- (b) [Deleted]
- (c) Commencing on 1 July 2009, and at least every twelve months thereafter each Distributor must identify distribution supply points in their distribution region that are basic meters and with energy consumption greater than the basic meter limit as at that date, and procure that interval meters are installed in accordance with the timeframe set out in clause 2.9.3.
- (d) Each Distributor must procure that an interval meter is installed at every custody transfer point that is connected to their distribution region , and that they are entitled to read that interval meter .
