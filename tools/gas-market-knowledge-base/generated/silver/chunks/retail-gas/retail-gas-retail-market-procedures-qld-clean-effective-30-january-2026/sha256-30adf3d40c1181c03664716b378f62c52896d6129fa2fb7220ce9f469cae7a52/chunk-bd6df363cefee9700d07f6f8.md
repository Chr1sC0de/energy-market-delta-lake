- (a) Each User must provide their aggregated injections for  a non-STTM withdrawal zone and distribution region for  a gas day as  required  by AEMO for balancing purposes where AEMO has published the required MIRNs under clause 6.2.3(b).
- (b) Each User must use reasonable endeavours to provide their aggregated injections for each withdrawal zone and distribution region referred to in clause 6.4.1(a) to AEMO by:
- (i) 5.00 pm on the 15th settlement business day after the end of each billing period and covering the aggregated injections for all gas days in that billing period, as a final extract ;
- (ii) 5.00 pm on the fifth settlement business day before the end of the month nine months after the end of each billing period and covering the aggregated injections for all gas days in that billing period, as a revision extract ; and
- (iii) as requested by AEMO and covering the aggregated injections for all gas days in the requested billing period, as a revision extract .
