Subject to clause 3.2.2(a), any Retailer or AEMO may deliver a MIRN discovery request in relation to a distribution supply point to a Distributor . A MIRN discovery request must include at least the MIRN or the mandatory components of the discovery address for the distribution supply point to which the MIRN discovery request relates.
