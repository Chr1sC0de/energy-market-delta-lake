- (a) No  later  than  20 business  days prior  to  the UAFG  provider commencing deliveries  of UAFG the  relevant Distributor must  register  with AEMO one interval meter MIRNs for each withdrawal zone and distribution region for which the UAFG provider has been appointed.
- (b) The registration of the UAFG MIRN referred to in clause 11.2 (a) must follow the process for the new interval meter referred to in clause 5.2.3 (b).
