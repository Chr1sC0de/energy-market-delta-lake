Each Distributor must calculate the consumed energy for every interval meter that is a distribution  supply  point in  their distribution  region or  a custody transfer point that  is connected to their distribution region according to the following formula:
Consumed Energy (MJ) = corrected flow (m 3 ) x Heating Value (MJ/m 3 ) .
