Where, pursuant to clauses 4.6.1 or 4.7.1 (as the case may be), AEMO registers a User in the AEMO meter register as the FRO for a distribution supply point , AEMO must, by midnight on the first business day after it registers that User in the AEMO meter register as the FRO for that distribution supply point , deliver notice of that registration, together with the date from which that registration is deemed to take effect ( registration notice ) to:
- (a) that User ;
- (b) where  the proposed  transfer  date nominated  in  the transfer  request is  a prospective transfer date , the person who was the FRO for that distribution supply point immediately  prior  to  the  registration  of  that User as  the FRO for  that distribution supply point ;
- (c) where  the proposed  transfer  date nominated  in  the transfer  request is  a retrospective  transfer  date ,  the retrospectively  affected  FRO in  relation  to  the transfer request ; and
- (d) the Distributor in whose distribution region that distribution supply point is located.
