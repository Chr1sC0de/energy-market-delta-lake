FlowDirection replaces OutlookType PRODC, TRANSC, REVC, WDLC or INJC RECEIPT: A flow of gas into the BB facility, DELIVERY: A flow of gas out of the BB facility CapacityType displays STORAGE or MDQ. STORAGE refers to the holding capacity of the storage facility MDQ refers to the production or flow capacity of the facilities. Outlook Year and Month The old report only displayed outlook month, whereas the new report displayed outlook year and month. Gate Station Standing Capacities (INT 910), From 30/9/2018 = GATE_STATION_NAMEPLATE_RATING. Gate Station Standing Capacities (INT 910), What's new/changed = Modified fields ▪ GateStationID is now ConnectionPointID. Gate Station Standing Capacities (INT 910), Notes/Comments = Date range The EffectiveDate in the new report replaces the 'FromDate' and 'ToDate' in the old report.
