Flow direction and capacity In the old report, the OutlookType field was a combination of capacity type and its associated flow direction. In the new report, flow direction and capacity type are explicitly split into FlowDirection and CapacityType. ▪ FlowDirection is a new field, replacing OutlookType TRANSC and REVC. RECEIPT: A flow of gas into the BB facility, DELIVERY: A flow of gas out of the BB facility ▪ CapacityType displays STORAGE or MDQ. STORAGE refers to the holding capacity of the storage facility MDQ refers to the production or flow capacity of the facilities.
