Demand Location, Definition = A location where the natural gas load is delivered by one or more BB pipelines.. Gas Day, Definition = A period of 24 consecutive hours that commences in accordance with the respective agreements or rules that apply to the facilities and pipelines covered by the BB. The Gas Day Start Hour that applies to each facility or pipeline is published in the Facility report.. Procedures, Definition = The Bulletin Board procedures made under Part 18 of the National Gas Rules.. Supply Location, Definition = A location in which natural gas is produced from one or more facilities and is injected into one or more BB pipelines that transport the gas to other supply location or Demand location.. Rules, Definition = The National Gas Rules.. TJ, Definition = 1 Terajoule, 1,000 Gigajoules, 1,000,000 Joules. A Joule is a unit of energy.
