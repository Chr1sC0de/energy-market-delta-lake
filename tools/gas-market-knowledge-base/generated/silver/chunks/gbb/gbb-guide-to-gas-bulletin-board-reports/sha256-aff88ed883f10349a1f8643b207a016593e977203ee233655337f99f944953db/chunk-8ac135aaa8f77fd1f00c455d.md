Transaction report name, 1 = GASBB_LINEPACK_CAPACITY_ADEQUACY_FULL_LIST / G AS BB_LINEPACK_CAPACITY_ADEQUACY_FUTURE. Purpose, 1 = Provides a report for the Linepack Capacity Adequacy for each Pipeline for the current and next 2 gas days (D to D+2).. Production frequency, 1 = Daily GASBB_LINEPACK_CAPACITY_ADEQUACY_FULL_LIST is updated daily / GASBB_LINEPACK_CAPACITY_ADEQUACY_FUTURE is updated within 30 minutes of a submission. Report period, 1 = GASBB_LINEPACK_CAPACITY_ADEQUACY_FULL_LIST includes historical and future data / GASBB_LINEPACK_CAPACITY_ADEQUACY_FUTURE includes current and the next 2 Gas Days (D to D+2)
