Transaction report name, 1 = GASBB_NOMINATION_AND_FORECAST / GASBB_NOMINATION_AND_FORECAST_NEXT_7. Purpose, 1 = The report shall return Nomination and Forecast data submitted to the market . Nomination and Forecasts data shall be aggregated by BB facility.. Production frequency, 1 = GASBB_NOMINATION_AND_FORECAST is updated daily. GASBB_NOMINATION_AND_FORECAST_NEXT_7 is typically updated within 30 minutes of receiving new data. Report period, 1 = GASBB_NOMINATION_AND_FORECAST report contain historical data as well as nominations for D+0, D+1, D+2, D+3, D+4, D+5, and D+6. GASBB_NOMINATION_AND_FORECAST_NEXT_7 report covers the outlook period of D+0, D+1, D+2, D+3, D+4, D+5, and D+6.
