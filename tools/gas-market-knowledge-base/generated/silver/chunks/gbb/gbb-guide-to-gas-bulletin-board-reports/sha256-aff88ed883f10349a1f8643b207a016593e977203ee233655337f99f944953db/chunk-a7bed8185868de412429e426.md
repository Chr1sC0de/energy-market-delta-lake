AEMO uses the concept of nodes to link facilities and their connection points.
- A node consists of up to four connection points and can have up to two facilities connected to it.
- A facility can have two unidirectional connection points connected to a node, for example, one connection point for gas receipt, and one connection point for gas delivery as shown in the following diagram.
- A facility can have any number of nodes.
