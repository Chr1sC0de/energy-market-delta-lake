This BB Data Submission Guide sets out specific data elements and validation rules for submitting information to AEMO for the Gas Bulletin Board (Part 18 of the National Gas Rules) and the East Coast Gas System (Part 27 of the National Gas Rules). The National Gas Rules, the National Gas Law, the BB Procedures and the East Coast Gas System Procedures prevail over this BB Data Submission Guide to the extent of any inconsistency.
