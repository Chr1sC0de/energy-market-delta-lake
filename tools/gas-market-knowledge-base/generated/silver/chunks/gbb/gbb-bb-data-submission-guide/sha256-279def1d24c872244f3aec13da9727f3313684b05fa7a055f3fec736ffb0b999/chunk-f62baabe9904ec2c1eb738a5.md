Transaction name, 1 = DAILY_STORAGE. Purpose, 1 = Provide on each gas day D+1, the actual quantity of gas held in each BB storage facility and LNG import facility for gas day D and any cushion gas quantity for BB storage facilities .. Submission cut-off time, 1 = 1:00 pm on gas day D.. Rollover, 1 = No Rollover.. Required by, 1 = The BB reporting entity for BB storage and LNG Import facilities.. Exemptions, 1 = No exemptions are given for this submission.
