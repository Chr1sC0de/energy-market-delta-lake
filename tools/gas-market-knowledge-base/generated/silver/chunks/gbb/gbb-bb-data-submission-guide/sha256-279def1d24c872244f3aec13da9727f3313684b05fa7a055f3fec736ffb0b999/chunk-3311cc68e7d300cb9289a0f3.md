Purpose, NOMINATIONS_AND_FORECASTS = For BB pipelines forming part of a Declared Transmission System, provide on each gas day, the aggregated scheduled injections and aggregated scheduled withdrawals at each controllable system point for D to D+2. For all other BB reporting entities , provide on each gas day D-1 the aggregated nominated and forecast injections and aggregated nominated and forecast withdrawals for D to D+6.. Submission frequency, NOMINATIONS_AND_FORECASTS = Daily. Submission cut-off time, NOMINATIONS_AND_FORECASTS = For storage facilities providing nominations, one hour after the start of gas day D. For storage facilities providing forecast information and for other facilities in all cases, 9:00pm on gas day D-1. Rollover, NOMINATIONS_AND_FORECASTS = No rollover.. Required by, NOMINATIONS_AND_FORECASTS = The BB reporting entity for BB pipelines , BB production facilities, BB storage facilities, BB blend processing facilities , BB compression facilities and LNG Import facilities.. Exemptions, NOMINATIONS_AND_FORECASTS = Two facilities connected to a single connection point may both be registered by AEMO.
