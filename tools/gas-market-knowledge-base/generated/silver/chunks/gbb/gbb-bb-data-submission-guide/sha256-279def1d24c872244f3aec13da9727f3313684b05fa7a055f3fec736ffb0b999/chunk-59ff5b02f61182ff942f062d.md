Exemptions, MEDIUM_TERM_CAPACITY_OUTLOOK = No Part 18 exemptions are given for this submission.. Notes, MEDIUM_TERM_CAPACITY_OUTLOOK = Part 18 Medium Term Capacity Outlook (MTCO) is a weekly submission with one or more start and end dates and is typically only provided if there is a change of capacity materially different to nameplate rating. A subsequent submission for all or part of the date range of a previous MTCO will replace the MTCO for that BB facility, therefore each MTCO submission must account for the: • Part 18 MTCO requirements; • Part 27 recall times; • Part 27 extended daily capacity outlooks; and • Part 27 medium term maintenance demand. Where a BB reporting entity submits a Facility Id with record blank values for the remaining fields, this clears previous Medium Term Capacity Outlook submissions where the From Gas Date is on or after the current gas day (D) for the BB facility.
