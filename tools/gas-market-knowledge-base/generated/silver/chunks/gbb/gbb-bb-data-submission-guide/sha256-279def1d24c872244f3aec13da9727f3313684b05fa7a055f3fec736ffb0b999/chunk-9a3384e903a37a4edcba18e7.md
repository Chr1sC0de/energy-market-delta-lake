- Max Gas Date and Min Gas Date must conform to the date format YYYY-MM-DD.
- Max Gas Date and Min Gas Date must be within the specified month and year being reported.
- Submissions must only contain Facility Ids registered to the BB reporting entity .
- Connection Point Ids can only be submitted for BB Pipelines and BB blended distribution systems that transports a gas blend through only a part of the facility and must be registered against the Facility Id.
- Percentage values must be <= 100
- Gas Blend Curtailment Events must be >= 0
- Gas Blend Curtailment Quantity must be >= 0
- Gas Blend Low must be >= 0
- Gas Blend High must be >= 0
- Gas Blend Avg must be >= 0
BB Data Submission Guide
- Month must be between greater than equal to 1 and less than or equal to 12.
- Year must be current year or prior.
- FlowDirection is case sensitive
