Transaction name, 1 = GASBB_BLEND_AND_BLEND_CURTAIL. Purpose, 1 = Provide by fifth gas day in each month, the BB reporting entities gas blend information and gas blend curtailment information for the previous month.. Submission cut-off time, 1 = fifth gas day of each month.. Rollover, 1 = No rollover.. Required by, 1 = The BB reporting entity for BB pipelines and BB blended gas distribution systems that transport gas blend and is subject to or applies a blending limit .. Exemptions, 1 = No exemptions are given for this submission.. Notes, 1 =
