- Nomination Quantity values greater than zero must be submitted in TJs accurate to three decimal places.
- A BB pipeline must provide Nomination Quantities for each connection point.
- Where a BB storage or BB production facility, BB blend processing facility has flows in both directions, an aggregated Nomination Quantity and Flow Direction must be submitted for the facility.
- A BB compression facility must provide Nomination Quantities of gas compressed by the facility
- Where there are zero receipts or zero deliveries at a connection point, a Nomination Quantity of zero must be submitted for the relevant direction.
- Where a connection point is unidirectional a Nomination Quantity for this direction, either as a receipt or delivery must be provided.
- Submitted connection points must be registered against the Facility ID during the connection point registration process.
- For each connection point in a submission, check the connection point's 'Flow Direction' as defined in the reference data.  If the connection point is bi-directional, a submission must contain a Nomination Quantity for receipt and delivery.
- Submissions must only contain BB pipelines, BB production facilities, BB storage facilities, BB blend processing facilities, BB compression facilities or LNG Import facilities for which the BB reporting entity is registered.
