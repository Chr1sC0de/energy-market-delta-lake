Transaction name, 1 = LINEPACK_CAPACITY_ADEQUACY. Purpose, 1 = Provide on any gas day D, the BB pipeline and BB compression facility operator's Linepack Capacity Adequacy (LCA) flag for gas days D to D+2.. Submission cut-off time, 1 = 7:00 pm on gas day D.. Rollover, 1 = Submitted values roll forward in the following manner: • the last 3-day LCA Outlook provided for that BB pipeline or BB compression facility is deemed to be unchanged. • the LCA flag for the subsequent gas day is deemed to be the same as the LCA flag for D+2.
