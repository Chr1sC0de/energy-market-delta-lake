- DevelopmentFacilityID must be a registered DevelopmentFacilityID.
- Submissions must only contain Development Facility ID's for which the submitter is the Reporting Entity.
- CapacityFrom and CapacityTo must be submitted in TJs accurate to three decimal places.
- DevelopmentStage must be one of PROPOSED, COMMITTED, CANCELLED or ENDED.
- DevelopmentStage input is case sensitive.
- CapacityFrom must be greater than or equal to 0.
- CapacityTo must be greater than or equal to 0.
