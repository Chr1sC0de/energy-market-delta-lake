- Actual Quantity values represent physical gas flows and must be submitted in TJs accurate to three decimal places.
- Where there are zero gas flows at a connection point, an Actual Quantity of zero must be submitted with a flow direction corresponding to the standing data flow direction of that connection point.
- A BB pipeline must provide gas flow for each connection point during a gas day.
- Where a BB storage has flows in both directions during a gas day, an aggregated Actual Quantity and Flow Direction must be submitted for the facility.
- A BB compression facility must provide the quantity of gas compressed by the facility during the gas day.
- A BB Production facility must provide the quantity of gas injected into one or more pipelines on the gas day.
