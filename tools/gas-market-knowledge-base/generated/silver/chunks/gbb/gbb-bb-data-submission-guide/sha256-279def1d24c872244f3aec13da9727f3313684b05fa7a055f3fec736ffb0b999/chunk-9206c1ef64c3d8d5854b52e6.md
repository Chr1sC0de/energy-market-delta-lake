Transaction name, 1 = SHORT_TERM_TRANSACTION. Purpose, 1 = Provides information on: Short term gas transactions, excluding those concluded through the gas trading exchange .. Submission cut off time, 1 = The earlier of: • 1 business day after the trade date for the BB short term gas transaction; and • The day prior to the day on which the supply period for the BB short terms gas transaction starts. or where the supply period for a BB short term gas transaction starts on the trade date for the transaction, as soon as reasonably practicable on the trade date .. Rollover, 1 = No Rollover. Required by, 1 = The gas seller of a BB short term gas transaction or reporting agent. Exemptions, 1 = No exemptions are given for this submission.. Notes, 1 = All submission detail available in participants private report in the Markets Portal
