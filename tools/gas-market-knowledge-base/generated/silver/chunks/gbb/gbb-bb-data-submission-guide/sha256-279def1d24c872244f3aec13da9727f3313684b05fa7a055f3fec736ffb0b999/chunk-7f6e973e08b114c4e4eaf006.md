Transaction name, 1 = STORAGE_CAPACITY_TRANSACTION. Purpose, 1 = Provides information on: Storage capacity transactions. Submission cut-off time, 1 = The earlier of: • 1 business day after the trade date for the BB capacity transaction ; and • the day prior to the date on which the service term for the BB capacity transaction starts. or where the service term for a BB capacity transaction starts on the trade date for the transaction, as soon as reasonably practicable on the trade date. Rollover, 1 = No Rollover. Required by, 1 = The capacity seller of a BB capacity transaction or reporting agent. Exemptions, 1 = No exemptions are given for this submission.. Notes, 1 = All submission detail available in participants private report in the Markets Portal
