6.3.2. Linepack/capacity adequacy (LCA)

        Rules 179 and 180 requires a BB reporting entity to provide to AEMO the LCA flag for each of
        its BB pipelines and BB compression facilities.

        (a)     A BB reporting entity must provide to AEMO the LCA flag data by 7.00 pm each gas day
                D-1 for gas days D to D+2, subject to paragraph (e).
