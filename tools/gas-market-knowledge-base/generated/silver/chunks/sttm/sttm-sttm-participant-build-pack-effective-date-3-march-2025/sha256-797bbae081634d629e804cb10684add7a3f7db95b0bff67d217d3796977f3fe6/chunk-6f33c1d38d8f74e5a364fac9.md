Table 1 Transaction description
Ex Ante Bid, Description = means a bid submitted by an STTM Shipper or STTM User for a hub to withdraw quantities of gas from that hub on a gas day at a specified price or prices, as defined by Rule 364.. Ex Ante Offer, Description = means the price for gas for a hub and a gas day as set out in the ex ante market schedule for that hub for that gas day, as defined by Rule 364.. Contingency Bid, Description = means a bid submitted by a Trading Participant in accordance with rule 436 to provide contingency gas by decreasing the quantity of gas supplied to a hub, or increasing the quantity of gas withdrawn from a hub, on a gas day, as defined by Rule 364.. Contingency Offer, Description = means an offer submitted by a Trading Participant in accordance with rule 435 to provide contingency gas by increasing the quantity of gas supplied to a hub, or decreasing the quantity of gas withdrawn from a hub, on a gas day, as defined by Rule 364..
