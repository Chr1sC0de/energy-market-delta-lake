Price-quantity steps must be specified in pairs. That is, if price for a particular step has been specified, then the quantity for the same step must be specified too. The opposite is also true: if the quantity is specified, the corresponding step's price field becomes mandatory. There must be no gap in the steps, i.e. if step n exists in the file, steps 1 to n-1 should also exist in the file.
