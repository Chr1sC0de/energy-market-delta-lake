marginal value means, for a constrained quantity, the incremental change in cost of the solution to the SPA resulting from an incremental change in that constrained quantity.
AEMO
marginal flow direction value means the marginal value of the capability to increase withdrawal from a hub on an STTM facility when that withdrawal is limited by the flow to the hub on that STTM facility .
- market facility means an STTM facility or STTM distribution system. market long offer means an AEMO generated offer used in determining the ex post imbalance price which reflects the extent to which the gross flows to the hub in the ex ante market schedule exceed the gross allocations of gas flow to the hub . market long offer price means the price associated with the market long offer. market long offer quantity means the quantity associated with the market long offer. market short bid means an AEMO generated bid used in determining the ex post imbalance price which reflects the extent to which the gross allocations of gas flow to the hub exceed the gross flows to the hub in the ex ante market schedule . market short bid price means the price associated with the market short bid . market short bid quantity means the quantity associated with the market short bid .
