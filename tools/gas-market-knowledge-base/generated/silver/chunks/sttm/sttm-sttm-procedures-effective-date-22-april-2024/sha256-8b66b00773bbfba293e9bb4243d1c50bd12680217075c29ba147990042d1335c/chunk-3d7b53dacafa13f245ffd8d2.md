(a), 1 = For the purposes of rule 415(2)(a)(v), no other details are required to be determined for a schedule to be taken to be issued.. (b), 1 = For the purposes of rule 415(2)(b)(iv), no other details are required to be made available for a schedule to be taken to be issued.. (c), 1 = For the purposes of rule 415(3), AEMO must make available to each STTM distributor at a hub , other than the Brisbane hub , the following information in relation to a provisional schedule or ex ante market schedule for that hub :. , 1 = (i) the quantity of gas scheduled from ex ante bids for each STTM User ;. , 1 = (ii) the quantity of gas scheduled from price taker bids for each STTM User ; and. , 1 = (iii) the quantity of gas not scheduled from price taker bids for each STTM User .
