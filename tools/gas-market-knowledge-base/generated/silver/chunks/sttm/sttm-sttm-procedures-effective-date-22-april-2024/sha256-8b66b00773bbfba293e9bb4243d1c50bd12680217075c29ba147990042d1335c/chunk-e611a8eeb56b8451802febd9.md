For the purposes of rule 419(2)(c), a MOS step allocation for an STTM pipeline must contain the following additional information:
- (a) the gas day to which the MOS step allocation relates;
- (b) the identifier of the STTM pipeline to which the MOS step allocation relates; and
- (c) the identifier of the MOS stack to which the MOS step allocation relates.
