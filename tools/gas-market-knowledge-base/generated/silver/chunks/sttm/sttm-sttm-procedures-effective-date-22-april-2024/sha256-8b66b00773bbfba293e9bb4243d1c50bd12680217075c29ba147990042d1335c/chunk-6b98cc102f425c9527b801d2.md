Note :  Under the Rules , if a notice is received under paragraph (a) from an STTM distributor or under paragraph (a1) from an STTM pipeline operator in time for AEMO to make a determination of an administered price cap state no later than 6½ hours after the beginning of gas day D-1, then the effect of the trigger will be to cap the prices in the ex ante market schedule as well as the ex post prices.  If the determination is made after that time, the effect of the trigger will be to cap only the ex post prices.
