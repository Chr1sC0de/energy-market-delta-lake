For the purposes of rule 420(3)(c), an allocation notice for a registered facility service must contain the following additional information:
- (a) the gas day to which the allocation notice relates;
- (b) the identifier of the STTM facility to which the allocation notice relates; and
- (c) the identifier of each registered trading right that relates to the registered facility service.
