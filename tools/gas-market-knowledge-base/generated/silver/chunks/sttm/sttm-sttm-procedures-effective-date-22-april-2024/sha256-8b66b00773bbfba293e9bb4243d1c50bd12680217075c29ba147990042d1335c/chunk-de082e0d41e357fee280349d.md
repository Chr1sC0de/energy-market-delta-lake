- (a) The SPA must schedule so that the sum of:
- (i) the quantities scheduled from each price step associated with an ex ante offer ; plus
- (ii) the quantity scheduled from the market long offer;
equals the sum of:
- (iii) the quantities scheduled from price taker bids ; plus
- (iv) the quantities scheduled from each price step associated with an ex ante bid ; plus
- (v) the quantity scheduled from the market short bid.
- (b) In implementing the SPA , AEMO may include additional terms in the equation described in clause 6.5.3(a) to implement the required functionality of the SPA in accordance with clauses 6.5.6 and 6.5.7.
