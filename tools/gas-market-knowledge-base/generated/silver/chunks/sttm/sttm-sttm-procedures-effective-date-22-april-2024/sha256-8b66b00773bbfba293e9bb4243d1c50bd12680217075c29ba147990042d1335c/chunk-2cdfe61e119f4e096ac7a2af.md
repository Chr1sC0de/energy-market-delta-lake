- (h) AEMO must publish the methodology employed to determine the quantities required by rule 397 for each STTM pipeline and, subject to clause (i), must before making changes to that methodology consult with:
- (i) Trading Participants; and
- (ii) any other person AEMO considers would be affected by the proposed changes.
- (i) If the changes to the methodology described in clause (h) will alter the published MOS estimate value by less than 10% compared to the MOS estimate value determined for the 12 MOS periods leading up to the date of publication of an updated methodology, AEMO may, but is not obliged to, consult with (i) Trading Participants; and (ii) any other person AEMO considers would be affected by the proposed changes.
