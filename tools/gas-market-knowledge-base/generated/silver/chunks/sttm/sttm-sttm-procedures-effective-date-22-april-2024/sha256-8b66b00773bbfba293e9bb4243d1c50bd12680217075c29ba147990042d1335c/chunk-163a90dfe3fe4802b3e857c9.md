- (a) For the purposes of rule 435(3)(b), a contingency gas offer must specify:
- (i) the identifier of the Trading Participant submitting the contingency gas offer; and
- (ii) the gas day to which the contingency gas offer relates; and
- (iii) either:
- (A) the identifier of the STTM distribution system; or
- (B) the identifier of the STTM facility and the direction of flow,
to which the contingency gas offer relates; and
- (iv) at least one and up to ten price steps .
