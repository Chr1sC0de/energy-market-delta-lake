10.7.4(a).. MCOC(p,d), Definition = The MOS cash-out charge for Trading Participant p for a hub for gas day d for the restoration of MOS gas provided as overrun MOS . This is determined in clause 10.7.4(d).. MCOP(p,d), Definition = The MOS cash-out payment to Trading Participant p for a hub for gas day d for the restoration of MOS gas provided as overrun MOS . This is determined in clause 10.7.4(c).. MCP(p,d), Definition = The payment to Trading Participant p for the provision of MOS to a hub on gas day d. This payment excludes settlement of gas flowed (or not flowed) or any payments or charges for overrun MOS . This is determined in clause 10.7.3(a).. MINP(d), Definition = The minimum deviation price to be applied in the settlement of gas day d for a hub .
