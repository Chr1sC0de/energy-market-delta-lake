FDCP(d,k), Definition = The pipeline flow direction constraint price for gas day d on market facility k  SP (an STTM facility ). This price is positive in value if the constraint restricts flow and is zero otherwise. This value is determined in accordance with rule 417.. FGO(p,d,ct(k)), Definition = The deemed firm gas offered to be supplied to the hub by Trading Participant p (as an STTM Shipper ) for gas day d on registered trading right ct(k) for firm capacity on market facility k  SP (an STTM facility ). This is determined in clause 10.9.1(b).. FSC(p,d,k,fd), Definition = The change, due to market schedule variations , to be applied to the market schedule quantity for Trading Participant p for gas day d for market facility k for flows in direction fd, where the change is not subject to variation charge s. This value is determined in clause 10.5.1.. GMI, Definition = The gross market income for a hub for a billing period . This is the total amount, excluding variation charge s, received by AEMO for a hub for a billing period .
