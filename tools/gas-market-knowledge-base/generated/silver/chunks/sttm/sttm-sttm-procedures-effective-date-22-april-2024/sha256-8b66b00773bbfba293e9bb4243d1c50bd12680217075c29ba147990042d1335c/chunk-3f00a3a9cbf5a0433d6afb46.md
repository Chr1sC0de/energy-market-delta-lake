Example: Suppose a hub has a scheduled flow of 200 TJ in the ex ante market schedule .  A shipper with 80 TJ is unable to supply all its gas to the hub .  If that shipper's underdelivery quantity is 8 TJ then that shipper alone cannot trigger the state because it has lost only 10% of its flow.  However, if the underdelivery is 20 TJ, even though it has not hit the 50% individual threshold, it has reached the overall hub threshold of 10%, so can trigger the state.
