- (a) The settlement surplus payment to Trading Participant p for the hub for the billing period is:
SSP(p) = MAX(0, DVA(p)) + MAX(0,WDA(p))
- (b) The settlement shortfall charge to Trading Participant p for the hub for the billing period is:
SSC(p) = MAX(0, -1 × DVA(p)) + MAX(0, -1 × WDA(p))
