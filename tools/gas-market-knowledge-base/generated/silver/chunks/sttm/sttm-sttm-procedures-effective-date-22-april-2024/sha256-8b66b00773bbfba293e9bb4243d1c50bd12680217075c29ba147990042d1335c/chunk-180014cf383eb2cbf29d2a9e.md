specific term.. VarC(p,d), Definition = The variation charge for Trading Participant p for gas day d for a hub . This is determined in clause 10.5.5.. VQ(p,d), Definition = The total GJ variation of Trading Participant p for gas day d for a hub . This term is greater than or equal to zero. This is determined in clause 10.5.2.. WDA(p), Definition = The settlement shortfall or surplus amount for a hub for a billing period allocated to Trading Participant p as a result of its allocated withdrawals from the hub over the billing period (whether as an STTM Shipper or as an STTM User ). This amount includes a share of variation charge s applied to Trading Participants over the billing period and any settlement shortfall or surplus not included in the value of DVA(p). This is determined in clause 10.10.4.
