These payments and charges will only arise where there is a non-zero pipeline flow direction constraint price . This is expected to be rare, and will only occur if the scheduled flows to and from the hub on the STTM pipeline are equal.
- (a) The pipeline flow direction constraint payment to Trading Participant p for the ex ante market supply of gas to the hub for gas day d is:
PFDCP(p,d) =  k  SP {FDCP(d,k) ×  ct(k) MQ S (p,d,ct(k))}
- (b) The pipeline flow direction constraint charge to Trading Participant p for the ex ante market withdrawal of gas from the hub for gas day d is:
PFDCC(p,d) =  k  SP {FDCP(d,k) ×  cf(k) MQ S (p,d,cf(k))}
