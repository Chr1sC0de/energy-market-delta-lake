- (i) if available as at 4½ hours after the beginning of a gas day , the last notice for that hub and gas day provided to AEMO in accordance with rule 414(1) or clause 7.1.3B; otherwise
- (ii) the capacity information determined by AEMO in accordance with paragraph (b).
Note: AEMO will determine capacity information by using data provided at 3 hours after the beginning of a gas day that passes validation. If AEMO has received capacity information, but that capacity information has an unconfirmed warning, then AEMO will use updated information provided by 4½ hours after the beginning of a gas day that passes validation or is confirmed by the STTM facility operator . For the avoidance of doubt, the only way a STTM facility operator can obtain an extra 1½ hours to submit their capacity nomination is to have a warning notice that has NOT been confirmed.  If no data has passed validation or been confirmed, AEMO will use the most recently provided data. AEMO will only use a default capacity if the STTM facility operator has not provided any data.
