For the purpose of rule 414(2B), if notified by AEMO , an STTM facility operator must:
- (a) update a notice provided under rule 414(1) by 4½ hours after the beginning of a gas day ; or
- (b) on the gas day before the gas day to which that information relates, confirm a notice provided under rule 414(1) by 4½ hours after the beginning of a gas day .
