- (b) For the purposes of rule 422(3), AEMO must scale a quantity determined under paragraph (a) by multiplying that quantity by a scaling factor calculated as:
- (i) the sum of all STTM facility allocations for flow to the relevant hub on the relevant gas day , less the sum of all STTM facility allocations for flow from that hub on that gas day ;
- (ii) divided by the sum of all STTM distribution system allocations at that hub for that gas day .
- (c) For the purposes of rule 422(4), AEMO must determine an updated STTM distribution system allocation for each gas day in a billing period prior to the date on which AEMO must issue a preliminary statement, final statement or revised statement in accordance with Division 10 of the Rules , or the date on which it intends to issue a revised statement .
