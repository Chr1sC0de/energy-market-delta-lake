The amount payable by an STTM Shipper for the restoration of MOS gas provided at a hub on gas day d-2 is determined in accordance with clause 10.7.4 by multiplying the ex ante market price for gas day d by the quantity of MOS allocated to that STTM Shipper under a MOS decrease offer or as overrun MOS for decreased flow to the hub on gas day d-2.
