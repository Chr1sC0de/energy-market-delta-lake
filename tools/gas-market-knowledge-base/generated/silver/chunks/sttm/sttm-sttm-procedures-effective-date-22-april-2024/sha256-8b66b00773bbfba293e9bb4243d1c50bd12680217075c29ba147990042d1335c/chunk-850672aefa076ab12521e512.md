- (a) For the purposes of rule 408(2), an ex ante bid must specify:
- (i) the identifier of the registered trading right to which the ex ante bid relates; and
- (ii) the gas day to which the ex ante bid relates; and
- (iii) at least one and up to ten price steps .
- (b) The gas day specified under clause 7.1.2(a)(ii) must be within the range of gas days for which the registered trading right is registered .

- (i) a single price, expressed in $/GJ to four decimal places, greater than or equal to MMP and less than or equal to MPC ; and
