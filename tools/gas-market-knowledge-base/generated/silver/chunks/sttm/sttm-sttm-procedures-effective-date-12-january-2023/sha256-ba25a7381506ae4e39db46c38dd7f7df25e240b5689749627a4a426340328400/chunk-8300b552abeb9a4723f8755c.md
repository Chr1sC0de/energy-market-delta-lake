- (a) For the purposes of rule 419(2)(c), an allocation notice for an STTM facility must contain the following additional information:
- (i) the gas day to which the allocation notice relates;
(ii), 1 = the identifier of the STTM facility to which the allocation notice relates; and. (iii), 1 = the identifier of each registered facility service that is provided by means of the relevant STTM facility .. For the purposes of rule 419(4), a billing period allocation statement must be provided to AEMO four business days prior to:, 1 = For the purposes of rule 419(4), a billing period allocation statement must be provided to AEMO four business days prior to:. (i), 1 = the date on which AEMO must issue a preliminary statement , final statement or revised statement in accordance with Division 10 of the Rules ; or. (ii), 1 = any date in respect of which AEMO advises the relevant allocation agent that it intends to issue a revised statement .
