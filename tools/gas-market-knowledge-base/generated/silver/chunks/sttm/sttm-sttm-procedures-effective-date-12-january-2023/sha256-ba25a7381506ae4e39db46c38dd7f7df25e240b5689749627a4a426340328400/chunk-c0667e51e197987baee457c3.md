- (b) If a notice given by a Trading Participant under paragraph 8.2.4 indicates that the operational constraint impacts as available capacity , AEMO may only take a related quantity of gas into account for the purposes of paragraph (a)(ii):
- (i) if the as available capacity relates to an STTM pipeline; and
- (ii) if the status of the relevant STTM pipeline on the National Gas Services Bulletin Board was red or orange for gas day D at the time the notice was given; and
- (iii) up to the limit of the quantity that was either:
- (A) scheduled in the ex ante market schedule for that Trading Participant on gas day D, and for which the relevant STTM pipeline operator had accepted a nomination; or
- (B) not scheduled in the ex ante market schedule for that Trading Participant on gas day D, but for which the relevant STTM pipeline operator had accepted a nomination,
as supported by acceptable evidence in accordance with clause 8.2.5.
