Note : This method of cost recovery first calculates what proportion of the total contingency gas called was due to the net short deviation of market participants.  The corresponding proportion of the total compensation cost is recovered from those participants that had short deviations on the gas day .  Any residual amount could be due to mis-estimation of the amount of CG needed, or CG called in multiple directions, or a market administered scheduling state , and this amount is smeared according to withdrawal quantities.
