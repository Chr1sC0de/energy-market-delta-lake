- (a) By the times specified in accordance with the STTM Interface Protocol , AEMO must make available to Trading Participants and publish as soon as possible after that time:
- (i) the default gas day capacity; and
- (ii) the maximum gas day capacity; and
- (iii) the validation thresholds to be applied under clause 7.1.3A.
- (b) By 10½ hours after the beginning of a gas day for that gas day, AEMO must make available to Trading Participants and publish as soon as possible after that time, the validation thresholds to be applied under clause 7.2.1A.
