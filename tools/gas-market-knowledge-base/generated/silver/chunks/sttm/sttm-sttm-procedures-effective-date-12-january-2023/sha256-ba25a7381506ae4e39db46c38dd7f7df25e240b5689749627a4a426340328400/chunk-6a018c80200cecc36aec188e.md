- (a) If AEMO has delayed the publication of the ex post imbalance price under rule 426(1A) it must determine a provisional ex post imbalance price for the preceding gas day using:
- (i) the inputs used to determine the ex ante market schedule for that hub and gas day in accordance with rule 415(1); and
- (ii) the market scheduled quantities specified in the ex ante market schedule for that hub and gas day; and
- (iii) any available STTM facility allocations; and
- (iv) if an STTM facility allocation is not available for an STTM facility , the quantity determined under clause 7.2.1C(c).
- (b) AEMO must make available the provisional ex post imbalance price to Trading Participants by 5½ hours after the beginning of a gas day , and must publish that provisional ex post imbalance price as soon as practicable after that time.
