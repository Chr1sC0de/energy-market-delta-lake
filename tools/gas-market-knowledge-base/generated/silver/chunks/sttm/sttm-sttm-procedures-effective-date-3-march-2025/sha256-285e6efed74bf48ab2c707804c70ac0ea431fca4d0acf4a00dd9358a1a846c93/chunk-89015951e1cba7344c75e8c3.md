- (d) In the event a compensation charge is not paid to AEMO by a Trading Participant after AEMO has issued a suspension notice to that Trading Participant (under Rule 488), AEMO may recover the compensation charge amount not paid by that Trading Participant from each other Trading Participant in proportion to the share of withdrawals of each Trading Participant on the impacted gas day (s) (excluding the Trading Participant to which AEMO has issued a suspension notice).
- (e) For the avoidance of doubt, pursuant to Rule 135JH(4), AEMO is not required to give effect to a final determination of the independent expert and pay compensation amounts to the claimant until the end of the period determined under Rule 135JK(4) or review of a compensation claim determination is finalised (if required) under Rule 135JK.
