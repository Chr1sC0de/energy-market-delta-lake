Note : The MOS increase cost is only calculated if the net MOS gas requirement at the hub is positive (increase MOS).  The MOS increase cost is the sum of MOS and overrun MOS payments for gas day d, and MOS cash-out payments from gas day d+2 (for MOS provided on gas day d) for all increase MOS allocated on gas day d at the hub, divided by the quantity of all increase MOS allocated on gas day d at the hub.
