10.5.3(a).. RDevN(d), Definition = A revised deviation price for a short deviation quantity at a hub on gas day d. This value is determined in clause 10.6.2 and applied only for the purposes of contingency gas resettlement.. RDevP(d), Definition = A revised deviation price for a long deviation quantity at a hub on gas day d. This value is determined in clause 10.6.2 and applied only for the purposes of contingency gas resettlement.. S (superscript), Definition = Indicates an STTM Shipper specific term.. SCC(p,d), Definition = The capacity charge for Trading Participant p as an STTM Shipper for a hub for gas day d. This is determined in clause 10.9.3(a).. SCP(p,d), Definition = The capacity payment for Trading Participant p as an STTM Shipper for a hub for gas day d. This is determined in clause 10.9.3(b).. SDQ(p,d), Definition = The short deviation quantity for Trading Participant p at a hub on gas day d.
