- (b) the identifier of the STTM facility to which the allocation notice relates; and

- (d) if the STTM facility to which the allocation notice relates is a net metered facility , the quantity of gas reported in the registered facility service allocation must be calculated as the STTM facility's metered injection quantity for the gas day minus the STTM facility's metered withdrawal quantity for the gas day . If the calculation results in:
- (i) a net energy injection then the registered facility service allocation must report the net energy injection .
- (ii) a net energy withdrawal then the registered facility service allocation must report a value of zero. This net energy withdrawal will be reported as per clause 7.2.9(c)(ii).
- (e) AEMO may specify validations, as per rule 368(4) and 368(5), for STTM facility service allocations in the STTM Interface Protocol .
