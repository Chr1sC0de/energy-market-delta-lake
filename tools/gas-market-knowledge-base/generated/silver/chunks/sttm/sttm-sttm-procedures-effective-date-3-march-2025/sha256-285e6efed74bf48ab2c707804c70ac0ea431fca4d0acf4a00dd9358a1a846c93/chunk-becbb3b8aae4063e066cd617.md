- (A) If the blending vessel is part of the STTM injection facility and the custody transfer point is at the point between the blending vessel and the STTM distribution system , then the STTM injection facility may be a net metered facility .
Note: if an STTM injection facility meets the criteria for a net metered facility , AEMO may be required to consult on amendments to these Procedures and the calculation of the STTM distribution system allocation as required by clause 7.2.9.
- (b) Appropriate engineering documentation must be provided as part of the net metered facility application , that shows the facility operation meets the criteria in subclause (a).
- (c) If the physical operation of the STTM injection facility that is classified as a net metered facility is proposed to change, then the STTM facility operator for the STTM injection facility must provide AEMO with updated engineering documentation at least 20 business days before undertaking works to alter the net metered facility .
