Subject to clause 6.5.2, the SPA determines values for the following quantities:
- (a) the quantity scheduled from each price step associated with an ex ante offer , which must be between zero and the incremental price step quantity for that price step ;
- (b) the quantity scheduled from each price step associated with an ex ante bid , which must be between zero and the incremental price step quantity for that price step ;
- (c) the quantity scheduled from each price taker bid , which must be between zero and the incremental price step quantity for that price taker bid ;
- (d) the quantity scheduled from the market long offer , which must be between zero and the market long offer quantity ; and

- (e) the quantity scheduled from the market short bid , which must be between zero and the market short bid quantity .
