- (f) For the purposes of rule 422(5), if AEMO updates the quantity of gas withdrawn by a user or self-contracting user in accordance with the relevant Retail Market Procedures , AEMO must update the STTM distribution system allocation for the relevant registered trading right holder's registered distribution service as soon as practicable.
- (g) In this clause 7.2.9, the terms user, retailer and self contracting user have the same meanings as in Part 15A of the National Gas Rules for the relevant retail gas market .
- (h) AEMO may specify validations, as per rule 368(4) and 368(5), for STTM distribution system allocations in the STTM Interface Protocol .
