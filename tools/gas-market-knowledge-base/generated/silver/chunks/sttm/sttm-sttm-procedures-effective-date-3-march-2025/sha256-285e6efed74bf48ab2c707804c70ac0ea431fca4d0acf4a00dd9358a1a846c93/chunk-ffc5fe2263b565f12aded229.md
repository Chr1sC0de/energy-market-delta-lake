d, Definition = refers to a gas day . Example : There will be an ex ante market price for a hub and an ex post imbalance price for a hub determined for gas day d. On gas day d an ex ante market schedule for a hub will be determined for gas day d+1 and an ex post imbalance price for a hub will be determined for gas day d-1.. d-n (1, 2, 3, etc.), Definition = refers to the gas day that is n or a specified number (1, 2, 3 etc) of gas days before gas day d.. d+n (1, 2, 3 etc.), Definition = refers to the gas day that is n or a specified number (1, 2, 3 etc) gas days after gas day d.. D, Definition = refers to the gas day in respect of which a matter is to be calculated or determined. Example : An ex ante market schedule for gas day D will be determined on gas day d = D- 1 while an ex post imbalance price for gas day D will be determined on gas day d = D+1.. D-n (1, 2, 3, etc.),
