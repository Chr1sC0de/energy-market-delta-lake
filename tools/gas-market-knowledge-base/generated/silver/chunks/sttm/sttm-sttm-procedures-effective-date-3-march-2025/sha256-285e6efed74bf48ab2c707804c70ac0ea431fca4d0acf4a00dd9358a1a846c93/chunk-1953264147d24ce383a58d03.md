(a), 1 = A notice published by AEMO under rule 441(1) regarding a contingency gas trigger event must:. , 1 = (i) specify whether AEMO was notified of the contingency gas trigger event under rule 440(2)(a), or whether AEMO considers that the contingency gas trigger event has occurred as contemplated in rule 441(1)(b); and. (ii), 1 = specify the date and time at which the notification was received or AEMO decided that the contingency gas trigger event had occurred; and. (iii), 1 = identify the relevant contingency gas trigger event; and. (b), 1 = AEMO will chair the CG assessment conference convened in accordance with rule 441(1)(d).. (c), 1 = AEMO must convene an additional CG assessment conference prior to updating a contingency gas requirement in accordance with rule 444(4).
