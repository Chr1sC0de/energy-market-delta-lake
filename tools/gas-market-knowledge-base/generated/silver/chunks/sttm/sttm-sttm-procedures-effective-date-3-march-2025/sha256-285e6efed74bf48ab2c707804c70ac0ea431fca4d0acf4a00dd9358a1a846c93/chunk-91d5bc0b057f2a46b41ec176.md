- (a) The variation quantity of Trading Participant p variations assigned to step f is defined as follows, starting from step f = 1 and then increasing f:
For f=1:
GVarU(p,d,f) = MIN(VQ(p,d), GVarR(f))
For f>1 and f <Maxf:
GVarU(p,d,f) =  MIN(VQ(p,d), GVarR(f)) - f'<f GVarU(p,d,f')
For f= Maxf:
GVarU(p,d,f) =  VQ(p,d) - f'<f GVarU(p,d,f')
Note : That is, the total of variations is assigned to steps, where each step is defined as a fraction of the ex ante market schedule for STTM Users and STTM Shippers withdrawing from the hub .
