NOTE: AEMO expects the blending vessel will be a pipe owned and operated by the distributor and be part of the distribution system, and as a result the custody transfer point (e.g. meter) is not required for the STTM injection facility withdrawals from and injections to the distribution system on a gas day. The STTM injection facility will only be required to appropriately meter the primary gas injection point into the blending vessel.
This approach means changes to market system are not required to account for net energy injection and net energy withdrawal.
