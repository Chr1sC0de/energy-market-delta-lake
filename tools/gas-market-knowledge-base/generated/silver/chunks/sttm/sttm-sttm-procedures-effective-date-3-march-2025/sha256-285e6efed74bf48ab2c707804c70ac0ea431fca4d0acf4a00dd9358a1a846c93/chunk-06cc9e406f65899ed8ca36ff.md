- (i) a single price, expressed in $/GJ to four decimal places, greater than or equal to MMP and less than or equal to MPC ; and
- (ii) the maximum quantity of gas that the STTM Shipper or STTM User is willing to withdraw from the hub at the price specified under subparagraph (i), expressed in whole GJ.
- (d) If the price in a price step (referred to in this paragraph as the higher price step ) is greater than the price in any other price step in the ex ante bid , then the quantity in the higher price step must be lower than the quantity in that other price step .
Note :  The purpose of paragraphs (c) and (d) is to ensure that each price step represents a cumulative quantity of gas , and that prices decrease with increasing price step quantities.
