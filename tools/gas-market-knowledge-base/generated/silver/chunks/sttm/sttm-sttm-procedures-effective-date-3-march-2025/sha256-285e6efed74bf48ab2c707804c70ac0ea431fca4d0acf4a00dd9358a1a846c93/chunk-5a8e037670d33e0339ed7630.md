- (a) The outputs required from the SPA when used to produce provisional schedules or ex ante market schedules are:
- (i) the market schedule quantity, or forecast market schedule quantity, for each registered trading right, being the total of the quantities scheduled from each price step or price taker bid associated with that registered trading right in accordance with clause 6.5.1, rounded in accordance with clause 6.5.9;
