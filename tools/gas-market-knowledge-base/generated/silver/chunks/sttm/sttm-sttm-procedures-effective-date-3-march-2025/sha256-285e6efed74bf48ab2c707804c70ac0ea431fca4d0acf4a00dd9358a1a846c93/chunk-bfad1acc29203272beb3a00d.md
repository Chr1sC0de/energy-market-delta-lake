- (a) The independent expert may reduce (but may not increase) any amount of compensation calculated in accordance with the steps in this clause 10.11 by an amount that it considers to be equitable, taking into account the conduct of the Trading Participant and any other circumstances relating to the relevant offer or the claim.
- (b) The independent expert may, under paragraph (a), reduce an amount of compensation to zero.
