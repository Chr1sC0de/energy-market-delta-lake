- (a) A Trading Participant must have a single registered facility service for the positive net energy injection allocations at a net metered facility and the registered facility service may be suballocated to other Trading Participants as trading rights .
(b), 1 = Trading Participant(s) may make an ex ante injection offer for a trading right for a net metered facility for a gas day .. (c), 1 = A Trading Participant that is a STTM User must be assigned any net metered facility's withdrawal quantity for a gas day.. (d), 1 = Net metered facility withdrawals will be included in this STTM User ' s pricetaker bid or ex ante bids at the hub .
