- (a) For the purposes of rule 378B(1)(a), an STTM injection facility may be classified as a net metered facility if the STTM injection facility meets the following criteria:
- (i) the STTM injection facility is registered as a STTM injection facility in the list of information published by AEMO for the purpose of rule 377(3);
- (ii) the STTM injection facility withdraws gas from a hub , blends it with a primary gas produced at the STTM injection facility and then at the same time reinjects the gas blend into the hub ;
- (iii) a STTM injection facility may store a primary gas for the purpose of injection into a hub , but may not store or consume gas withdrawn from a hub ; and
- (iv) the custody transfer point for the STTM injection facility is not at the point between the STTM injection facility and the blending vessel (being the pipe in which a primary gas and covered gas is blended).
