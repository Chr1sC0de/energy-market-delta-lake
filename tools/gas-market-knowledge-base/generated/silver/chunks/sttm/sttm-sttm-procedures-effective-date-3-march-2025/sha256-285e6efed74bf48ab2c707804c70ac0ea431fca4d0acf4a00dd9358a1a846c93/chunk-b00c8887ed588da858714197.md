- (a) For the purposes of rule 377(2), subject to paragraph (b), AEMO must determine a maximum gas day capacity for an STTM facility by multiplying the default gas day capacity provided or determined for the STTM facility by the value 1.13.
- (b) In determining a maximum gas day capacity , AEMO may take into account any relevant information given to AEMO by the STTM facility operator .
