- Deviation payments and charges (a) The deviation payment for Trading Participant p for a long deviation quantity in withdrawals from the hub (i.e. lower withdrawal than expected) on market facility k on gas day d is: DevPFA(p,d,k) = MAX(0 , DQF(p,d,k)) × PDevPF(p,d,k) (b) The deviation charge for Trading Participant p for a short deviation quantity in withdrawals from the hub (i.e. higher withdrawal than expected) on market facility k on gas day d is: DevNFA(p,d,k) = MAX(0, -1 × DQF(p,d,k)) × PDevNF(p,d,k)
