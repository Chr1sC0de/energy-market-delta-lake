- (a) For the purposes of rule 401(2), AEMO must:
- (i) publish the MOS increase stack and a MOS decrease stack in accordance with rule 401(2)(a); and
- (ii) make available to each relevant STTM pipeline operator the information set out in rule 401(2)(b),
No later than 10 gas days before the start of the relevant MOS period.
