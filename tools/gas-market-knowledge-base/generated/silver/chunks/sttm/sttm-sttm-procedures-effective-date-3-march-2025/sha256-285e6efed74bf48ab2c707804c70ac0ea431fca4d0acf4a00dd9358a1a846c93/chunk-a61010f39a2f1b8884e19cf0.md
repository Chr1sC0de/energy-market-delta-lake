- (a) a person may propose to add, remove or amend a custody transfer point in the custody transfer point register by submitting a proposal ( STTM CTP Proposal ) to AEMO's Support Hub for the attention of Gas Market Operations at least 40 business days prior to the proposed date for addition, removal or amendment of the custody transfer point , or such earlier date as agreed with AEMO.
- (i) For the avoidance of doubt, multiple existing custody transfer points for two or more injection facilities may be changed via the arrangements in this clause for the purpose of an STTM aggregated injection facility.
- (b) a STTM CTP Proposal must include (as applicable):
- (i) the proposed name and facility identifier of the new or amended STTM facility for the custody transfer point ;
- (ii) the name and facility identifier of the STTM facility being decommissioned;
- (iii) the following information for the relevant STTM facility (as applicable):
- (A) proposed effective from date (i.e. proposed commissioning date).
- (B) proposed effective to date (i.e. proposed decommissioning date).
