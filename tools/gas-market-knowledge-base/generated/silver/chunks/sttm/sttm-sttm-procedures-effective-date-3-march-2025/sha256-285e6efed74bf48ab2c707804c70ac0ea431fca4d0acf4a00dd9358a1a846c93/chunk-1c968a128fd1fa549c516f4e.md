- (a) A request for classification as a net metered facility under Part 20 of the Rules must be made in in writing to AEMO's Support Hub for the attention of Gas Market Operations from the STTM facility operator, who must be the STTM facility operator in the information registered by AEMO in accordance with rule 377 for the relevant STTM facility, and must include the following information:
- (i) The name of the facility operator;
- (ii) The maximum daily quantity (GJ/d) that can be injected by the facility;
- (iii) The maximum daily quantity (GJ/d) that can be withdrawn by the facility;
- (iv) The maximum daily net energy injection (GJ/d) that can be injected by the facility;
- (v) The name and address of the facility;
- (vi) Details of the custody transfer point configuration of the intending net metered facility which must have:
- (A) an injection custody transfer point for Trading Participant ex ante offer and allocation to registered facility services and trading rights for net energy injection; and
