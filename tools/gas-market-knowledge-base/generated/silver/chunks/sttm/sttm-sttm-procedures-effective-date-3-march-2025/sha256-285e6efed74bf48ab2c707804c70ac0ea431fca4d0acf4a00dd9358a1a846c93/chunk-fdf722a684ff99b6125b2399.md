- (a) For the purposes of rule 449(3), a Trading Participant must provide reasonable evidence, in accordance with the applicable provisions of this clause 9.5, of:
- (i) the quantity of contingency gas provided by that Trading Participant on a gas day ;
- (ii) the location at which it was provided; and
- (iii) the period of time over which it was provided.
- (b) Evidence must be provided to AEMO no later than 40 business days after the end of the relevant gas day .
