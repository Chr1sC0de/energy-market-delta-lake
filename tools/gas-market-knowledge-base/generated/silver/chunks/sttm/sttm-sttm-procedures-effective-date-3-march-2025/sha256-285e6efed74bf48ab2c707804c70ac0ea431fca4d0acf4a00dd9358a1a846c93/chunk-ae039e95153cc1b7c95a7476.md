```
DQB(p) =  d  BP {  k  SN  [MAX(0,DQF(p,d,k)) × LI(d,k) -  MIN(0,DQF(p,d,k)) × LD(d,k)] +  k  SP  [MAX(0,DQF(p,d,k)) × LI(d,k) -  MIN(0,DQF(p,d,k)) × LD(d,k) + MAX(0,DQT(p,d,k)) × LI(d,k) -  MIN(0,DQT(p,d,k)) × LD(d,k)]}
```
