To view an MSV, search for the relevant MSV and alter, as detailed below:
1.  On the Home page, click Market Schedule Variations .
The screen will display the search options and below that the 30 most recent MSV transactions.
Figure 58 Manage MSVs screen
2.  Under MSV Status , click the relevant MSV status parameter option.
3.  In the From Gas Date and To Gas Date boxes, type the gas dates.
4.  Under MSV Role :
- Select the relevant organisation from the Submitter/Counter Party drop-down menu
- Click the organisation's role.
5.  In the Facility box, select the facility relevant to the MSV.
6.  Click Search .
7.  Under MSV Details , locate the MSV.
8.  Click the View Market Schedule icon to the right of the MSV transaction.
