custody transfer point (CTP), Definition = The physical location at which gas passes from a pipeline or an injection facility (including a storage facility or production facility) into a distribution system.. deemed STTM distribution system, Definition = A distribution system from which a transmission-connected STTM user withdraws gas from the hub.. deemed STTM distribution system allocation, Definition = The quantity of gas allocated by a pipeline operator to a transmission-connected STTM user on a gas day.. deviation, Definition = The difference in the quantity of gas that a trading participant is scheduled to flow - as modified by market schedule variations, MOS, and contingency gas - and the actual quantity of gas that flowed. Also see long deviation and short deviation .. deviation charge, Definition = A charge paid by a trading participant for a short deviation.. deviation payment, Definition = A payment received by a trading participant for a long deviation.. deviation quantity, Definition = The quantity in GJ of a trading participant's deviation.. distribution contract, Definition = An agreement between an STTM distributor and an STTM user that allows the STTM user to transport gas across an STTM distribution system.. distribution network (obs.), Definition = See STTM
