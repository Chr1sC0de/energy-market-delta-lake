1.  On the Home page, click Contingency Bids and Offers .
2.  Click Contingency Gas Bids .
3.  On the Facility drop-down menu, select the facility relevant to the bid.
4.  On the Contingency Gas Historical Bids drop-down menu, select the bid.
Note : If there are no historic bids, then a message displays advising of no previous bids.
5.  Click Retrieve a Contingency Bid .
6.  In the Start Date and End Date boxes, type the new start and end dates.
7.  For each price-quantity step:
- In the Price $ box, type the offer prices in increasing order.
- In the GJ box, type the GJ amounts in increasing cumulative order.
8. Click Confirm Contingency Gas Bid
9. Click Confirm Offer on the confirmation screen .
A message confirms the successful offer.
