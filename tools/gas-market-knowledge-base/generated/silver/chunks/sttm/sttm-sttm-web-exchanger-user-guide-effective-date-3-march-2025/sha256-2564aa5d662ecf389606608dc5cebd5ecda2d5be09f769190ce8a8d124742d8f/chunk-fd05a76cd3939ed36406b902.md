The key steps in establishing a Registered Facility Service are:
1.  The Contract Holder (i.e Shipper or User) registers the service in the market systems;
2.  The Contract Issuer (Pipeline or Distributor) confirms the service in the market systems; and
3.  The Contract Holder then creates an initial trading right associated with the Registered Service.
Note : The Registered Service is not activated until the initial Trading Right is created for that Registered Service.
