1.  On the Home page, click Price Taker Bids . The Price Taker Bids Selection screen displays:
2.  In the Gas Date box, type the date that applies to the bid.
3.  On the TRN Details drop-down menu, select the trading right that applies to the bid.
4.  Click Retrieve .
5.  In the Quantity (GJ Value) box, type the new quantity .
6.  Click Submit to submit the bid.
