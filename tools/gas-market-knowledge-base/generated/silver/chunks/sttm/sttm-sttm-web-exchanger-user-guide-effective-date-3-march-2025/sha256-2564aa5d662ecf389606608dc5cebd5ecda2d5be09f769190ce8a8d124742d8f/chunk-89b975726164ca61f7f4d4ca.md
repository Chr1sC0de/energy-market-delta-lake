2.  Click the Submitter Type drop-down menu and select your submitter option type.
3.  In the Gas Date box, type the date of the MSV.
4.  In the Counter Party Type drop-down menu, select the counter party's option type.
5.  In the Quantity (GJ) field, type the variation amount in GJ.
6.  In the Submitter Facility drop-down menu, select the faculty attached to the market schedule.
7.  In the Counter Party drop-down menu, select the counter party organisation.
8.  In the Counter Party Facility drop-down menu, select the counter party's facility.
Note : The Counter Party Facility is only required when a submitter is a shipper to the hub and the counter party is shipper from the hub. This must be left blank for all other transactions.
9.  Select either the Increase or Decrease option to define the impact of the market schedule variation.
10.  Click Submit .
11.  On the Confirmation screen, click Confirm .
