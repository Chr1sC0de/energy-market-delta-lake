Submitting a new contingency gas bid for the same hub and gas date overrides the old contingency bid for that hub and gas date. This can change the:
- From Gas Date
- To Gas Date
- Price
- Quantity
Note : If an existing bid is for a period of the 1st to 31st of the month, and the replacing bid is for a week within that month, then the replacing bid affects the week only. The remaining period of the month is as the original bid.
