STTM distribution system, Definition = A network of distribution pipelines that (typically) supplies gas from a hub to end users. Also see deemed STTM distribution system .. STTM distribution system allocation, Definition = The quantity of gas allocated by AEMO (in its role as retail market operator) to an STTM user on a gas day.. STTM distributor, Definition = The operator of an STTM distribution system.. STTM facility, Definition = A transmission pipeline or injection facility (including a production facility or storage facility) that is connected to a hub.. STTM facility allocation, Definition = The quantity of gas allocated by an STTM pipeline operator to an STTM shipper, which is submitted to AEMO for settlement of the market.. STTM facility hub capacity, Definition = The operational capacity of an STTM facility to flow gas to a hub.. STTM facility operator, Definition = The operator of an STTM facility (transmission pipelines or injection facilities (including storage facilities and production facilities))..
