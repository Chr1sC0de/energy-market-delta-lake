Definition = The contractual rules by which gas is allocated to shippers by a pipeline operator.. as available, Definition = Pertaining to a facility contract that has a haulage priority that is not firm. Also described as non- firm capacity (compared with firm capacity ).. bid, Definition = A generic term for a submission made by a trading participant to withdraw specified quantities of gas at specified prices. Bids should, in general, be referred to explicitly by type as 'ex ante bid', "price taker bid", or 'contingency gas bid'.. capacity charge, Definition = The charge paid by a shipper with a lower haulage priority when they displace a shipper with firm capacity on a capacity-constrained pipeline.. capacity limit, Definition = The maximum quantity of gas that can be scheduled to be withdrawn or delivered over a gas day on a facility service, distribution service, or trading right.. capacity payment, Definition = The payment made to a shipper with firm capacity when displaced by a shipper with a lower haulage priority on a capacity-constrained pipeline.. capacity price, Definition = The price calculated on each pipeline on which a capacity charge or payment is made.. constrained, Definition = An STTM
