1. On the Home page, click Contingency Bids and Offers
2.  Select the Contingency Gas Bids option
3.  On the Facility drop-down menu, select the facility that relates to the bid.
4.  Select the Direction option for the gas bid, that is, from the hub or to the hub.
5. Click New Contingency Gas Bid.
The Contingency Gas Bid screen displays:
Figure 40 The Contingency Gas Bid screen
6.  In the From Gas Date box, type the gas bid start date.
7.  In the To Gas Date , type the gas bid finish date.
8.  For each price-quantity step:
- In the Price $ box, type the bid prices in decreasing order.
- In the Daily Qty GJ box, type the daily GJ amounts in increasing cumulative order.
9.  Click the Submit Contingency Gas Bid button to submit the contingency bid.
10.  Click Confirm Contingency Gas Bid .
A message confirms the successful bid.
