Where a current facility contract is profiled (different capacities over different periods), the contract holder must create one or more registered facility services (and associated trading rights).
This can be done in several ways; for example:
- Register one Registered Facility Service (RFS) and modify the capacity for specified date ranges which means only a single trading right is used when making PTBs and ex ante bids or ex ante offers as per Figure 2 (a).
- Register a series of RFS capacities which, when stacked together, create the required profile as per Figure 2 (b), (c) and (d).
Use the choice of method that best suits your contractual terms, contract administration procedures, and trading procedures.
