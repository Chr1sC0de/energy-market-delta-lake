1. On the Home page, click Registered Services & Trading Rights.
2.  On the Registered Services drop-down menu, select the contract.
3.  Click Retrieve Registered Service .
4.  In the field under the heading 'Insert Registered Service Details' input the:
5. (a) Start Date and End Date of the new Registered Service Capacity.
6. (b) New Capacity Limit [GJ] reflected by the Facility/distributor contract
7. (c) Comments , type any comments detailing the modification.
8. (d) Click Submit .
The modified registered service now has a status of "Submitted" and is sent to the facility operator to confirm or reject the new modification as per the process detailed in section 3.8.
