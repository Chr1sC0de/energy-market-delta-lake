Submitting a new contingency offer for the same hub and gas date overrides the old contingency offer for that hub and gas date. This can change the:
- From Gas Date
- To Gas Date
- Price
- Quantity
Note : If an existing offer is for a period of the 1st to 31st of the month, and the replacing offer is for a week within that month, then the replacing offer affects the week only. The remaining period of the month is as the original offer.
