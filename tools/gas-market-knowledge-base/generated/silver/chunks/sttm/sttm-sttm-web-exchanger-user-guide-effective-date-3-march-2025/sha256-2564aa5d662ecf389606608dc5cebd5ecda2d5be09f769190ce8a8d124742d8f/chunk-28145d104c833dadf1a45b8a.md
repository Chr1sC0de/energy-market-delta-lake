modified market schedule, 1 = A trading participant's ex ante market schedule adjusted for confirmed market schedule variations, allocations of MOS gas, and scheduled contingency gas.. MOS, 1 = See market operator service .. MOScost cap, 1 = The maximum price that the market operator will pay for MOS gas.. MOSdecrease stack, 1 = An ordered list of price steps by which MOS gas is allocated to MOS providers when the gas flow expected under a pipeline schedule exceeds the quantity of gas that flowed.. MOSdecrease offer, 1 = An offer made by a MOS provider, comprising one or more price steps, to decrease flow on a pipeline to a hub.. MOSgas, 1 = Gas that is allocated to MOS providers (shippers) under the market operator service.. MOSincrease stack, 1 = An ordered list of price steps by which MOS gas is allocated to MOS providers when the gas flow expected under a pipeline schedule is less than the quantity of gas that flowed.. MOSincrease offer, 1 = An offer made by a MOS provider, comprising one or more price steps, to increase flow on a pipeline to a hub..
