The AEMO market systems validate STTM Bids and offers against the trading right that the bid or offer is submitted against. AEMO also validates the bid or offer against the trading right when that bid or offer is used by the STTM Market Clearing Engine.
Further details of the validations that AEMO uses are contained within the STTM Participant Build Pack Business Validations Addendum. This is part of the STTM SIP, which is available on the AEMO website.
