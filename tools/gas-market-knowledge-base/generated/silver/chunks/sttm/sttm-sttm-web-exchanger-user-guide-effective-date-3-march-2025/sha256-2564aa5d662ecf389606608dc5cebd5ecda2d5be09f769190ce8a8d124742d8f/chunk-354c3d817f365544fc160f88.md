To terminate a sub allocated trading right it is necessary to edit the trading right and amend the end date.
To edit a trading right,
1.  On the Trading Rights right:
2. page, select the edit icon against the contract holder's trading
Figure 28 Trading Rights page
This brings up the trading right information.
Note : Users should note the fields that are available for editing will change depending on the creation date and the edit date.
Figure 29 Trading Rights information dialog box
2.  Select the End Date box and insert a new End Date against the trading right.
3.  Click Submit .
This now becomes the new end date for the trading right.
Note : If a contract issuer and contract holder require the cancellation of a registered service earlier than the system date then the capacity of that registered service can be reduced to zero or the remainder of the contract period.
The contract holder can initiate this action through the central market systems by submitting an amendment to the capacity for the relevant date range of the registered service.  Alternatively, the contract issuer can cancel the registered service by making a request to AEMO in writing.
