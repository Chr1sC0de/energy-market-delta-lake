1.  On the Home Page, click Contingency Gas Bids and Offers Confirmation . The default is Contingency Gas Offer Selection.
The Contingency Gas Confirmation Selection screen displays.
Figure 43 Contingency Gas Offer Selection Screen
2.  Select the Type .
3.  On the Hub drop-down menu, select the hub that relates to the offer.
4.  On the Facility drop-down menu, select the facility that relates to the offer.
5.  On the Gas Date , select the gas day that relates to the offer.
6.  Select the Direction option for the gas offer, that is, from the hub, or to the hub.
7. Contingency Gas Offers drop down is populated with active contingency gas offer.
