1.  On the Home page, click Contingency Bids and Offers .
2.  Select the Contingency Gas Offers option.
3.  On the Facility drop-down menu, select the facility that relates to the offer.
4.  Select the Direction option for the gas offer.
5.  Click the New Contingency Gas Offer button.
The Contingency Gas Offer screen displays:
Figure 42 Contingency Gas Offer screen
6.  In the From Gas Date box, type the gas offer start date.
7.  In the To Gas Date , type the gas offer finish date.
8.  For each price-quantity step:
- In the Price $ box, type the offer prices in increasing order.
- In the Daily Qty GJ box, type the daily GJ amounts in increasing cumulative order.
9.  Click Submit Contingency Gas Offer to submit the contingency offer.
10. Click Confirm Contingency Gas Offer.
A message confirms the successful offer.
