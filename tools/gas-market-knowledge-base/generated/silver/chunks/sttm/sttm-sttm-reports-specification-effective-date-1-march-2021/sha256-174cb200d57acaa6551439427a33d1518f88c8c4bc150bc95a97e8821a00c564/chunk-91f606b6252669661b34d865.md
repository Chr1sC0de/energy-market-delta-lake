The purpose of this report is to provide the Trading Participant with their active Contingency Gas bids and offers for the next gas day. The Trading Participant can refer to this report to view a summary of the CG bids and offers that can be used in the CG bid/offer confirmation process if a CG event is called in the next gas day.
Access:
TP
Issued By:
18:00 AEST after Contingency Gas Bids/Offers close for the next gas day
Report Period:
All contingency gas bid/offer records valid for the next gas day
