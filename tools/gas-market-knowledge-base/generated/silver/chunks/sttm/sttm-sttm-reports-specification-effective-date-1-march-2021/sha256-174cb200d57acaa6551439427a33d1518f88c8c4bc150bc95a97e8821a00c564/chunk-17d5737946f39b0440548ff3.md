int\d\d\d([a-z]?)_v[0-9]{1,2}_([a-z0-9_\-\\_]{9,52})_rpt_[0-9]{1,3}[~]20\d\d(0[1-9]|1[012])(0[1-9]|[12][09]|3[01])([01][0-9]|[2][0-3])(0[0-9]|[12345][0-9])(0[0-9]|[12345][0-9])[.](CSV|csv)
Format of each component in the filename is detailed below:
Name Part, 1 = Regular Expression. Interface Number, 1 = int\d\d\d([a-z]?)
