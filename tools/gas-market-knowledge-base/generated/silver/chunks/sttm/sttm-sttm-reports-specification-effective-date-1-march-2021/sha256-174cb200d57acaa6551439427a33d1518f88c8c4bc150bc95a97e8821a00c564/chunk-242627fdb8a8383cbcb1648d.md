CFC, Quantity Derivation = Sum of all Registered Service allocation quantities for the Trading Participant for 'from the hub' facility services and distribution system services. Registered Service allocations for distribution systems are scaled.. COC, Quantity Derivation = Sum of all negative (non overrun) MOS allocation quantities for a gas date that is two days earlier than the current gas date.. COP, Quantity Derivation = Sum of all positive (non overrun) MOS allocation quantities for a gas date that is two days earlier than the current gas date.. CPC, Quantity Derivation = Sum of the 'as available' ct(k) EAQs(p,d,ct(k)) quantities as defined in the settlement equations.. CPP, Quantity Derivation = Sum of the 'firm' ct(k) EAQs(p,d,ct(k)) quantities as defined in the settlement equations.. CSC, Quantity Derivation = Sum of the 'from the hub' MQS(p,d,cf(k)) quantities as defined in the settlement equations.. CSP, Quantity Derivation = Sum of the 'to the hub' MQS(p,d,cf(k)) quantities as defined in the settlement equations..
