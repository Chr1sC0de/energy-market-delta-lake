This report contains deviation price and the data used to determine the deviation price for each STTM hub for a gas day.
NOTE: Publication of this report will commence on 1 November 2014. This is in line with the effective start date of National Gas Amendment (STTM Deviations and the Settlement Surplus and Shortfall) Rule 2014 No. 2.
Access:
Public
Issued By:
When the auto deviation calculation job is run successfully at 11.38 every day. It is also issued when the prudential monitoring job is run successfully on business days.
Report Period:
Gas days greater than or equal to report date minus SEVEN days
Trigger:
Successful completion of the auto deviation calculation job and prudential run

int690_v1_deviation_price_data_rpt_1~yyyymmddhhmmss
