- (a) A STTM Participant must maintain with AEMO at least two user accounts (if required by the STTM participant to comply with the Rules) to access the Market Information System (MIS), the STTM WebExchanger (SWEX) and the STTM Webexchanger Interface Engine (SWEXIE).
- (b) A STTM participant must nominate one or more individuals to be a registered contact to act on behalf of the STTM participant in dealings with AEMO.
- (c) A STTM participant is responsible for requesting AEMO update any registered contacts detail when they change.
- (d) AEMO will implement reasonable security and verification measures to address the risk of unauthorised access. STTM participants are solely responsible for managing and ensuring the security of their allocated accounts.
