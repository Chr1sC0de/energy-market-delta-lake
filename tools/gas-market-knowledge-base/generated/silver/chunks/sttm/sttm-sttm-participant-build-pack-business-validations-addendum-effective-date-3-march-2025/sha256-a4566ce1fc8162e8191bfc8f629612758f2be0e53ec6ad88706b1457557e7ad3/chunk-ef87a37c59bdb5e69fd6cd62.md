A Trading Participant may submit Ex Ante (applies to D-1, D-2 and D-3 schedules) Bids and Offers to AEMO via a CSV file. AEMO sends the ACK/NACK depending upon the initial validation results. Subsequent validation is performed on the data and a transaction acknowledgement is sent to the Trading Participant.
