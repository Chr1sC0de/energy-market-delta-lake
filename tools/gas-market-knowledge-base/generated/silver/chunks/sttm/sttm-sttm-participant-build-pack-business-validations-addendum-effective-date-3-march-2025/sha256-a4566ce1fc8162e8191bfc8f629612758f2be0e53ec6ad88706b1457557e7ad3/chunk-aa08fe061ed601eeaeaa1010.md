Table 49 Deemed STTM Distribution System Allocation Transaction Context
Trigger, 1 = Whenever the allocation agent for an STTM pipeline operator gives AEMO an allocation notice in accordance with rule 419(1) or an updated allocation notice in accordance with rule 419(4), the STTM pipeline operator must, for the relevant gas day, provide to AEMO: (a) an update of the quantities provided under STTM Procedures clause 7.2.5; and (b) the quantity or an updated quantity of gas it deems to be withdrawn by each STTM User to each deemed STTM distribution system.. Rules and Procedures References, 1 = STTM Procedures clause 7.2.9. Pre-condition, 1 = The facility allocation agent must have: • a list of all STTM Users with 'Active' Deemed STTM Distribution System capacities for the facility for the relevant gas day. Facility operators may source this from the STTM report INT733.
