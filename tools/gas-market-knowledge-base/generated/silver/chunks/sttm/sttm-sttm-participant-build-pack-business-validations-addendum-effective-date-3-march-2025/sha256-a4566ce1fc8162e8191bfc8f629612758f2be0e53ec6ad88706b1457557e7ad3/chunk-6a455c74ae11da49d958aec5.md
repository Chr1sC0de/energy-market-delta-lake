Trigger, 1 = This transaction is initiated by a Trading Participant, who is a STTM Shipper or User, when they intend to bid or offer for contingency gas in the STTM.. Rules and Procedures References, 1 = Rules 435, 436. Pre-condition, 1 = A valid STTM Shipper or User on the days for which the contingency gas bid or offer is submitted must have decided the quantity of contingency gas they intend to offer or bid into the STTM.
Post-condition The STTM Participant's submitted Contingency Gas bid or offer is validated and stored in the STTM systems for use in Contingency Gas scheduling and settlements.
