Trigger, 1 = This transaction is initiated by a Trading Participant when they intend to bid for gas at the hub market price in the STTM.. Rules and Procedures References, 1 = Rule 409. Pre-condition, 1 = The Trading Participant must hold a valid Trading Right for the days for which the price taker bid is submitted and must have decided the quantity of gas required.. Post-condition, 1 = The Trading Participant's submitted price taker bid is validated and stored in the STTM systems for use in scheduling.
