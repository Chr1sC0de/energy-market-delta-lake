Trading Participant.
 ad hoc payment means an amount determined under clause 8.4.4 or 10.6.2 that is payable by AEMO to a
                                 Trading Participant.

 default gas day capacity        means the capacity of an STTM facility for a gas day that is provided to AEMO by the STTM
                                 facility operator under rule 376(1)(f), or determined by AEMO under rule 377(2) or clause
                                 7.1.6.

 deemed STTM distributor means a user who is taken to be an STTM distributor in respect of a facility under rule
                                 372A(3)(c)

 deviation settlement            means a mathematical function used to define deviation charges and deviation payments to be
 function                        applied to a Trading Participant's deviation quantities.

 exiting retailer                means an STTM User in respect of whom responsibility for customers that are connected to
                                 an STTM distribution system has been assumed by a retailer of last resort.

 hub price means the price for gas at the hub determined under clauses 6.5.8(a)(i) and 6.5.8(c) which
                                 may represent either the ex ante market price or ex post imbalance price (as applicable).
 incremental price step means the incremental increase in quantity bid or offered in a price step which can be
 quantity                        scheduled within the capacity limit of a registered trading right.

 linepack                        in respect of an STTM pipeline at any time means the total quantity of gas in that STTM
                                 pipeline at that time.
 linepack range See clause 9.2.1.

 major retailer of last resort See clause 8.3.2(b).
 event

 marginal capacity value means the marginal value of a unit of facility hub capacity determined under clause 6.5.8(a)(ii).
 marginal cost means, for a quantity of gas purchased, the incremental cost, as implied by the SPA, of
                                 supplying an increment of that quantity.

 marginal value means, for a constrained quantity, the incremental change in cost of the solution to the SPA
                                 resulting from an incremental change in that constrained quantity.

 marginal flow direction means the marginal value of the capability to increase withdrawal from a hub on an STTM
 value facility when that withdrawal is limited by the flow to the hub on that STTM facility.

 market facility means an STTM facility or STTM distribution system.
