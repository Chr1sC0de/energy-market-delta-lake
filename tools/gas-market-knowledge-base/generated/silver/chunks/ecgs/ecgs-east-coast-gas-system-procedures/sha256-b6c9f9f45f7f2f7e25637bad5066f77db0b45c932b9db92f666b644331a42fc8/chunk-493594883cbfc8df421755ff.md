- (a) Initial applications for registration as the responsible reporting entity for an LNG export project must be made within 10 business days of these Procedures coming into effect.
- (b) Applications must be submitted to AEMO by email to: onboarding@aemo.com.au.
- (c) The application must contain the following information:
- (i) company name;
- (ii) ABN;
- (iii) primary contact name;
- (iv) primary contact role;
- (v) primary contact phone number;
- (vi) primary contact email address;
- (d) The application must be signed by an authorised signatory of the applicant organisation. If there is more than one owner, operator or controller of the LNG export project , written evidence of appointment of the applicant as responsible reporting entity for the LNG export project must be provided by each owner, operator or controller.
- (e) The application must include a statement of any other registrable capacity that the applicant organisation has with AEMO and indicate whether the organisation has access to AEMO's market systems .
