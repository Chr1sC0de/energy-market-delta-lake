- (B) Where paragraph (A) does not apply, or a price specified in paragraph (A) is not available, the benchmark rate will be the Wallumbilla benchmark price as defined in the Gas Supply Hub benchmark price methodology published on AEMO's website.
- (iii) Storage and compression services: the standing price published in accordance with Part 18A of the Rules will apply, or, where no such standing price has been published, the benchmark rate will be determined by reference to the standing price of a similar service for which a price has been published.
- (b) Where clause 4.2(a) requires a benchmark price to be determined by reference to a similar service, AEMO will apply the following principles to the extent practicable:
- (i) the service should be in the same State;
- (ii) the facility should be similar in size; and
- (iii) where there are multiple similar services, an average of the standing prices for such services should be used.
