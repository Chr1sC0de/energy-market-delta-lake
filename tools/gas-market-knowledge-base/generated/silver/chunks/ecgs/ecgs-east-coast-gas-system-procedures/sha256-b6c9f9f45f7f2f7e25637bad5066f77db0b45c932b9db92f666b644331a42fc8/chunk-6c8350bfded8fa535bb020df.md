- (a) AEMO may send notices to mobile numbers and email addresses on the Part 27 register .
- (b) A public notice (e.g. a risk or threat notice , conference notice, direction or trading notice, or notice of claim under rule 705(1)) is taken to have been published by AEMO at the time it is published on AEMO's website.
- (c) A private notice (e.g., a direction ) to a specific relevant entity is taken to have been received at the time it is sent by AEMO to a primary contact email address of the relevant entity on the Part 27 register or, if the relevant entity is not on the Part 27 register , to an email address as advised by the relevant entity to AEMO.
