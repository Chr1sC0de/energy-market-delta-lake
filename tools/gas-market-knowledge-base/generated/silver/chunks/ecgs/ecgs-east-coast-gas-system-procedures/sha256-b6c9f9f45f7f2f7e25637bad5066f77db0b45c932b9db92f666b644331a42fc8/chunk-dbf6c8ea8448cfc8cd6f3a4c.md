- (b) The quantity of gas that the project expects to supply to the domestic market for consumption in an east coast jurisdiction must be specified in TJ/month.
- (c) If there has been a change to any of the values submitted in accordance with clause 2.2.7(b) in the most recently submitted domestic supply forecast, updated information must be provided as soon as practicable where the absolute value of the change for a month is greater than 100 TJ.
- (d) The supply side of a gas swap transaction (both time and/or location) must be included in the domestic supply forecast.
- (e) Gas that is to be made available on a gas trading exchange administered by AEMO must be excluded from the domestic supply forecast.
