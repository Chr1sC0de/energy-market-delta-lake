- (e) For the purpose of Part 27 of the NGR and these Procedures, maintenance work, in relation to a facility, means work that results in a constraint on the normal operation of the facility by an amount that exceeds the greater of A and B where:
- (i) A is 5 TJ/day; and
- (ii) B is the lesser of 10% of the relevant nameplate rating of the facility and 30 TJ/day.
- (f) If there has been a change to any of the values submitted in accordance with clause 2.2.2(b)(iii), (b)(iv) or (b)(v), updated information must be provided as soon as practicable where that change exceeds the greater of A and B where:
- (i) A is 5 TJ/day; and
- (ii) B is the lesser of 10% of the relevant nameplate rating of the facility and 30 TJ/day.
- (g) The medium term maintenance submission will utilise the Part 18 medium term capacity outlook submission as specified in the BB Data Submission Guide.
