- (c) Where these Procedures contain a summary of a Rule in italics, the summary is for ease of reference only and does not form part of the Procedures.
- (d) A reference to:
- (i) gas day D is a reference to whichever gas day is designated by the relevant clause;

- (iii) gas day D+n is a reference to the gas day occurring n gas days after gas day D;

- (vi) W+n is a reference to the week occurring n weeks after week W;
