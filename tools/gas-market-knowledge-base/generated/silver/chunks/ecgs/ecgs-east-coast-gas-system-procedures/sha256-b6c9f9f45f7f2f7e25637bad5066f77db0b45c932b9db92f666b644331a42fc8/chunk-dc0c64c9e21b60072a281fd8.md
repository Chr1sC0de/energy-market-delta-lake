(a), 1 = Where a BB reporting entity for a BB pipeline is required under Part 27 of the NGR to provide to AEMO a daily capacity for each pipeline segment ( short term capacity outlook ), the BB reporting entity must provide the information by 7.00 pm each gas day D-1 day for the period D to D+6.. (b), 1 = The short term capacity outlook must be provided in TJ/day.. (c), 1 = A BB reporting entity will satisfy the requirements of this clause through the submission of the Bulletin Board short term capacity outlook .. (d), 1 = The short term capacity outlook for each BB pipeline must include the outlook for each pipeline segment of that facility..
