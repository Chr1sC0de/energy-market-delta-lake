Rule 698(1) requires AEMO to publish a post-intervention report in accordance with the Procedures within 4 months after the end of the exercise of a direction or trading function.
- (a) AEMO will endeavour to publish a preliminary post-intervention report within 1 month of:
- (i) the end of the exercise of a single direction or trading function ; or
- (ii) the end of the exercise of all direction or trading functions AEMO considers relate to the same identified risk or threat .
- (b) AEMO will publish a final post-intervention report within the time specified in Rule 698(1).
- (c) AEMO will publish post-intervention reports on AEMO's website .
