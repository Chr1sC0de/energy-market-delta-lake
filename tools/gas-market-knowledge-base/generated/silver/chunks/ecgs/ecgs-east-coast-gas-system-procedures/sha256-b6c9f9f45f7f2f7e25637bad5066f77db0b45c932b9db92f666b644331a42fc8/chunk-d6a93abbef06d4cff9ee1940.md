Rule 713(1) provides that AEMO may keep and maintain a register of relevant entities for the purpose of Part 27 and may publish the register in accordance with the Procedures. Rule 135EA(7) provides that AEMO may specify in the Procedures the classes of relevant entities who must provide contact details to be included in the Part 27 register, the information that must be recorded in the Part 27 Register and the manner and form in which the Part 27 register must be kept and maintained.
