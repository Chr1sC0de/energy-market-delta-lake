Rule 691(1)(a) requires the responsible reporting entity for an LNG export project to provide forecasts of expected supply to the domestic markets for consumption in an east coast jurisdiction.
- (a) Where the responsible reporting entity for an LNG export project is required under Part 27 of the NGR to provide to AEMO a monthly forecast of expected supply to the domestic market for consumption in an east coast jurisdiction , the responsible reporting entity must provide the information by 10.00 pm on the last gas day of each month M-1 for month M to M+5.
