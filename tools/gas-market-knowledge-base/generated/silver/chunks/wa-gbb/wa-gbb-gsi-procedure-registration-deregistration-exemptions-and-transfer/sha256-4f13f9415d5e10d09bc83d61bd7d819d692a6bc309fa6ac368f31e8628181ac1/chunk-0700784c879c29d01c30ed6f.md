- 4.2.1. The Applicant must provide the following Standing Data in the applicable Facility Registration Application Form in relation to a Storage Facility that is required to be registered [subrule 25(2)] :
- (a) the GBB Pipeline(s) to which the Storage Facility is connected and the physical Delivery Point(s) and Receipt Point(s) at which the facility is connected [subrule 62(1)] ;
- (b) the Production Nameplate Capacity of the Storage Facility;
- (c) the Refill Nameplate Capacity of the Storage Facility;
- (d) the Storage Nameplate Capacity of the Storage Facility; and
