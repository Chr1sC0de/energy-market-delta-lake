- 3.2.6. Where the Applicant is not a Registered Participant, it must submit a Participant Registration Application Form at the same time as a Facility Registration Application Form.
- 3.2.7. Where the Applicant is applying for registration of a Production Facility, it must provide the billing contact information specified in the Production Facility Application Form.
- 3.2.8. An Applicant must include the applicable Standing Data in each Facility Registration Application Form. Further details on Standing Data to be included in respect of each type of GBB Facility are set out in CHAPTER 4 of this Procedure.
- 3.2.9. Where an Applicant has been nominated by the owner, controller or operator (as applicable) of the Facility, the Applicant must include in the Facility Registration Application Form information to demonstrate that each owner, controller or operator (as applicable) of the Facility consents to the Applicant submitting a Registration Application in relation to that Facility.
