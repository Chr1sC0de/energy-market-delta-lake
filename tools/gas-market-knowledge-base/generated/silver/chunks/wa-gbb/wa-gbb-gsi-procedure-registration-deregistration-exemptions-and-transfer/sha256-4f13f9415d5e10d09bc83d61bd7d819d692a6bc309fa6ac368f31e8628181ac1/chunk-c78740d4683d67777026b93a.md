- 8.3.1. Where AEMO reasonably believes that a Facility previously granted an Exemption, no longer satisfies the requirement of an Exemption, it may revoke the Exemption. [rule 48]
- 8.3.2. Where AEMO decides to revoke an Exemption, it must notify the operator of the  Facility by email or post, of the:
- (a) reasons for the revocation; and
- (b) Exemption Cancellation Date which must be no earlier than 20 Business Days after the date of the revocation notice.
- 8.3.3. As soon as practicable after issuing the revocation notice, AEMO must:
- (a) publish the reasons for revocation on the GSI Website;
- (b) amend the GSI Register to record the revocation; and
- (c) publish a notice of the change to the GSI Register on the GSI Website.
- 8.3.4. If a Gas Market Participant receives a revocation notice, it must, as soon as practicable, make a Registration Application in relation to the Facility in accordance with Chapter CHAPTER 3 of this Procedure.
