- 4.1.4. Where AEMO advises the operator of a GBB Pipeline that it must provide information in relation to one or more Gate Stations, the operator of the GBB Pipeline must, by no later than 8:00 AM on the Gas Day on which the operator must start submitting data to the GBB in relation to the GBB Pipeline, provide to AEMO the Nameplate Capacity of each Gate Station.
