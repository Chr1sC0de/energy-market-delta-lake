- 1.2.1. This Procedure outlines the process of:
- (a) participant registration and deregistration;
- (b) Facility registration, deregistration and transfer;
- (c) exemption from providing Nominated and Forecast Flow Data, Daily Actual Flow Data and Daily Actual Consumption Data; and
- (d) Exemption from registration.
