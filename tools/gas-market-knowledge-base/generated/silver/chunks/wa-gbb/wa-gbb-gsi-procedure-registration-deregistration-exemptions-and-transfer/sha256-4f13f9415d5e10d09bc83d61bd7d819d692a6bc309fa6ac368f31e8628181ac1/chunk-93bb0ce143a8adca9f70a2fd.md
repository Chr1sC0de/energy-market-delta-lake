- 3.3.15.  Where AEMO refuses a Registration Application for any of the reasons in subrule 28(3), AEMO must notify the Applicant, and the owner, controller or operator (as applicable) by email or post, that its application has been refused and give reasons for refusal, by the relevant date in subrule 28(4).
- 3.3.16.  Where AEMO refuses the application because of insufficient information provided in the Registration Application within the specified timeframes, the Gas Market Participant must reapply for registration in the appropriate category and must provide the required information.
