Information and help desk service for Gas Supply Hub market operations are provided by the AEMO Information and Support Hub.  The Information and Support Hub also assists Participants with the resolution of IT related problems. Support for IT related problems is available 24 hours, 7 days a week through the Support Hub phone number. Contact details are available through the AEMO website 2 .
