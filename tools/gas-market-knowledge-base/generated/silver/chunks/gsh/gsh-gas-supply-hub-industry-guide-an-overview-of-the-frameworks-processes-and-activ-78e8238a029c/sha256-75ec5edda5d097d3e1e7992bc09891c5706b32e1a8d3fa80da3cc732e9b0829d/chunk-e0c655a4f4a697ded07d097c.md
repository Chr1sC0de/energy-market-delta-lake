Active orders in the market:
- Trader B places a bid to buy gas at MSP for $6.00 / GJ.
- Trader C places an offer to sell gas at SWQP for $4.00 / GJ.
- The combination of Trader B and Trader C's orders creates an implied offer of -$2.00 for the spread product.
In this example, Trader A has a Gas Transportation Agreement for the transportation of gas from Wallumbilla to Moomba. Trader A submits a bid for the Spread Product at a price of -$2.00 / GJ.  The exchange matches the spread product orders creating the following transactions:
- Trader A buys gas from Trader C at the SWQP trading location for $4.00 / GJ, and
- Trader A sells gas to Trader B at the MSP trading location for $6.00 / GJ.
