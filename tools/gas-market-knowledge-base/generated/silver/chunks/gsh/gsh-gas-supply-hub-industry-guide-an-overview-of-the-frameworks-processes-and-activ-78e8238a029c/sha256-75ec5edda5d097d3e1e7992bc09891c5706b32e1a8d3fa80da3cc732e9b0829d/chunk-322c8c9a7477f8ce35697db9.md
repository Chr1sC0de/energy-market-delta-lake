AEMO may, in accordance with the Exchange Agreement, restrict a member from access to or use of the Trading System in whole or part if a suspension event has occurred.  AEMO may also suspend a Member at the request of the AER.
A trading halt is lifted only when AEMO is satisfied that the reason for the suspension no longer applies, or the act giving rise to the suspension no longer warrants continued suspension.
