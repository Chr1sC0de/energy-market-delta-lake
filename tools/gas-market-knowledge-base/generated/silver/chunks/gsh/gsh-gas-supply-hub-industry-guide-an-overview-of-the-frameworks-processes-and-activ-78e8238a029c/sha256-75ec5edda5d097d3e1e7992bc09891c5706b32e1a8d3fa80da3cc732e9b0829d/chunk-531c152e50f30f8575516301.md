All transactions executed on the exchange are financially settled by AEMO. For each transaction, the buyer makes a payment to AEMO (Physical Gas Charge) and in turn AEMO makes a payment to the seller (Physical Gas Payment).
The settlement amount is the product of the transaction price and quantity.  Settlement for all Transactions, including Weekly products, is calculated separately for each Gas Day.  The Trading Amounts are then invoiced in the Billing Period in which the Gas Day falls.
