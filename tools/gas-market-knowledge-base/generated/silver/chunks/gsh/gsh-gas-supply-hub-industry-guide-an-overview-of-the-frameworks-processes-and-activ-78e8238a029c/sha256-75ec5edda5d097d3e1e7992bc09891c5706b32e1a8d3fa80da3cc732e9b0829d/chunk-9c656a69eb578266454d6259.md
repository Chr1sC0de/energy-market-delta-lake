In this example, Trader E who holds pipeline capacity to transport gas from Moomba to Wallumbilla sells the spread product at $1.25/GJ. The matching of spread product orders would create a transaction in the Wallumbilla and Moomba products:
- Trader E buys gas at Moomba for $5.25 (matched with the Trader A's sell order in the Moomba product), and
- Trader E sells gas at Wallumbilla for $6.50 (matched with Trader C's buy order in the Wallumbilla product).
Trader A enters a buy order for $1.25 / GJ in the spread product. Trader B enters a sell order at Wallumbilla for $6.25/GJ. This creates an implied sell order in the Moomba product of $5.00/GJ.
