The Alternative Delivery Point (ADP) addendum is a standard contract that can document a bilateral agreement to carry out gas delivery at an alternative delivery point. The ADP addendum can be from an existing product location or a location that is not defined within an existing product (i.e. the delivery point does not have to be located at the Wallumbilla or Moomba hub).Trading participants can agree on an ADP when they receive their Delivery Obligation report post trade, or during an off-market trade negotiation for a Balance-of-Day and Day-Ahead product. The ADP addendum can also be used with non-netted products to negotiate and then designate an alternate delivery point for an off-market trade in all product tenors. For further information on non-netted products, see Section 8.5.
