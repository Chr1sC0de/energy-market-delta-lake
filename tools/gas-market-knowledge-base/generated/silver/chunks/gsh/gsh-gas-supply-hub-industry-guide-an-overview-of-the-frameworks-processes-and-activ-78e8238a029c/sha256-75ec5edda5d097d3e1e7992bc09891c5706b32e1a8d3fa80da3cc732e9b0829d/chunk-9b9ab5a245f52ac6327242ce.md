The delivery period for the day-ahead product is a single Gas Day. The trading window for the day-ahead product opens at the start of trading on D-1 and closes at 12:30 AEST.
Day-ahead products are netted against other day-ahead, daily, weekly and monthly transaction obligations.
Note . This guide refers to market operations relating to a particular gas day D, including activities that occur before (D-) or after (D+) the gas day. The term D-1 refers to actions taken the day before the gas day with respect to gas day D.
