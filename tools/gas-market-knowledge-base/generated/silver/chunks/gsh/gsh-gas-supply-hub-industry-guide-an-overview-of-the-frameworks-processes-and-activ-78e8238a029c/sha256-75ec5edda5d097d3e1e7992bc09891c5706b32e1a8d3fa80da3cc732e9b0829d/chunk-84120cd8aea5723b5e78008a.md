A Trading Participant can withdraw their active orders through the ETS.  The withdrawal of an order means that it cannot be matched by the exchange and can only be viewed by Trading Participant that created the order.  An order that has been withdrawn can be reactivated by a Trading Participant avoiding the need to re-enter the components of the order.
