If netting is applicable to a product then it is defined as such in the product specification.  The netting of delivery obligations is applicable to all Day-ahead (netted), Daily, Weekly and Monthly products. Pre-matched transactions are included in the delivery netting process, unless the Wallumbilla or Moomba Non-netted product is used.
As shown in Figure 11, following the close of the trading day AEMO retrieves all Daily, Weekly and Monthly transactions covering the netting gas day (D-2).
