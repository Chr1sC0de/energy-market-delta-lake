Products are for physically delivered gas, the terms of which include a warranty that the transacting parties have the necessary rights at the delivery point to give effect to the delivery and receipt of the gas. Products listed on the exchange are for the sale and purchase of gas delivered at one of the three major connecting pipelines at Wallumbilla or one of two pipelines connecting at Moomba. There are separate products for each pipeline (Trading Location) and delivery period (on-the-day, day ahead, daily, weekly and monthly).
Products are available on a short-term basis and provide Trading Participants with an efficient option for balancing their gas portfolio requirements around long-term agreements.
