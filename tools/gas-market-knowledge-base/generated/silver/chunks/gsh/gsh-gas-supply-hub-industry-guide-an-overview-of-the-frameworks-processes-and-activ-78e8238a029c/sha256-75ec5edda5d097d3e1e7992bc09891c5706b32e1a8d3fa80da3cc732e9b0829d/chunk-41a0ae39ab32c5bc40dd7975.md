The Gas Supply Hub was initially established to trade gas at Wallumbilla because it is close to significant gas supply and demand and is a major transit point between Queensland and the gas markets on Aus tralia's east coast. As illustrated in Figure 1, Wallumbilla acts as a collection point for major gas fields and a supply point for demand centres in Gladstone and Brisbane and is located near gas storage facilities and gas-powered generation. The diversity of business activities and the number of industry participants at Wallumbilla creates a natural point of trade.
