The exchange provides participants with the ability to deal multiple orders with a single submission by entering an order for the cumulative volume at the highest (buying) or lowest (selling) order price.
This feature provides efficiencies for participants when entering trades. Instead of entering separate orders at different prices, the submitting party can realise the same outcome with a single order submission.
