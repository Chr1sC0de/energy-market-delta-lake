Introduction to the type of reports provided to participants There are three different categories of report distribution for the Gas Supply Hub:
- Private : Report contains information pertaining to the participant it is issued to only.
- Participants : All Market Participants receive a copy of the report.
- Public : report is made available to all participants and is also made available on the AEMO website.
