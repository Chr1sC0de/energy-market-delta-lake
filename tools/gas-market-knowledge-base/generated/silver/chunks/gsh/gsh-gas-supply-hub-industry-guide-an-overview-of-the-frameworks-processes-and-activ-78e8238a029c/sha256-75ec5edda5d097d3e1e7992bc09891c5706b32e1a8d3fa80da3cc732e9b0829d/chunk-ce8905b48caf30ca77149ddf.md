If a participant fails to make a payment due in relation to an invoice, then the following steps occur:
1. AEMO issues notice to participant
2. The participant is suspended and given 1 hour to pay amounts outstanding and re-instate collateral.
3. If payment is not received after 1 hour, AEMO draws down collateral, does not allow submission of new orders and suspends open orders not yet traded.
4. The participant has until next business day after non-payment to pay the outstanding amount and reinstate collateral.
