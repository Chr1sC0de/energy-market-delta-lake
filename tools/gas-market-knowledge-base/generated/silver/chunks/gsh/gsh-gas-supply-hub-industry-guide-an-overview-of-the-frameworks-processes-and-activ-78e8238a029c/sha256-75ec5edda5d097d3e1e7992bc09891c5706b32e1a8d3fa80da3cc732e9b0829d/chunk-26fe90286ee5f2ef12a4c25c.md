The Moomba-Wallumbilla spread product has a positive value for gas notionally moving east to Wallumbilla, and a negative value for gas notionally moving west to Moomba. In accordance with the pricing convention for Moomba-Wallumbilla Spread Products, buy and sell order prices represent:
Spread Product buy order price = Wallumbilla buy order price less Moomba sell order price Spread Product sell order price = Wallumbilla sell order price less Moomba buy order price
