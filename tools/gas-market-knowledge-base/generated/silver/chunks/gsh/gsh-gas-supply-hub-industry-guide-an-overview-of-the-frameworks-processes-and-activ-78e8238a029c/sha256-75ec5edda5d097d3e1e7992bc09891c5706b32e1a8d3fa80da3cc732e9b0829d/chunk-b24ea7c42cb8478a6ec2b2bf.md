The Transaction Quantity for all products is measured as a quantity per Gas Day (GJ/day). Minimum quantities and parcel size for the product are detailed in the product specifications found in the Exchange Agreement.
Unless otherwise agreed between the parties, gas delivery is at a constant hourly flow rate for the period of a transaction.
