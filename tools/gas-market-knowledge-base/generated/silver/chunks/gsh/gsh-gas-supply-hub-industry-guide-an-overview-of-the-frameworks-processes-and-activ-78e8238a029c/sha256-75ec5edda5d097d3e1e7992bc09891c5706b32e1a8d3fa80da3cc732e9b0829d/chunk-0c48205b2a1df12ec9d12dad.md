AEMO reports on the average transaction price and the aggregate quantity of transactions at the end of each day:
- Daily transaction summary report : is a summary of trades executed during the trading day including high and low prices, total traded quantities and number of trades.
- Historical gas day transaction report : average price for historical gas days.
The average price is determined for each Gas Day and Trading Location as a volume weighted average of transaction prices across all products.  The average price is used in the settlement of energy reallocations and in the settlement of delivery variations for netted products.
