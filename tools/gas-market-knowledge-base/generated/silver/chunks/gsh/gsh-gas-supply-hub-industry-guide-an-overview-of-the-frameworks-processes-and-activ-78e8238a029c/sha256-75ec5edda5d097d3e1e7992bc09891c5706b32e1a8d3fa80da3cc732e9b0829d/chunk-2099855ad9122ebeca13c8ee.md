The electronic transfer of funds is required to support the following functions:
- The clearing of the market
- The processing of a security deposit for prudential purposes.
AEMO uses the Austraclear system for all the markets it operates, as defined in the AEMO Market Clearing Procedure. This system provides real-time gross settlement of transactions and is widely used in the finance industry for the settlement of transactions.
