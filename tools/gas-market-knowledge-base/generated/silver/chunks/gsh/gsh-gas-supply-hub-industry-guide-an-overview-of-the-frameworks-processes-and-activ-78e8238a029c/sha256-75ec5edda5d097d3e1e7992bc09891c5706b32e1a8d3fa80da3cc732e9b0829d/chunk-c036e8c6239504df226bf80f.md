Access to Gas Supply Hub Direct is through AEMO's Energy Market Systems (EMS) web portal.  Organisations already participating in the NEM, STTM or the DWGM can access the Gas Supply Hub Direct using their existing EMS login.
Organisations registering in the Gas Supply Hub only are issued with user account details for a super user. The super user can then create and maintain user accounts for other representatives of their organisation.
