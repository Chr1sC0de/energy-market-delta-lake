Transaction fees are settled based on the quantity of transactions executed by a Trading Participant. The transaction fee applied is different for weekly and daily type products.
The Transaction Fee is settled with reference to the day the transaction is executed.
Note . Information about the current fee schedule is published in the Gas Supply Hub Exchange Fees document.
