The designation of the Delivery Point at the Moomba trading locations to a transaction is a seller led process
1. Seller must specify a Delivery Point at time of order entry.  The Delivery Point specified by seller becomes the transaction delivery point.
- a. If the product is netted for delivery then delivery point associated with the most recent transactions are applied to the gas delivery obligation.
2. Once orders have been matched to form a transaction, the buyer and seller receive a report detailing the assigned Delivery Point.
- a. If the product is netted for delivery this confirmation is issued after the netting process has been completed.
3. Buyer nominates to the relevant pipeline operator for the receipt of gas at the Delivery Point.
4. Title transfer occurs in accordance with the allocation agreement at the Delivery Point.
