The Gas Supply Hub has been established through amendments to the National Gas Law (NGL), National Gas Rules (NGR), an Exchange Agreement and a set of procedures. The Exchange Agreement sets out the terms of participation in the Gas Supply Hub and the terms governing transactions entered into through the exchange.
