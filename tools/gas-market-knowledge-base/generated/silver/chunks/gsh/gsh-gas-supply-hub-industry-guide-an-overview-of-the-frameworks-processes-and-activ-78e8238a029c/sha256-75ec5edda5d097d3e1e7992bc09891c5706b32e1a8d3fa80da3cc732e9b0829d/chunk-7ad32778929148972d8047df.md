A participant's Trading Limit is equal to the amount of credit support lodged with AEMO.
In the event of a payment default AEMO would make a call on that participant's collateral so that it can meet the payments to traders that are owed money from the market. Any residual shortfall would reduce the payments to traders on a pro rata basis, as in the STTM and the DWGM.
