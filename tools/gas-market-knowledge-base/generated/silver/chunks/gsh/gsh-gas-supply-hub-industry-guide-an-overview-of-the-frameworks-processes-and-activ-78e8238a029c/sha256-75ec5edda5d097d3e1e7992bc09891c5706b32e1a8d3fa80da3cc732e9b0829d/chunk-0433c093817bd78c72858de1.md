- Authorised to view orders and transactional information through the trading exchange.
- Does not have any financial involvement in the market.
- Can use the Capacity Listing Service.
Trading Participants and Viewing Participants have access to the exchange and can view price and quantities of active orders and recent transactions.
In the Exchange Agreement, the term Market Participant is used in place of Trading Participant and Reallocation Participants when describing settlement and prudential obligations.
Viewing Participants pay an upfront annual fee for a licence to access the exchange, while Market Participant fees are recovered on a monthly basis through the transaction settlement process.
