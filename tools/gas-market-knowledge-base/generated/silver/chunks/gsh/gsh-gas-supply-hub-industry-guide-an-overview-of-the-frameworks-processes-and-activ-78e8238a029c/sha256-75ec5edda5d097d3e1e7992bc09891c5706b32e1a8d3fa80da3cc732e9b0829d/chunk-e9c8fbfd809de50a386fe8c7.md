The seller must ensure that gas delivered meets the quality specification and is within the pressure range for the relevant delivery point.  Gas quality and pressure specifications are defined in the product schedules.
Note . Section 14.5 of the Exchange Agreement outlines the responsibilities and the process for managing off-specification gas.
