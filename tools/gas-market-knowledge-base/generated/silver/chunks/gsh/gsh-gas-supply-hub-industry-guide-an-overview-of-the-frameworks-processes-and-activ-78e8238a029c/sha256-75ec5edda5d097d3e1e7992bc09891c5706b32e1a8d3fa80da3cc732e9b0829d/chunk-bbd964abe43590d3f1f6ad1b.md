As a major trans-shipment point, Moomba is a natural location for wholesale gas trading. Like Wallumbilla, Moomba is located at the intersection of gas pipelines that supply downstream markets and large industrial users in eastern Australia. With its significant gas processing and storage capacity, a Moomba hub can pool together a broad spectrum of potential trading participants operating in southern/eastern Australia.
The Moomba Hub operates with the same market and legal framework as Wallumbilla. The standardisation of gas products and the easy-to-use electronic trading platform reduces transaction costs and assists short-term wholesale gas trading.
