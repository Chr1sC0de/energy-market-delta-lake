The prudential dashboard allows participants to view their trading limit, prudential exposure, bank guarantees and security deposits.
The dashboard allows participants to retrieve detailed information about the components of their prudential exposure including settlement and reallocation amounts.  Participants can also download the latest version of the Prudential Exposure Report from the dashboard.
