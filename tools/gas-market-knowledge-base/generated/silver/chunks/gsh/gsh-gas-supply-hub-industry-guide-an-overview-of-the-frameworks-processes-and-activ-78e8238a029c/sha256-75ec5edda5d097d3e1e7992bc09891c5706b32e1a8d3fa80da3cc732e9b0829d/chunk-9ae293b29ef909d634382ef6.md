Offsetting buy and sell transactions are aggregated together to determine a net delivery position for each gas day and Trading Location. In the case of Weekly and Monthly transactions, each gas day within the transaction is considered at close of trading on D2 when calculating a participant's net gas day position.
Figure 12 Net delivery position
The Delivery Point submitted by a seller at the time of their order submission is applied to their net position.
However, a Trading Participant may enter into offsetting buy and sell transactions that could result in multiple delivery points being be attributable to a net position.  In this event, the Delivery Points associated with the most recent transactions are applied to the net position.  The net position is split by Delivery Point so that the inputs to the matching process have a single Delivery Point.
