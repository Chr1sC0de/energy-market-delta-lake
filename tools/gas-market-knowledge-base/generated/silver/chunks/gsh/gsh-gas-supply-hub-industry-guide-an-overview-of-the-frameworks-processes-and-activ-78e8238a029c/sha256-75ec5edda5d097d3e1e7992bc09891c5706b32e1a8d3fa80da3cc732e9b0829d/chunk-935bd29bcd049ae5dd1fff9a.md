Table 1 Trading window
On the day, Trading window = The Balance of day product is available for trading on the gas day.. On the day, Product instances = One active product. On the day, Gas delivery = Each individual transaction must be delivered. Day-ahead (non-netted), Trading window = A gas day product that is available on for trading on the day prior to the delivery gas day, from 12:30 AEST.. Day-ahead (netted), Trading window = A gas day product that is available on for trading on the day prior to the delivery gas day, up until 12:30 AEST.. Day-ahead (netted), Product instances = One active product. Day-ahead (netted), Gas delivery = Delivery obligations are netted. Daily, Trading window = A gas day product that is available for trading between 2 and 7 days prior to the delivery gas day.
