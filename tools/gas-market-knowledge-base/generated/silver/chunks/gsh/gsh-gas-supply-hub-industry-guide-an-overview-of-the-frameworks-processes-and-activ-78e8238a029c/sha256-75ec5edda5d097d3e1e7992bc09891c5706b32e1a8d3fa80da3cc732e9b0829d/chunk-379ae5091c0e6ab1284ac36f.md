- Authorised to enter OTC trades to AEMO for settlement
- Does not incur variable transaction fees as per the GSH Exchange fees schedule
Under this arrangement, the Broker Participant enters two 'netted' trades (i.e. stand on each side of the transaction) to ensure they have a net zero position and no gas delivery obligations. Trades are submitted to AEMO using the pre-match trade functionality and as such require the participants involved in the transaction to confirm the trade. The two Trading Participant trades are matched for delivery in AEMO's netting module as per the standard process (that is the non-broker parties involved in the trade may or may not be matched for delivery).
