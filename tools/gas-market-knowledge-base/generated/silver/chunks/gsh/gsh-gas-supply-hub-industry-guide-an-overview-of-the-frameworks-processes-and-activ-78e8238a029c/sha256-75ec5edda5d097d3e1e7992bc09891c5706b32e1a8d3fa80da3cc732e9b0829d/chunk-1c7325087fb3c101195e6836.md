Trading Participants submit an order for a product onto the exchange to initiate the trading process.  Trading Participants, once logged into the ETS, have direct access to submit orders onto the exchange:
1. Trading Participants select the product they would like to trade and then complete the required information.
2. The order is validated by the Trading Systems
3. Confirmation of the order submission is provided on screen and through a market report.
All orders are anonymous within the exchange until a transaction is formed and the counterparty for delivery is communicated.
