The roles of governance bodies within the national energy market framework are:
- AEMO:
- -Establish and modify the Exchange Agreement.
- -Operation of the Gas Supply Hub.
- -The Exchange Agreement permits the Operator to give a default notice to a member for defined 'default events', such as financial default or insolvency events.
- -Investigate unusual market outcomes or behaviour and refer breaches of market conduct to the AER for further action.
