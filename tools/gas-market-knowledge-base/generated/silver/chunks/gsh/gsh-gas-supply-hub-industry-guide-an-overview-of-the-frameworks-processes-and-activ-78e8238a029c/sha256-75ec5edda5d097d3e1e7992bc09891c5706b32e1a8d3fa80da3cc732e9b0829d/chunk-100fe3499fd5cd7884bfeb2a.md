The Trading Participant must indicate if the order is to buy or sell the product:
- Buy order (Bid) indicates that the participant is willing to receipt gas at the Trading Location at a price equal to or below their order price.
- Sell order (Offer) indicates that the participant is willing to deliver gas to the Trading Location at a price equal to or above their order price.
