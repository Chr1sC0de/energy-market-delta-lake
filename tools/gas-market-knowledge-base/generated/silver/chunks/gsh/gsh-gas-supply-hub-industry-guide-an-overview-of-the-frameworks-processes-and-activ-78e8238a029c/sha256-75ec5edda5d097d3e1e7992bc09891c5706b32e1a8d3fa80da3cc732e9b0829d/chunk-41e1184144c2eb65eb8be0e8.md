Access to the capacity listing service is through the ETS.  Both viewing and trading participants can use the ETS to list capacity. The capacity listings are available on a trading screen adjacent to the gas commodity trading screens to help participants identify gas commodity and transportation trading opportunities.
Once a participant has submitted their capacity listing it is made available for other participants to view through the ETS as per the example shown below. The capacity listing is displayed up until the expiry date submitted by the listing party.
