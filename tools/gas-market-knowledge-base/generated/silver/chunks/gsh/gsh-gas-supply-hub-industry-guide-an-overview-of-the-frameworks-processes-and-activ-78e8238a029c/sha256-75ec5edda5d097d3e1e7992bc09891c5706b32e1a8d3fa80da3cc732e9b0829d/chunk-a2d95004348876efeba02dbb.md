An organisation intending to participate in the Gas Supply Hub can obtain the Membership Agreement and registration form from the registration page of the AEMO website.
The registration process is summarised in the following four steps:
1. Execute the Membership Agreement.
Applicants should conduct a legal review of the Exchange Agreement prior to executing the Membership Agreement. Once the Membership Agreement has been executed, the organisation becomes a member of the exchange and is bound by the terms of the Exchange Agreement.
2. Complete the registration form.
2. -This step requires information relating to the organisation, participation category, the number of user accounts required for the Exchange Trading System, MarketNet connection, and contact details of the person submitting the registration.
More detailed information on how to register is available in the Gas Supply Hub Registration Guide.
3. Submit the Membership Agreement and registration form.
