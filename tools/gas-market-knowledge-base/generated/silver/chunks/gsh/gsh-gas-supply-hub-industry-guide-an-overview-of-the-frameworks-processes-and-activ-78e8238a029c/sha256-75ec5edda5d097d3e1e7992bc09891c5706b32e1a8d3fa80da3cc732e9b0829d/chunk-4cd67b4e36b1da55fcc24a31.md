The transaction history of specific products can be viewed through the Deals Window of the ETS. The Deals Window shows details of the most recent 1,000 deals held in the ETS.  See the Trader User Guide for Exchange Trading System for details. The Deals Window functionality within Trayport can be used to manage a trading participant's trade book across multiple Products.
Note . Time viewed through Deal window and Order management screens is in computer local time.
