AEMO will suspend operation of the ETS (entire exchange or individual products) where it confirms that there is a problem with the trading system.
The suspension triggers include:
- The Trading System has been unavailable to Trading Participants for a period of 15 minutes or longer.
- Core functionality of the exchange isn't available:
2  AEMO Support Hub, at http://www.aemo.com.au/Contact-us.
- -Order submission,
- -Execution of transactions, or
- -Prudential validation of orders.
