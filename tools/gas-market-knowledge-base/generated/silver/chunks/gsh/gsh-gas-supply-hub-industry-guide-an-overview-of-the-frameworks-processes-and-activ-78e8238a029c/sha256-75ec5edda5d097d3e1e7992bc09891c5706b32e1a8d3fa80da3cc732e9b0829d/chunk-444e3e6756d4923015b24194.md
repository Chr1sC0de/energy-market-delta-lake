The Gas Producer is matched with parties that have complementing net buy positions to form the delivery schedule:
- Gas Producer must deliver 5TJ to Party B at Run 3.
- Gas Producer must deliver 5TJ to Party C at Run 7.
Figure 13 Matched delivery schedule
Each matched delivery obligation consists of a gas day, delivering participant (or seller), a receipting participant (or buyer), the gas delivery quantity and the delivery point at which gas must be delivered to.
The party to each matched delivery obligation is provided with a delivery schedule at the completion of the delivery netting process.
