Traders enter a spread product price and quantity when submitting an outright order.  When two outright spread product orders are matched, the exchange knows the difference between the price of the two trading locations but not the price at which the trades should be set. As such, a reference price is used to set the transaction prices for the Moomba and Wallumbilla trades.
The reference price methodology uses the price of the most recent (on the trading day) transaction at either trading location if available.  If no trades have occurred on the trading day then the default reference price (Wallumbilla Benchmark Price - see section 12) is used in the calculation.
