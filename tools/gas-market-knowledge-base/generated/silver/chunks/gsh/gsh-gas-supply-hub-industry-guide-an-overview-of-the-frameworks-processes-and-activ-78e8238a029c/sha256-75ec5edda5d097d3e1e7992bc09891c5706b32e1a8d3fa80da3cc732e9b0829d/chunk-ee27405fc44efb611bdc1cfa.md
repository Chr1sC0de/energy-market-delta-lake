A member's prudential exposure is estimated based on the lates t transactions, gas delivery information, active buy orders and reallocations.  The prudential exposure is the aggregate of calculated amounts for:
- Outstanding Amounts
- Forward Trading Exposure
- Forward Reallocation Amount
