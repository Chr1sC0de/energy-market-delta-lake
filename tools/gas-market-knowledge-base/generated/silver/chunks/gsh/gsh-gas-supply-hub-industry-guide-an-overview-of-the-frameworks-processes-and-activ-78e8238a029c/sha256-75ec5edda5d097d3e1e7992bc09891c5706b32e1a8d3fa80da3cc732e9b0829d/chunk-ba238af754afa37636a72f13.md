Normal supply hub processes, such as settlement and delivery confirmation, continue during trading suspension to the extent possible.
The process for suspending the market is as follows:
1. AEMO determines that a trigger event has occurred.
2. AEMO suspends operation of the ETS operation and sets the status of all orders to ' with held' .
3. AEMO issues a notice to participants of the trading suspension; providing as much notification and information on the expected duration as possible.
