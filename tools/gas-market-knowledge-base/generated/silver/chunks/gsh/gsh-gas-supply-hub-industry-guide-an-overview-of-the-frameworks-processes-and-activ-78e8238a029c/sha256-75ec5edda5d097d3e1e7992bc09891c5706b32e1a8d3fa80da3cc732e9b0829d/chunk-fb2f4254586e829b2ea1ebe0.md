Product specifications for Spread Products are set out in Schedules 14, 15 and 16 of the Exchange Agreement. The specifications define the physical gas products that are linked together by a spread product (for example, day-ahead Wallumbilla and MSP products). The specifications also define the trading window, default delivery points, default reference price, parcel size and price limits.
