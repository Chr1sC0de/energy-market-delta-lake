- (a) A party waives a right under this agreement only by written notice that it waives that right.  A waiver is limited to a right of the party giving the waiver and the specific instance to which it relates and to the specific purpose for which it is given.
- (b) No failure, or delay, in exercising any right, power or remedy by a party operates as a waiver.  A single or partial exercise of any right, power or remedy does not preclude any other or further exercise of that or any other right, power or remedy.
