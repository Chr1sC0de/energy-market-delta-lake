- (b) The Affected Party must:
- (i) as soon as reasonably practicable, provide any additional information reasonably requested by a person notified under paragraph (a) in respect of the event or circumstance of Force Majeure;
- (ii) use its reasonable endeavours to remove or overcome the event or circumstance of Force Majeure; and
- (iii) give notice to each party notified under paragraph (a) of the cessation of the event or circumstance giving rise to Force Majeure.
- (c) If the Affected Party is the Operator, the Operator must:
- (i) promptly publish on its website as much information as is reasonably practicable about the event or circumstance giving rise to the Force Majeure, the expected duration of that event and the cessation of that event; and
- (ii) use its reasonable endeavours to remove or overcome the event or circumstance of Force Majeure.
