Daily (D), Trading Window = For Gas Day D, Trading Hours on each of Gas Day D-7 to Gas Day D-2.. Weekly (W), Delivery Period = Seven consecutive Gas Days, beginning and ending at Gas Day commencement on a Sunday.. Weekly (W), Trading Window = For a Delivery Period commencing on Gas Day D, Trading Hours on each Gas Day commencing 4 weeks prior to Gas Day D and ending on D-2.. Monthly (M), Delivery Period = Calendar month consecutive Gas Days, beginning at Gas Day commencement on the first day of the calendar month and ending at Gas Day commencement on the first day of the immediately following calendar month.. Monthly (M), Trading Window = For a Delivery Period commencing on Gas Day D, Trading Hours on each Gas Day commencing 3 calendar months and 1 calendar day prior to the calendar month in which Gas Day D falls and ending on D-2 prior the commencement of the calendar month in which Gas Day D falls.
