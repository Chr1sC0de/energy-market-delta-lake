- (a) The remainder of this clause 14.7 (other than this paragraph (a)), ceases to apply if Exemption Regulations cease to be in effect.  The term ' Exemption Regulations ' means regulations made under the Corporations Act or other regulatory instrument that have the effect that neither the Operator nor Trading Participants are required to obtain a licence under Chapter 7 of the Corporations Act to exercise their rights and perform their obligations under this agreement in respect of Transactions for Physical Gas Products where Delivery Netting applies. The term Exemption Regulations is taken to include regulations 7.6.01(1)(z), 7.10.03 and 9.12.05 in the Corporations Regulations 2001 as in force on 1 December 2016.
- (b) In respect of any Gas Day and a particular Trading Location, subject to clause 14.7.3 and clause 13.2(a3), Delivery Netting applies to Transactions:
- (i) in respect of Products to which Delivery Netting is specified to apply in the Product Specification;
- (ii) for delivery at that Delivery Point and for which the Delivery Period includes, or comprises, that Gas Day.
