- (A) make available for delivery on that Gas Day at the Swap Receipt Point the Order Quantity, and must submit accurate nominations for that purpose; and
- (B) accept on that Gas Day at the Swap Delivery Point the Order Quantity, and must submit accurate nominations for that purpose;
- (ii) each Seller must:
- (A) accept on that Gas Day at the Swap Receipt Point the Order Quantity and must submit accurate nominations for that purpose; and
- (B) make available for delivery at the Swap Delivery Point the Order Quantity and must submit accurate nominations for that purpose.
Note :  In this agreement, the term 'Delivery Point' includes the Swap Receipt Point and the Swap Delivery Point. The Buyer of the Location Swap is the Delivering Participant at the Swap Receipt Point and the Receiving Participant at the Swap Delivery Point.  The Seller of the Location Swap is the Receiving Participant at the Swap Receipt Point and the Delivering Participant at the Swap Delivery Point.
