- (a) An Order is open and will be displayed in the Trading System when:
- (i) the Order has been validated and not rejected;
- (ii) the Order is made available for viewing by Trading Participants in the Trading System; and
- (iii) none of the circumstances in paragraph (b) applies to the Order.
- (b) The Operator will cease to display an Order when:
- (i) the Order has been validly accepted; or
- (ii) the Order has been cancelled or withdrawn,
- (iii) whichever occurs first.
- (c) The Operator is only required to change the information displayed in the Trading System during Trading Hours.
