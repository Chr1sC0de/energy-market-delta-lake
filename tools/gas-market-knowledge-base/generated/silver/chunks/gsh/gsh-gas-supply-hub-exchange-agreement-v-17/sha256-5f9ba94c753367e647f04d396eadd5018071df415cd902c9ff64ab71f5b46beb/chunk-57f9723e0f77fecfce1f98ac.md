- (a) Each Member must be registered as a Trading Participant, a Viewing Participant, a Reallocation Participant or a Broker Participant.
- (b) Subject to subclause (d) and (e), the same Member may be registered more than once in any or all participant categories.
- (c) A Trading Participant may on registration nominate to trade:
- (i) both Physical Gas and Physical Capacity; or
- (ii) Physical Capacity, and may change that nomination from time to time.
- (d) A Member may only be registered as a Trading Participant or a Broker Participant, not both.
- (e) A Member that is registered as a Broker Participant may not be registered as a Trading Participant, a Viewing Participant or a Reallocation Participant.
