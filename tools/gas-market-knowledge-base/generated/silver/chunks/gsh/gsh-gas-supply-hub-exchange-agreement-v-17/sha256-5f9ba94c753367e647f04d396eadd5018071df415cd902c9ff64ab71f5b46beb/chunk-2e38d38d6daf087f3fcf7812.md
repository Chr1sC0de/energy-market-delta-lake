- (a) This clause 20.6.3 applies for a relevant Gas Day and Capacity Type only if the Offset Quantity is equal to TCQS.  TCQS and TCQB have the meanings set out in clause 20.6.2(a) when used in this clause.
- (b) The Operator must calculate a capacity sell reduction factor (CSRF) for the Gas Day and Capacity Type as follows:
CSRF = Close Out Capacity Quantity / TCQB.
