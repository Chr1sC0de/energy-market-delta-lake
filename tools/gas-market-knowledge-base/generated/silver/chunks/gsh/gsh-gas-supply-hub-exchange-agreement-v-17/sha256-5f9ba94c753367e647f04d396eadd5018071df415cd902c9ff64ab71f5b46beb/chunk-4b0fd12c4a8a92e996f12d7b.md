On-The-Day (OTD), Delivery Period = The Delivery Period starts at the Start Time and ends at the end of the Gas Day in which the Start Time falls. The Start Time is the start of the first full hour (being one o'clock, two o'clock and so on) which commences at least 60 minutes after the time the Transaction is confirmed by the Trading System.. On-The-Day (OTD), Trading Window = For Gas Day D, Trading Hours on that same Gas Day.. Day-Ahead (DA), Delivery Period = One Gas Day. Day-Ahead (DA), Trading Window = For Gas Day D, Trading Hours on D-1.. Daily (D), Delivery Period = One Gas Day. Daily (D), Trading Window = For Gas Day D, Trading Hours on each of Gas Day D-7 to Gas Day D-2.. Weekly (W), Delivery Period = Seven consecutive Gas Days, beginning and ending at Gas Day commencement on a Sunday.. Weekly (W), Trading Window = For a Delivery Period commencing on Gas Day D, Trading Hours on each Gas Day commencing 4 weeks prior to Gas Day D and ending on D-2..
