Each Member makes the representations in clause 23.3 to the Operator and each other Member at the time it enters into the Membership Agreement.  Each representation is deemed to be repeated by the Member on each date that a new Member enters into a Membership Agreement and on each date that a Transaction is concluded on the Exchange to which the Member is a party.
