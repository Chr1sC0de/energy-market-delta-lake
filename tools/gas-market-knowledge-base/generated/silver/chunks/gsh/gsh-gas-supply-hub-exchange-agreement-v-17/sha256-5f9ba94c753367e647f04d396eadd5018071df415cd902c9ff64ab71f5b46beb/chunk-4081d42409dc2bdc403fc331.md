This clause 14.5.2 does not apply to Physical Gas Transactions in respect of a Delivery Point at a gas storage facility.
Trading Participants must maintain deliveries and receipts of gas at Delivery Points as nearly as practical at a uniform hourly rate of flow during a Gas Day.
