13.7.2.. Last Trading Date, Definition = A date specified for a Product in its Product Specification, used to fix the last day on which a Product will be available for trading on the Exchange.. matched, Definition = See clause 13.1.2.. Offer, Definition = For a Spread Product, represents an offer to enter into a Transaction for the Premium Product as a Seller and an offer to enter into a Transaction for the Base Product as a Buyer.. open, Definition = See clause 12.6.. Order Quantity, Definition = The number of Units of Commodity to be delivered during each Gas Day in the Delivery Period, as specified in the Order.. originating, Definition = In relation to an Order - see clause 13.7.6(a)(i).. Parcel Size, Definition = The minimum permitted increment in the size of an Order, expressed in Units of the Commodity, as specified in the Product Specification.. Premium Product, Definition = For a Spread Product, the Physical Gas Product designated as such in the applicable Product Specification.. Price, Definition = Price per Unit of Commodity (exclusive of GST), as specified in the Order.. Product Limit, Definition = A highest or lowest Price or Order
