A fixed fee is payable by each participant in accordance with their participation category.
Table 25: Participation fees

Participant Type, 1 = Annual Fee ($). Trading Participant, 1 = 14,500. Trading Participant Additional Licence, 1 = 5,500. Reallocation Participant, 1 = 9,000. Viewing Participant, 1 = 5,500
Fees for Market Participants (Trading Participants and Reallocation Participants) are billed monthly as part of the regular Billing Period settlement of transactions.  Whereas the participation fee for a Viewing Participant is paid upfront on an annual basis.
SP 4.2.1
MPF(p,d*) = ATF / 12
+ Number of additional licences x ALF / 12
+ ARF / 12
In the example, Participant One is a Trading Participant and does not have any additional licences.
End to End Example
