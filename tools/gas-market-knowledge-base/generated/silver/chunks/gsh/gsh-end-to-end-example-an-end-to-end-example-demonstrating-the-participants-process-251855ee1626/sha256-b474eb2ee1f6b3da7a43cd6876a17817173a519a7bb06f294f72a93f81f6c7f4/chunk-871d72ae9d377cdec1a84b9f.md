The Reallocation Amount debited to the Debit Participant and credited to the Credit Participant is the product of the Energy Reallocation Quantity (GJ/day) and the Applicable Reference Price ($/GJ).
The Reallocation Request for Energy Reallocations must specify a Trading Location, which will determine the Applicable Reference Price.
