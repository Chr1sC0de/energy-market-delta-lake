Net Position
The Net Position is calculated for each Gas Day and Trading Location as the difference between a participant's aggregate buy and sell transactions.
SP 5.2.3.a: NTQ(m,d,l) =  c  t' TQ(t',d,c,l) - c  t'' TQ(t'',d,c,l)
Offset Position
The Offset Quantity is also calculated for each Gas Day and Trading Location and is the quantity of buy transactions offset by sell transactions.

The buy quantity includes active orders but the sell quantity only includes transactions in both the Net Quantity and Offset Quantity calculations.
