If Delivery Netting applies to a Transaction, then the Delivery Price (DP) is equal to the Average Price (AP) for that Gas Day and Trading Location.  If Delivery Netting does not apply to a Transaction, DP is equal to the Transaction Price (TP) associated with the gas delivery obligation.
