A reallocation is a financial arrangement between two Market Participants and AEMO to transfer settlement commitments between the Market Participants.  The Gas Supply Hub supports Dollar Reallocations and Energy Reallocations.
The Credit Participant's settlement amount has a negative sign (offsetting a pre-existing payment obligation) and the settlement amount of the Debit Participant has a positive sign.
The settlement amount is determined separately for each Gas Day of the Reallocation.  If a Reallocation covers multiple Billing Periods then the Reallocation Amount for each Gas Day is settled for the Billing Period in which that Gas Day falls.
