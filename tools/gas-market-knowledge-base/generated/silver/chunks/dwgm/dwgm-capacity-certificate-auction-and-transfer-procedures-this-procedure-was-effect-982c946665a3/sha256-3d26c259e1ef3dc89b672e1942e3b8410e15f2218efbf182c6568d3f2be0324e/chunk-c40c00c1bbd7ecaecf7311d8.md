Table 2 Transitional Capacity Release Schedule

January 2023 - June 2023, Auction Quantity = 80% of the CC Modelled Capacity for the auction product. July 2023- December 2023, Auction Quantity = 60% of the CC Modelled Capacity for the auction product. January 2024 - June 2024, Auction Quantity = 45% of the CC Modelled Capacity for the auction product. July 2024 - December 2024, Auction Quantity = 30% of the CC Modelled Capacity for the auction product. January 2025 - June 2025, Auction Quantity = 15% of the CC Modelled Capacity for the auction product. July 2025 - December 2025, Auction Quantity = 0% of the Modelled Capacity for the auction product
