(a), 1 = AEMO may suspend the operation of the Auction Platform to undertake maintenance and upgrading at any time.. (b), 1 = AEMO must use its reasonable endeavours to conduct maintenance and upgrading of the Auction Platform at times when in its reasonable opinion the impact on the capacity certificates auction will be minimised.. (c), 1 = AEMO must give auction participants as much notice as reasonably practicable of any period during which the Auction Platform will be unavailable as a result of any maintenance or upgrade.
