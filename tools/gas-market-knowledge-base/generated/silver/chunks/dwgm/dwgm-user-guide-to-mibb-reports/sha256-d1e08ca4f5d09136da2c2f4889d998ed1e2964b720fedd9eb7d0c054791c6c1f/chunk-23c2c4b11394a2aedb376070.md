A report is produced each time an operational schedule (OS) is approved by AEMO. Therefore, it is expected that each day there will be at least 9 reports issued consisting of:
- l 5 reports for the standard current gas day schedules
- l 3 reports for the standard 1 day ahead schedules
- l 1 report for the standard 2 days ahead schedule.
