This a public report containing ancillary payments from the beginning of the previous month and is produced no later than the third business day after the gas day (D+3).
There are a number of participant specific reports and public reports relating to ancillary payments, in particular:
- l "INT116 - Participant Specific Ancillary Payments Reports Day + 3" on page 119
- l "INT116a - Participant Specific Estimated Ancillary Payments Report" on page 42
- l "INT116b - Participant Specific Ancillary Payments" on page 120
- l "INT117b - Public Ancillary Payments Report (Day+1)" above
The ancillary payment amount can be positive or negative depending on the total ancillary payment for the schedule (if it is in credit or debit).
