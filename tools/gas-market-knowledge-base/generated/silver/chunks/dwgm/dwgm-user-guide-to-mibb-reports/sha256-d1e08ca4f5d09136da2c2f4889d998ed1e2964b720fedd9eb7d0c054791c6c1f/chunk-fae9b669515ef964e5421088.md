This report provides the hourly calorific value of gas delivered for each heating value zone in Victoria.
This information allows AEMO and other Market participant to convert the volumetric measurements taken at interval meters into units of energy for various purposes including:
- l operation of the gas system
- l settlement of the wholesale market
- l billing of interval metered retail customers.
