Once a ROLR event has occurred AEMO is to provide to the ROLR/s, a list of MIRNS and associated details for which they are to become the FRO.
The file will contain some records that have incomplete data as AEMO will add the new sites 'won' by the 'suspended' retailer subsequent to their last monthly update of transaction. The complete data will be the list of MIRNs and customer details sent by the failed retailer on a monthly basis. The incomplete data will be CATS transfers won by FBR (Failing Business Retailer) on or after suspension date. Since AEMO does not keep records of customer details like postal addresses, the CATS transfer will not have customer details.
