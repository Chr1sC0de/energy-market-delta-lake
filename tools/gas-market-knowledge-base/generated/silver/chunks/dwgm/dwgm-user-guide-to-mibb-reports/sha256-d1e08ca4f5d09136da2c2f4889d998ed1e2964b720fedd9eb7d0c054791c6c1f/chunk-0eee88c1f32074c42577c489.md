All columns used to display date or time information should comply with the following.
All date and time fields will be generated and reported as string values to provide clearer understanding and to prevent incorrect interpretation of dates by applications such as Excel.
The format for the date portion of the field will be dd Mon yyyy. This format has been chosen to clearly identify the month component of the date.
The format for the time portion of the field will be hh:mi:ss. All times are in Australian Eastern Standard Time (AEST) and do not change with daylight savings.
All times will use a 24-hour clock unless the column name includes _ampm, in which case a 12-hour clock with appropriate identifier will be displayed hh:mi:ss AMPM
