Each report contains the market prices for the current gas day and the estimated prices are for the following two days. Hence, each gas day will produce reports for the:
- l actual market price for the current gas day after each market schedule is run
- l estimated market prices for 1 day ahead and 2 days ahead.
The" Demand Type Name" typically contains three types of demand:
- l Normal
- l 10%
- l 90%.
Each report contains the:
- l demand type
- l the forecast market price excluding GST
- l transmission group identifier which is linked to the related operational schedule
- l market schedule identifier
- l transmission schedule identifier for prices
- l gas date
- l approved date and time
- l date and time when the report was produced
