Market participant can expect to see 2 months of UAFG adjusted metering data where the energy is summated by Distribution Network Zone.
Each report contains the:
- l participant organisation identifier
- l gas date
- l time interval (which shows each hour in the gas day, where 1 = 6:00 AM to 7:00 AM, 2 = 7:00 AM to 8:00 AM, until the 24th hour)
- l distributor identity
- l the total energy in gigajoules (with UAFG adjustment) by distributor for that Market participant
- l date and time when the report was produced
