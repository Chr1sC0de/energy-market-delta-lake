A report is produced each time an operational schedule (OS) or pricing schedule (PS) is approved by AEMO. Therefore, it is expected that each day there will be at least 9 of these reports issued, with any additional ad hoc schedules also triggering this report:
- l 5 being for the standard current gas day schedules
- l 3 being for the standard 1-day ahead schedules
- l 1 being for the standard 2 days ahead schedule
