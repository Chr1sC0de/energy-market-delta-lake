This report is a basic compliance report for ROLR (Retailer of Last Resort).
This is a Market participant specific report issued monthly after transfer has completed and MR validation completed. This report is sent to all retailers participating in the Retail VIC Gas Market and the Retail QLD Gas Market.
Chapter 6 of the Victorian Retail Market Procedures describes the various ROLR obligations.
Chapter 9 of the Queensland Retail Market Procedures describes the various ROLR obligations.
