This report contains information on group net flow point constraints (NFTCs) pertaining to the DTS. Grouped net flow points are points in the DTS where multiple injections and withdrawals can occur.
This report contains constraints for a group of two or more pipeline systems where the flow is in the same direction.
ZFTCs are part of the configuration of the network that can be manually changed by the AEMO Schedulers, and form one of the inputs to the schedule generation process.
Traders can use this information to understand the pipeline-based restrictions that constrain their ability to offer gas to the market on a given day in the reporting window.
See INT112b Net Flow Transportation Constaints.
