This report is a public version of INT116b. It shows the actual ancillary payments for the gas market by taking into account the Actual Gas Injected Negative Offset (AGINO) and Actual Gas Withdrawal Negative Offset (AGWNO) quantities, as well as the proportion of injections used to support an uplift hedge.
Participants may wish to use this report to gauge their actual ancillary payments (from INT116b) in the context of the whole gas market.
Participants should note that although the AGINO and AGWNO are included in the calculations for this report, the meter data used for this purpose is provisional data that may change at settlement.
