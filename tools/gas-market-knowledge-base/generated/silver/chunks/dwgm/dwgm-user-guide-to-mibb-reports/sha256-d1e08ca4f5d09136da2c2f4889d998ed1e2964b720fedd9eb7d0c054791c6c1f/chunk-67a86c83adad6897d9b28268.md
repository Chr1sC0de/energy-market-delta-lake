Each report identifies each of the schedules run for the previous 7 gas days. That is, the INT108 report for the gas date of 11August will contain details of each schedule run in the period 4 to 10 August inclusive.
For each day in the reporting window, there will be at least 18 rows of data:
- l 5 being for the standard current gas day operational schedules
- l 5 being for the standard current gas day pricing schedules
- l 3 being for the standard 1-day ahead operational schedules
- l 3 being for the standard 1-day ahead pricing schedules
- l 1 being for the standard 2 days ahead operational schedule.
- l 1 being for the standard 2 days ahead pricing schedule
For the INT108 for the gas date of 11-August, the schedules will have a gas_start_datetime ranging from 4-August to 12August (because on 10-August, a 2 day ahead schedule will be run with a gas_start_datetime of 12-August)
