This report should contain at least one month's worth of data.
Each report contains the:
- l participant organisation identifier
- l gas date
- l hourly time interval (where 1 refers to 6:00 AM to 7:00 AM interval, 2 is the 7:00 AM to 8:00 AM interval and so forth. So a gas day would contain 24 different hourly time intervals)
- l schedule number which indicates the schedule horizon where the imbalance had occurred (for example, schedule1 will refer to 6:00 AM to 6:00 AM and schedule2 will relate to 10:00 AM to 6:00 AM, and so forth)
- l imbalance injection in gigajoule
- l imbalance withdrawal in gigajoule
- l imbalance injection amount (excluding GST)
- l imbalance withdrawal amount (excluding GST)
- l market price (excluding GST)
- l date and time when the report was produced
