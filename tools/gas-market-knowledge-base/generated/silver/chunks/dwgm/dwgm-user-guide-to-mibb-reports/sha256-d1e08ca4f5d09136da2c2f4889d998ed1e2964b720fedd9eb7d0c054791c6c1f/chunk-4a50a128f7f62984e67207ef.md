It should be noted that not all basic meter reads are available at the time final settlements are produced. The most accurate version of this report occurs when the revision settlement is produced at the last business day of the 9th month after the end of the settlement period.
This report groups basic meters by their meter reading schedule and specifies the distributor, financially responsible organisation and distinct reading periods.
This report is a distributor or retailer specific report that is produced when settlement statements are issued at:
- l no later than month + 16 business days (provisional)
- l no later than month + 24 business days (final)
- l no later than last business day of the 9th month (revised)
This report is similar to VIC MIBB report INT469.
