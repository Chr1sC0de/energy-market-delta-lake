This report lists all the schedules that have been run for the previous 7 gas days. It provides transparency of AEMO demand forecasting and scheduling activities on the basis of inputs from Market participants and weather forecasts.
For instance, Participants may wish to rely on the information provided in this report for reconciliation of the scheduling process delivered by AEMO. The quantities and prices for each of the standard schedules in the reporting window are identified by the unique schedule identifiers (transmission_id and transmission_document_id) to assist with tracking the process across the day.
