Each report confirms the details of the accreditations recorded for the specific Market participant where the accreditation termination date is equal to or greater than the current date.
Each accreditation has a unique identifier and applies to a single MIRN (injection or withdrawal point).
Each row in the report contains accreditation details specific to each gas date that the accreditation applies.
