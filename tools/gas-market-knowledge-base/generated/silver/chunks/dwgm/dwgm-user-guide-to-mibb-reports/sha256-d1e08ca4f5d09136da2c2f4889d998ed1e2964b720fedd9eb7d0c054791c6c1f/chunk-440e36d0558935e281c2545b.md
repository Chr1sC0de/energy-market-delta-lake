This report is produced after the approval of each market schedule. The report has the actual price information of each schedule, as well as forecast process for the day ahead schedules published. Each of the day ahead schedules also have a sensitivity applied to the demand of either plus or minus 10%. This means that on any given gas day the report will be published:
- l 5 times for Day+0 after each of the 5 market schedules which reflects actual price that will apply for each scheduling horizon.
- l 3 times for Day+1 which includes estimated BoD price.
- l Once for Day+2 which includes estimated BoD price.
Participants can use this report to review the outcomes of the current day and to reflect on the options to adjust their positions for the coming days.
