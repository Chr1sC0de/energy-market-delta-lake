An INT235 report is triggered each time a schedule is approved. It contains details for both operational and market schedules.
The reporting window for INT235 includes:
- l the gas day date on which the report is being run (the 'current date')
- l 2 days prior to the current date
- l 2 days after the current date.
For each day in its reporting window, wherever possible INT235 will provide details of the:
- l last approved 2-day ahead operational schedule for the specified gas date
- l last approved 2-day ahead pricing schedule for the specified gas date
- l last approved 1-day ahead operational schedule for the specified gas date
- l last approved 1-day ahead pricing schedule for the specified gas date
- l first approved current gas day operational schedule for the specified gas date
- l first approved current gas day pricing schedule for the specified gas date.
- l last approved current gas day operational schedule for the specified gas date
- l last approved current gas day pricing schedule for the specified gas date.
