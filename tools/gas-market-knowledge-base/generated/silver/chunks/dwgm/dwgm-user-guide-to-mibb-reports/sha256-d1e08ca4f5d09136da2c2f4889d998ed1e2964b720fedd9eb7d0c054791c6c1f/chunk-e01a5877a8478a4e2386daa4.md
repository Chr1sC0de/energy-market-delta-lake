Distributors and Retailers can expect to see 2 months worth of aggregated metering data where the energy is summated by Distribution Region by withdrawal zone.
Each report contains the:
- l network name
- l participant organisation identifier
- l gas date
- l distributor name
- l total energy in gigajoules by distributor by withdrawal zone for that Market participant
- l date and time the report was produced
