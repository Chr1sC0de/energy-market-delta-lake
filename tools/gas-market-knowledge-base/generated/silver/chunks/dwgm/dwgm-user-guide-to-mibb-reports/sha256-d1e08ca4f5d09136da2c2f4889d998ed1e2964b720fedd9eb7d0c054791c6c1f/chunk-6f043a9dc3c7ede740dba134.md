Each report contains the:
- l statement version identifier
- l gas date
- l total imbalance payments for the gas day (across all scheduling horizons)
- l total deviation payments for the gas day (across all scheduling horizons)
- l linepack account payment (i.e. a credit or debit amount to AEMO's linepack account)
- l system net withdrawals
- l total Market participant net withdrawals
- l date and time when the report was produced
