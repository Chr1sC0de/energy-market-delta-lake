This report shows the basic meter reads for the month as provided to AEMO by the Distribution Network. Market participants may wish to use this report to review the energy associated with a MIRN and use it to reconcile their settlement invoices.
Note this report is produced monthly but references data that is three months in the past. This is to ensure most the meter reads that are to be used in a revision statement are available from the Distribution Network.
