- l gas date
- l withdrawal and injection points
- l meter registration number or withdrawal zone name
- l trading interval (where 1= 6:00 AM to 7:00 AM, 2= 7:00 AM to 8:00 AM, until the 24th interval)
- l energy value
- l date and time when the report was produced
