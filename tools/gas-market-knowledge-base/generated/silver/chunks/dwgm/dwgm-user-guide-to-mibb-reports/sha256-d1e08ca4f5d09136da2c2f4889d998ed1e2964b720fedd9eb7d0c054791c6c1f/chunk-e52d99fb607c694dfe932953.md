Each report contains the:
- l distributor participant identifier
- l gas date
- l time interval (which shows each hour in the gas day, where 1 = 6:00 AM to 7:00 AM, 2 = 7:00 AM to 8:00 AM, until the 24th hour)
- l Market participant identity
- l total energy in gigajoules (with UAFG adjustment)
- l date and time when the report was produced
