- l The report is for allocating flows at Culcairn.
- l This report contains details of the latest approved operational and pricing schedules for:
- l the current gas day
- l 1 day in the future and
- l 2 gas days in the future (if an approved 2-day ahead schedule exists at the time the report is run).
- l Culcairn injection quantities reported for each hour will match with the corresponding hourly quantity reported in the INT145 for the same schedule and injection point.
- l For the purposes of INT252:
- l 'type' will always contain the value 'a'
- l a 'flag' value of 'MS' refers to a Pricing Schedule (previously termed a Market Schedule).
