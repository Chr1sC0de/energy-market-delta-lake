This report is to list the Net System Load (NSL) for each distribution area for a rolling 3 year period. This report may be used to validate consumed energy produced in INT169. Section 2.8.4 of the Victorian Retail Market Procedures AEMO's obligation to publish the NSL and Attachment 6 of the Victorian Retail Market Procedures set out how AEMO calculates the NSL.
