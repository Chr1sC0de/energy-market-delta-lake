Each report contains data for a 14-day rolling period where each gas day will consist of the associated gas date and prices for the 5 pricing schedules and the 3 calculated reference prices (all prices excluding GST):
- l 6:00 AM
- l 10:00 AM
- l 2:00 PM
- l 6:00 PM
- l 10:00 PM
- l imbalance weighted average price
- l injection imbalance weighted average price
- l withdrawal imbalance weighted average price
- l the date and time when the report was produced
