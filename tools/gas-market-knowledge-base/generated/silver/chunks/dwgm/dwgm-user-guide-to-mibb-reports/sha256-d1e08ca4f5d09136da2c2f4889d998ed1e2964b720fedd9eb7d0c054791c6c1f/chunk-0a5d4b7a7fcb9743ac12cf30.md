This report will be published on the following gas day (D+1) if a transfer of capacity certificates has been approved on the previous gas day. The report will be published at midnight. If no transfer of capacity certificates has been approved, the report will not be published.
The report provides information the amount of CC transferred for a CC zone and CC product period to or from a Market participant.
