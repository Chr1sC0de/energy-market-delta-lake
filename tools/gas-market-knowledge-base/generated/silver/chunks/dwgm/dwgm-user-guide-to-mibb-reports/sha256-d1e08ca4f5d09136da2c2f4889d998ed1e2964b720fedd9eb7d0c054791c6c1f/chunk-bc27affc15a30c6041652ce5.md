A report is produced on the third business day after the gas date (Day+3). It is also produced at settlement on:
- l Month + 7 business days
- l Month + 18 business days
- l Month + 118 business days
The ancillary payment amount can be positive or negative depending if the participant organisation is in credit or debit.
There are a number of participant specific reports and public reports relating to ancillary payments, they include:
- l "INT116 - Participant Specific Ancillary Payments Reports Day + 3" on page 119
- l "INT116a - Participant Specific Estimated Ancillary Payments Report" on page 42
- l "INT117a - Public Estimated Ancillary Payments" on page 43
- l "INT117b - Public Ancillary Payments Report (Day+1)" on the next page
