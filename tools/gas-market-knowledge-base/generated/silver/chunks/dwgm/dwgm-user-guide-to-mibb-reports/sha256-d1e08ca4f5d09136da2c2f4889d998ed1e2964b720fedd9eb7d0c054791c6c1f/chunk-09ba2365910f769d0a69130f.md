One INT124 report is produced each gas day. It has a reporting window of the previous 7 gas days. That is, the INT124 report for the gas date of 11-August will contain details of each schedule run in the period 4 to 10 August inclusive.
For each gas day within the reporting window, the last approved operational schedule for the day is identified.
A row in the report will detail the hourly quantities that an Market participant was scheduled to inject at Longford through the specified schedule. Each row will provide details for a different Market participant.
