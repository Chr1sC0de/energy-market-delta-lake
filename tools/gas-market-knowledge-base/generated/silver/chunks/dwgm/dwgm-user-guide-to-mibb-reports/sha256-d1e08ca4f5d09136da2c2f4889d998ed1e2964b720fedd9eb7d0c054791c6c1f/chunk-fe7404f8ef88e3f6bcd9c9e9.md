Each report contains the:
- l Settlement statement version identifier
- l National metering identifier
- l Gas date
- l Time interval (where 1 refers to 6:00 AM to 7:00 AM, 2 refers to 7:00 AM to 8:00 AM, up until 24 intervals.)
- l Hourly energy amount in gigajoules
- l Unaccounted for gas adjusted hourly energy in gigajoules
- l Suality description (see table below)
- l Eettlement category type
- l Energy in percentage (percentage allocation for the mirn)
- l Date and time when report was produced
