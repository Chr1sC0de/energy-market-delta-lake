This report provides information required under 320(2)(i) and 320(3)(a) of the NGR.
It provides a view of the amount of gas that is flowing in each of the withdrawal zones, and in the network overall, on a given day. It therefore contributes to data on mid- to long-term trends for planning and load forecasting purposes.
