It should be noted that not all basic meter reads are available at the time final settlements are produced. The most accurate version of this report occurs when the revision settlement is produced at 118 business days after the month.
This report groups basic meters by their meter reading schedule and specifies the distributor, financially responsible organisation and distinct reading periods.
This report is a Market participant specific report which is produced when settlement statements are issued at:
- l month+ 7 business days (preliminary)
- l month+ 18 business days (final)
- l month+ 118 business days (revised)
This report relates to the Declared Transmission System (DTS) Network only.
