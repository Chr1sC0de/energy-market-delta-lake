Each report contains the:
- l statement version identifier
- l participant organisation identifier
- l participant organisation name
- l gas date
- l schedule number related to the scheduling horizon (where schedule1 will refer to 6:00 AM to 6:00 AM and schedule2 will relate to 10:00 AM to 6:00 AM, and so forth)
- l surprise uplift payment for a schedule
- l congestion uplift payment for a schedule
- l common uplift payment due to Transmission System Service Provider exceedence for a schedule
- l common uplift payment due to AEMO over-ride for a schedule
- l common residual uplift payment for a schedule
- l date and time when the report was produced
