Each curtailed MIRN is provided an hourly restriction quantity representing AEMO's expectation of the hourly reduction profile (maximum demand from a Tariff D site shutting down).
The hourly restriction quantity is used by AEMO to determine survivability time for the Victorian Declared Transmission System.
If possible a curtailed MIRN should seek to shut down plant and equipment at a faster rate than indicated by the hourly restriction quantity.
