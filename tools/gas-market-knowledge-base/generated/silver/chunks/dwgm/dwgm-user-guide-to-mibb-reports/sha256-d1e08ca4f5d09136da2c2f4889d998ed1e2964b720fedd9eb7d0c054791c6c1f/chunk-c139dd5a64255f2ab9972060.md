The statement version ID will appear empty as it is not linked to a settlement statement.
Each report contains the:
- l participant organisation identifier
- l statement version id (which will appear as null)
- l gas date
- l schedule interval (indicating 1 to 5 when the deviation occurred, where 1 refers to 6:00 AM to 10:00 AM, 2 will relate to 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM, and so forth)
- l deviation injection in gigajoules
- l deviation withdrawal in gigajoules
- l deviation injection amount (excluding GST)
- l deviation withdrawal amount (excluding GST)
- l deviation price (excluding GST)
- l scheduled injection in gigajoules
- l actual injection in gigajoules
- l scheduled withdrawal in gigajoules
- l actual withdrawal in gigajoules
- l date and time when the report was produced
