Market participants (MPs) can use this report to assess the effectiveness of their capacity certificate strategy for creating tiebreaking rights.
A report is produced each time an operational schedule (OS) is approved by AEMO; at least nine reports are expected to be issued daily:
- l 5 for the standard current gas day schedules (at 6:00 AM, 10:00 AM, 2:00 PM, 6:00 PM and 10:00 PM)
- l 3 for the standard 1-day ahead schedules (published at 8:00 AM, 4:00 PM and midnight)
- l 1 for the standard 2-day ahead schedule (published at midday)
