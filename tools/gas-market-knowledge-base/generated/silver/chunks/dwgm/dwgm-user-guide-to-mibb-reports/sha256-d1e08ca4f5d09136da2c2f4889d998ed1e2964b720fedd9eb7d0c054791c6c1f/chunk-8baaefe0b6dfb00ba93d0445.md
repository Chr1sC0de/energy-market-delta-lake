Each report contains the:
- l gas date
- l unaccounted for gas (for a 28-day rolling period which displays for each day the average uafg as a percentage over the last 28 days)
- l total scheduled injection in gigajoules (GJ)
- l sum of total scheduled controllable withdrawals, demand forecasts and AEMO's over-ride in gigajoules
- l total actual injection in GJ
- l total actual withdrawals in GJ
- l total Uplift payment ($)
- l total surprise uplift payment ($)
- l total congestion uplift payment ($)
- l total common uplift resulting from unallocated AEMO's demand forecast over-ride in $
- l total common uplift from exceedance of Transmission System Service Provider's liability limit in $
- l total residual common uplift payment ($)
