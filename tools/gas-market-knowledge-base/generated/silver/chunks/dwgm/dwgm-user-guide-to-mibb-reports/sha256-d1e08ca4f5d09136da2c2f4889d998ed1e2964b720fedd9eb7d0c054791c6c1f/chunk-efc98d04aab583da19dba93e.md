This is a public report containing ancillary payments from the beginning of the previous month and is produced after each schedule.
This report does not take into account AP Clawback. AP Clawback is a mechanism which recovers ancillary payments that have already been made to participants on the basis of a scheduled injection or withdrawal when those injections or withdrawals are de-scheduled in a later horizon.
There are a number of participant specific reports and public reports relating to ancillary payments, in particular:
- l "INT116 - Participant Specific Ancillary Payments Reports Day + 3" on page 119
- l "INT116a - Participant Specific Estimated Ancillary Payments Report" on the previous page
- l "INT116b - Participant Specific Ancillary Payments" on page 120
- l "INT117b - Public Ancillary Payments Report (Day+1)" on page 122
The ancillary payment amount can be positive or negative depending on the total estimated ancillary payment for the schedule (if it is in credit or debit).
