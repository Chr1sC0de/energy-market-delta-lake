Each report contains details of the:
- l latest approved schedule for the current gas day (which will change over the course of the day),
- l last approved operational schedule for each of the last 8 days prior to the current gas day
For each gas day date reported, a separate row will list the LNG usage for each Market participant at the LNG injection MIRN:
- l as an energy quantity in GJ
- l as a percentage of the total scheduled LNG injection quantity for the day
