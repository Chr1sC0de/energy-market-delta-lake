A report is produced after each schedule. It is only an estimate because it does not take into account
Actual Gas Injected Negative Offset (AGINO) and Actual Gas Withdrawal Negative Offset (AGWNO) quantities, as well as the proportion of injections actually used in support of an uplift hedge. To view a report that shows ancillary payments with AGINO and AGWNO quantities, see "INT116 - Participant Specific Ancillary Payments Reports Day + 3" on page 119.
Other reports relating to ancillary payments include:
- l "INT116b - Participant Specific Ancillary Payments" on page 120
- l "INT117a - Public Estimated Ancillary Payments" on the next page
- l "INT117b - Public Ancillary Payments Report (Day+1)" on page 122
This report does not take into account AP Clawback. AP Clawback is a mechanism which recovers ancillary payments that have already been made to participants on the basis of a scheduled injection or withdrawal when those injections or withdrawals are de-scheduled in a later scheduling horizon.
This report will show ancillary payments from the start of the previous month, produced after each schedule.
