This report contains information for each Market participant, that holds LNG stock, with a confidential report on:
- l their holdings on the current day
- l changes to their holdings over the previous 6 days (if any) and
- l forecast changes to their holdings over the next 5 days (if any).
Market participants can consider this information on their LNG holdings in forecasting their demand and for trading purposes.
