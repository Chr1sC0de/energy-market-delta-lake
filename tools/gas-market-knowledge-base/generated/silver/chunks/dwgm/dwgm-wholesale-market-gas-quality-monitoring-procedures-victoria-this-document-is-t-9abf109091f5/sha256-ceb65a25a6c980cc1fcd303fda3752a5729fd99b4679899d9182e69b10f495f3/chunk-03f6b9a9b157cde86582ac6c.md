Table 1 Glossary of terms
AEMO monitoring point, Definition = An AEMO monitoring point in these Procedures refers to system injection points , DTS monitoring points , and DDS transfer monitoring points . For the avoidance of doubt, gas quality monitoring arrangements for a monitoring point at a market injection point that is a DDS injection point is approved by the Distributor .. Becquerel (Bq), Definition = Number of nucleus decays per second in a given quantity of radioactive substance.. CTM, Definition = Custody Transfer Metering facility (CTM) is defined in the Wholesale Market Metering Procedures.. DDS, Definition = declared distribution system [Note only declared distribution systems that are directly connected to the DTS are covered by Part 19]. DTS, Definition = declared transmission system. ESV, Definition = Energy Safe Victoria. Gas Chromatograph (GC), Definition = Instrument used for measuring gas composition and calculating gas heating value.. gas composition data or GCD, Definition = Gas composition data (GCD) represents the measurement of gas for each gas quality specifications parameters at each gas quality monitoring system.
