This blend is used as the odour is familiar to the community. Other odorant blends and injection rates are acceptable if they also meet the standard gas quality specifications and Regulation requirements.
While odorant injection rates from 7 to 14 mg/m 3  of gas are acceptable, rates of up to 23 mg/m 3 (usually for a limited term associated with 'conditioning' new pipelines) are acceptable, provided that this rate of injection does not create 'nuisance' leak reporting.
