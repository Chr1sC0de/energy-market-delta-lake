As per rule 289E, testing and calibrations of the gas quality monitoring system must be conducted by the responsible gas quality monitoring provider . These tests may be either:
- (a) Conducted periodically as per the gas quality monitoring plan .
- (b) Requested on an ad hoc basis by AEMO.
The responsible gas quality monitoring provider must promptly provide AEMO with the result of gas quality sampling undertaken within the DTS and DDS by the responsible gas quality monitoring provider . AEMO may use this data to validate the heating value allocation model results meet the gas quality measurement uncertainty limits detailed in section 2.8.8.
The costs of testing are determined by rule 289E(5) and rule 289E(6). For the avoidance of doubt, AEMO's requests for testing are at the cost of the responsible gas quality monitoring provider .
