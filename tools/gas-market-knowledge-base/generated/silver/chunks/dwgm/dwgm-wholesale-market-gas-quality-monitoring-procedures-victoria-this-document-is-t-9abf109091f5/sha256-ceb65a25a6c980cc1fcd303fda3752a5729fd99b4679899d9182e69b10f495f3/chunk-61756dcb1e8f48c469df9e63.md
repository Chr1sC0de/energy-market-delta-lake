AEMO has applied the default maximum (50  C) and minimum temperatures (2  C), in consultation with the DTS SP for the gas quality specifications in relation to the DTS. Alternate temperatures may be agreed as part of the connection agreement with the DTS SP.
6  See section 4.3 of AS 4564 - 2020 - Specification for general purpose natural gas.
The temperature parameters are dictated by the connection agreement between the DTS SP and each connected party. The minimum and maximum temperatures are specified in the connection agreement are required for the safe operation of the DTS at that location.
