The responsible gas quality monitoring provider must periodically monitor the compliance of its gas quality monitoring system . This includes:
- (a) online monitoring, in accordance with section 2.9.4;
- (b) sampling and offline monitoring, in accordance with section 2.9.5;
- (c) gas quality monitoring system calibration, is to be maintained in accordance with section 2.8.8;
- (d) communications requirements are to be maintained in accordance with section 2.8.1 and the communication link reliability and availability in accordance with section 2.8.2,;
- (e) RTU and array data are to be maintained in accordance with section 2.8.4; and
- (f) any other components relevant to the accuracy of the gas quality monitoring system or integrity of the data outlined in this Procedure.
