The standard gas quality specifications (AS 4564) require the maximum water content to be determined by a dewpoint of 0  C at the highest MAOP in the relevant transmission system and in any case not to exceed more than 112.0 mg/m 3 .
For the purposes of gas injected at a system injection point , the relevant transmission pressure for determining water dewpoint is deemed to be 15,000 kPa. AEMO has determined that the water content of 73 mg/m 3  is the gas quality specifications limit for this parameter.
