AEMO notes that these new gas quality monitoring procedures incorporate primary gases (e.g. Hydrogen) that are not part of the standard gas quality specifications . Participants should be aware that a future update to the standard gas quality specifications (including an update to AS 4564 or modification by a regulatory instrument) to incorporate primary gases may require further consultation and updates to these Procedures.
AEMO has made reasonable endeavours to take into consideration potential changes to the Australian Standards based on information provided from industry and the Future Fuels CRC. However, AEMO notes that if the Australian Standards are changed and this is required to be AEMO
reflected within these Procedures, that gas quality monitoring systems may also need to change over time to reflect the updated standards.
The NGL and the NGR prevail over these Procedures to the extent of any inconsistency.
These Procedures may only be amended in accordance with Part 15B of the NGR.
