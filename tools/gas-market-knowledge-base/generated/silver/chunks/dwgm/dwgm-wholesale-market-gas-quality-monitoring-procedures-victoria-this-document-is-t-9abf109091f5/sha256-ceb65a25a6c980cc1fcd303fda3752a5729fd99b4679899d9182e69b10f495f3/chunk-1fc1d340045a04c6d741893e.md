For online monitoring at a gas quality monitoring system , the gas quality monitoring plan must consider the following items:
- (a) Type of gas quality monitoring equipment (e.g. GC, tuned laser, chilled surface, capacitance, etc.);
- (b) Gas quality data calculated in accordance with a standard listed in these Procedures, or otherwise agreed with AEMO;
- (c) Make and model of gas quality monitoring system equipment (including any fitted options and modifications);
- (d) Gas quality monitoring system equipment measurement range and expected uncertainty over that range as it complies with these Procedures;
- (e) Automated (e.g. GC) sampling frequency, reading update frequency and any averaging of output;
- (f) Monitoring point location (e.g. adjacent to meter, upstream of pressure reduction, downstream of filter etc.) and sample lag time to metering installation;
- (g) Any sample conditioning (e.g. heating, filters, liquids removal, drying, etc.);
