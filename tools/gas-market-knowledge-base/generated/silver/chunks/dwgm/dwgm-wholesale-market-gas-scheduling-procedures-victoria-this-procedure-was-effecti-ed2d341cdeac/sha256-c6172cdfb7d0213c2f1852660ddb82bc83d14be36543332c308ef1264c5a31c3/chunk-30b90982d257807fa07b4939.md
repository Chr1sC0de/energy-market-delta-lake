AEMO will review pricing schedules prior to publication to assess whether:
- (a) Market price , injections, controllable and uncontrollable withdrawals, and system LP match expectations, taking into account:
- (i) information from accreditation of quantities;
- (ii) SDPCs applied at system injection points and system withdrawal points ;
- (iii) DFPCs applied at bi-directional system injection points / system withdrawal points ;
- (iv) withdrawal NFTCs applied at a common location to system injection points and system withdrawal points ;
- (v) total demand forecast ;
- (vi) initial conditions; and
- (vii) the EoD LP target; and
- (b) the market price is consistent with bids that were scheduled.
AEMO may publish a market price sensitivity report linked with various demand forecast profiles.  The profiles correspond to ±10% deviation from the forecast demand, which may change from time to time.
