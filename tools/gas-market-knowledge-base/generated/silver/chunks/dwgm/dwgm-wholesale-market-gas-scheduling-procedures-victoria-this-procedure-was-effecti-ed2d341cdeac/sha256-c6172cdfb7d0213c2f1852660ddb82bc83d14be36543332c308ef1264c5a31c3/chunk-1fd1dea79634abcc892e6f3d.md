Where time does not permit, or AEMO considers commercial market responses are inadequate, AEMO will intervene in the operation of the Market by issuing directions to Market Participants under rule 343 of the Rules.
AEMO may declare an emergency in accordance with the Gas Emergency Protocol where it reasonably considers this step to be required to assist it with directly co-ordinating and managing action to alleviate the threat to system security .
