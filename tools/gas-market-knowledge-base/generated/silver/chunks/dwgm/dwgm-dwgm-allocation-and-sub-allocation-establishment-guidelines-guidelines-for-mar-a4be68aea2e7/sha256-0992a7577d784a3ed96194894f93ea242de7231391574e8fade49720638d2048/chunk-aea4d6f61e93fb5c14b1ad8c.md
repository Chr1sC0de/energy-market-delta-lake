AEMO, Task = Configures Production Gas Market Systems (configuration of AEMO meter registers, allocation agent software and MIBB Reports) and send market participant their new billing logical MIRN.. AEMO, No. of Business Days that task starts prior to the commencement of an allocation or sub-allocation = 4. AEMO, Task = Provides Market Participant their Billing MIRN detail again and request market participant to test and confirm they can view their physical injection / withdrawal point.. AEMO, No. of Business Days that task starts prior to the commencement of an allocation or sub-allocation = 2. Market Participant, Task = Confirm they can view their physical injection / withdrawal point through the Web Exchanger, for bidding purposes. This allows for the Market Participant to submit the 2 days ahead bids required under NGR 211(1).. Market Participant, No. of Business Days that task starts prior to the commencement of an allocation or sub-allocation = 2. AEMO, Task = Commences allocation / sub-allocation on the start date of the contract and publish allocated quantity in the Market Participant's MIBB reports..
