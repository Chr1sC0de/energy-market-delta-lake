AEMO, Task = Configures Gas Systems and MIBB reports and send billing logical MIRN number to the Market Participant and Allocation Agent ID to Allocation Agent.. AEMO, No. of Business Days that task starts prior to the commencement of allocation or sub-allocation = 10. AA, Task = Configures their allocation systems and interfaces with AEMO as per MIBB specification.. AA, No. of Business Days that task starts prior to the commencement of allocation or sub-allocation = 10. AEMO & AA, Task = Perform end-to-end testing confirming AA can retrieve all required reports from AEMO Test and Production MIBB, perform allocation and provide allocation to AEMO systems in the required format.. AEMO & AA, No. of Business Days that task starts prior to the commencement of allocation or sub-allocation = 6. AEMO, Task = Provides Market Participant with their Billing MIRN details again and request Market Participant to test and confirm they can view their physical injection / withdrawal point.. AEMO, No. of Business Days that task starts prior to the commencement of allocation or sub-allocation = 2. Market Participant, Task = Confirms they can view their Billing MIRN through the Web Exchanger, for bidding
