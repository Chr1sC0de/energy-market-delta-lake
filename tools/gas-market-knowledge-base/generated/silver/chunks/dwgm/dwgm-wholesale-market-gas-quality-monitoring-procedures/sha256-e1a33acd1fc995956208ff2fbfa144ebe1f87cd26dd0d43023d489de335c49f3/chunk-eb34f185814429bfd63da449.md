Any gas quality measurement exceeding the Notify Limit will result in AEMO sending a SWN to all Registered participants gas quality contacts in accordance with clause 2.6.5.
In the event of an offline test result exceeding the Notify Limit specified in these Procedures, the gas will be curtailed unless the responsible gas quality monitoring provider can provide an attestation of instrument error. This action is consistent with clause 2.6.3.1 for gas quality parameters measured online, as the excursion is expected to exceed the 12 continuous hours before the offline retesting could demonstrate a return to specification.
The responsible gas quality monitoring provider must:
- (a) Ensure the offline quality measurement test is conducted as soon as possible after the sample is taken;
- (b) Ensure that the offline quality measurement results are provided to AEMO as soon as they become available; and
- (c) If AEMO does not agree to accept off-specification gas (including at a modified flow rate ), cease the injection if the results confirm exceedance of the Notify Limit.
