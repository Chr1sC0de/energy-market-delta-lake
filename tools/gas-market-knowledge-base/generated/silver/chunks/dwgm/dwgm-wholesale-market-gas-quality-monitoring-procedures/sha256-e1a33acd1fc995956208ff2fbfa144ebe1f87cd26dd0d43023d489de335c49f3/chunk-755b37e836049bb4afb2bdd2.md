AEMO is provided the gas quality specifications data, as required by rule 289E(7) and 289G(3), by the responsible gas quality monitoring provider for each DDS injection point and publishes this data to the MIBB.
For the avoidance of doubt, AEMO is not required to undertake action in regard to the gas quality specifications at DDS injection points .
