- (d) Procedures for ensuring that the gas quality monitoring system including its data will remain free from interference;
- (e) Provision for storing all data relating to operation and calibration of the gas quality monitoring system ;
- (f) Provision of the maintenance plan for the gas quality monitoring system in accordance with the requirement of the maintenance planning procedures including:
- (i) Routine maintenance of gas quality monitoring equipment; and
- (ii) Checks and monitoring prior to returning to operation following plant maintenance/shut down; and
- (iii) Checks for abnormal operations or specific conditions that may lead to an incident.
- (g) Documented the risk assessment including methodology, assumptions, existing controls, actions taken, and outcomes achieved where a risk-based approach is adapted;
- (h) If required, provision for the measurement and monitoring of the alternate gas quality monitoring arrangements (e.g. online and offline monitoring), as required by clause 2.8.3 (which also includes for Hydrogen (clause 2.5.3) or for Biomethane (clause 2.4.4);
