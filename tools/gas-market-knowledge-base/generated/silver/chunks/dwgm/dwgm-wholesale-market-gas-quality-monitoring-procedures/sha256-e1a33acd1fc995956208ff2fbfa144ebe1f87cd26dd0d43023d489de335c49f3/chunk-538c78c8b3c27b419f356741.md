If the DWGM facility operator does not initiate self-curtailment, they may be directed by AEMO to cease injections under section 91BC of the NGL due to off-specification gas.
Injection must be ceased once exceeding the Notify Limit is confirmed. The limits and duration of offline testing conducted by DWGM facility operators can be found in Table 5.
Note that the historic basis for the Mitigate, and Curtail times is not applicable for offline testing due to the assumed retesting timeframes. This could be considered within the gas quality monitoring plan if retesting times can be demonstrated to be completed within a shorter timeframe.
Registered participants are required to carry out any necessary mitigation for off-specification gas appropriate for their organisations (including for Retailers their gas customers) based on independent expert advice. Curtail Limit steps may include notifications to specific categories of gas consumer.
Injection can be recommenced following that re-testing verifies that the gas is returned within the gas quality specification.
24 An equivalent of the high curtail limit for total sulphur applies.
