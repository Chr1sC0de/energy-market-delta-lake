Law, Definition = National Gas Law. NGR or Rules, Definition = National Gas Rules. Odour, Definition = AS 4564 uses the US spelling for odor in place of the Australian spelling of odour used in Australian legislation. Both words have the same meaning and are to be treated as interchangeable and not inferring a specific legislative instrument.. Odourant, Definition = AS 4564 uses the US spelling for odorant in place of the Australian spelling of odourant used in Australian legislation. Both words have the same meaning and are to be treated as interchangeable and not inferring a specific legislative instrument.. Odourisation, Definition = AS 4564 uses the US spelling for odorization in place of the Australian spelling of odourisation or odorisation used in Australian legislation. All three variations have the same meaning and are to be treated as interchangeable and not inferring a specific legislative instrument.
