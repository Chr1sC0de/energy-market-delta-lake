Any gas quality measurement exceeding the Mitigate Limit will result in AEMO sending a SWN to all Registered participants gas quality contacts in accordance with clause 2.6.5.
Exceedance of the Mitigate Limit is based on a duration of:
- (a) 15 continuous minutes, or
- (b) multiple separate off-specification gas excursions that occur for a total of 15-minutes in any 30minute period.
17 AEMO may direct the DWGM facility operator under section 91BC of the NGL to initiate curtailment of off-specification gas flows.
- (i) Where off-specification gas excursions are intermittent, an interval where the gas is within specification for more than 30 minutes, before again exceeding the Mitigate limit, defines the start of a separate excursion and further additional notifications are required.
