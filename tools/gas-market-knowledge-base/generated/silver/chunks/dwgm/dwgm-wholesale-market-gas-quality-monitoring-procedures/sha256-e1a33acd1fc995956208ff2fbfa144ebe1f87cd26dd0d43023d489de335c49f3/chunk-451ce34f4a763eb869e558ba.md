The standard gas quality specifications are defined in Part 19 of the NGR. The application of the standard gas quality specification is discussed in clause 1.3.
AEMO determines the application of the standard gas quality specifications in relation to system injection points, DTS monitoring points and DDS transfer monitoring points (collectively referred to as AEMO monitoring points).
The application of the standard gas quality specifications in relation to the DTS is shown in Table 3.
The units in the table are as follows:
- (a) Cubic metre (m 3 ) refers to a cubic metre of gas at standard conditions of 101.325 kPa absolute and 15°C.
- (b) Pressures are gauge unless otherwise specified.
AEMO has applied the normative values as required by the standard gas quality specifications . Other values required by AS 4564 are described in clause 2.4.
