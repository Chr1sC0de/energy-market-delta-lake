- (i) Documented calculation methodology for relevant gas quality parameters including C9+ tail characterisation and HCDP calculation; and the frequency of implementing extended tail analysis as required by clause 2.8.3.
The standard gas quality specifications set out that the testing of gas quality parameters must be at a frequency that ensures conformance at all times. Any additional site-specific requirements such as additional gas quality monitoring practices (see clause 2.8.3), biomethane facilities (see clause 2.4.4), etc must also be included.
The clauses below detail the requirements for inclusion in the gas quality monitoring plan .
