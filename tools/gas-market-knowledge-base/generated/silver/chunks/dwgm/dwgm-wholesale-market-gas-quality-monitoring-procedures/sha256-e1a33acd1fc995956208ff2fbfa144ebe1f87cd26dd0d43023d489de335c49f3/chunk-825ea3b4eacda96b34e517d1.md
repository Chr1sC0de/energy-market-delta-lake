Exceedance of the Curtail Limit is based on a duration of:
- (a) 15 continuous minutes, or
- (b) multiple separate off-specification gas excursions that occur for a total of 15-minutes in any 30minute period.
18 AEMO may direct the DWGM facility operator under section 91BC of the NGL to initiate curtailment of off-specification gas flows.
- (i) Where off-specification gas excursions are intermittent, an interval where the gas is within specification for more than 30 minutes, before again exceeding the Curtail Limit, defines the start of a separate excursion and further additional notifications are required.
