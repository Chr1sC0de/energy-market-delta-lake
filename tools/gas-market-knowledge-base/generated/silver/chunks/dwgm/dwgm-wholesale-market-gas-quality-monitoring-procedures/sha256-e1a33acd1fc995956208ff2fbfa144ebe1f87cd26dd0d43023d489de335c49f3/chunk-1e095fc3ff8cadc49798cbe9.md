To ensure continuous transmission of gas quality data to AEMO in real time as required by rule 289G(3),
the communications link must be highly reliable achieving at least a 99.9% level of availability per annum and the communications link should have minimised single points of dependency. AEMO must receipt the gas quality data at least once every 6 minutes from the gas quality monitoring
system .
