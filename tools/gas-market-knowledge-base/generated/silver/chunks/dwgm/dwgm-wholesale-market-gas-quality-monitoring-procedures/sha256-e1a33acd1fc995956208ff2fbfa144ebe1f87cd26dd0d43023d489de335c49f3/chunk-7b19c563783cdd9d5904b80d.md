4564 uses the US spelling for sulfide in place of the Australian spelling of sulphide. Both words have the same meaning and are to be treated as interchangeable and not inferring a specific legislative instrument.. SWN, Definition = System wide notice (SWN) as defined in the electronic communication procedures which is part of the Wholesale Market Management Procedures.. SWZ, Definition = A system withdrawal zone (SWZ) includes all settlement metering points that withdraw gas from the market within the specified zone as defined in the system security procedures .. TBM, Definition = Tertiary Butyl Mercaptan - a mercaptan used in gas odourisation. THT, Definition = Tetrahydrothiophene - a cyclic sulphide used in gas odourisation.. TJ, Definition = Terajoule (10 12 Joules)
