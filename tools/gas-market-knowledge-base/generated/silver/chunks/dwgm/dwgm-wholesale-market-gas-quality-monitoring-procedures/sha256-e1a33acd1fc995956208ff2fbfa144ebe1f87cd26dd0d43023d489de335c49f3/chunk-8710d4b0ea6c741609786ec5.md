AEMO has determined the limits for specific contaminants found in biomethane, as described in the standard gas quality specifications 10 :
- (a) Semi-volatile organic compounds (Limonene) -100 mg/m 3
- (b) Halogens:
- (i) Fluorine -5 mg/m 3
- (ii) Chlorine -1.5 mg/m 3
- (c) Ammonia -10 mg/m 3
- (d) Siloxanes -0.45 mg Si/m 3 (Siloxanes limit is quantified as concentration of silicon in milligrams per 1 cubic meter of dry gas)
