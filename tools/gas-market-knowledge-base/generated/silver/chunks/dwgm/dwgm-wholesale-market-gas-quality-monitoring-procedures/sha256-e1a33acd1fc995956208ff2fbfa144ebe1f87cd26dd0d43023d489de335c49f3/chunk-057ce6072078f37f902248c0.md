AEMO may use the data provided above to monitor the gas quality monitoring system.
In addition, AEMO may also monitor:
- (a) communication from the gas quality monitoring system of fault alarms (e.g. sulphur analyser tape low warning);
- (b) the data validation processes, outlined in clause 8.4 data validation process of the Wholesale Market Metering Procedures, to identify gas quality data issues;
- (c) the communication status to identify issues with the communication link at the gas quality monitoring system ; and
