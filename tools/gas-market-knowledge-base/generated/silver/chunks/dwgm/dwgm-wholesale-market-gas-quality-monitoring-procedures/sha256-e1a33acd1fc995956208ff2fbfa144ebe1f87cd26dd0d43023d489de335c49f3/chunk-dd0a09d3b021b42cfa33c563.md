For the purpose of AEMO considering a gas quality standard that is different to the standard gas quality specifications under rule 287(1), the responsible gas quality monitoring provider (or intending provider) must:
- (a) submit a proposal for consideration by AEMO for the applicable system injection point ; and
- (b) obtain AEMO's approval for an alternative gas quality monitoring arrangement in accordance with the Rules and clause 2.8.3.
For the avoidance of doubt, the consultation and approval under clause 2.8.3 may include the process to obtain approval from Registered participants who withdraw gas at an impacted transmission delivery point, as required by 287(5).
