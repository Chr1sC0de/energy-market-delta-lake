- (iv) If a gas quality monitoring plan is conditionally approved:
- (A) then condition(s) are not met, AEMO will reject the conditionally approved gas quality monitoring plan.
- (B) then condition(s) are met, AEMO will inform the responsible gas quality monitoring provider in writing of the decision to approve it as the gas quality monitoring plan .
- (v) If not approved under clause (a)(iv) above, the responsible gas quality monitoring provider may submit a plan to AEMO to provide an amended gas quality monitoring plan , under clause (a) above.
- (b) For an existing or intending responsible gas quality monitoring provider for a proposed new gas quality monitoring system , a draft gas quality monitoring plan must be:
- (i) Submitted by the existing or intending responsible gas quality monitoring provider to AEMO in writing via email.
- (ii) AEMO may request clarifications of the draft gas quality monitoring plan from the gas quality monitoring provider . The gas quality monitoring provider must provide a response to AEMO within 10 business days, or such other date as agreed with AEMO.
