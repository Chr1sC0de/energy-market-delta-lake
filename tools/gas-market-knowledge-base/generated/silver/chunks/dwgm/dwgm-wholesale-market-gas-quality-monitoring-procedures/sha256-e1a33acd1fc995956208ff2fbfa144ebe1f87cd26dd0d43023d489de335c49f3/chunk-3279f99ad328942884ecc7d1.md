AEMO has determined the limits for other objectionable constituents, as described in the standard gas quality specifications , as being the following gas quality specifications limits: 9
- (a) Elemental Sulphur -Max as 1.0 μ g/m 3
- (b) Mercury -Max as 1.0 μ g/m 3
- (c) Radioactivity -Max as 600 Bq/m 3
- (d) Arsenic -Max as 19 μ g/m 3
- (e) Toluene -Max as 191 mg/m 3
- (f) Xylene -Max as 100 mg/m 3
