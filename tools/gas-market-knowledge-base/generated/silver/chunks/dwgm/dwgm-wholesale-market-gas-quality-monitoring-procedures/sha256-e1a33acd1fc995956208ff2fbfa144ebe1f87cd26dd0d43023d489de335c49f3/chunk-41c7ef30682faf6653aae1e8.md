- (c) Monitoring and testing -The monitoring and testing of gas quality, coupled with suspension protocols, provide additional control measures. The risk assessment process shall determine the extent of monitoring and testing required and the effectiveness of this approach;
- (d) Co-mingling or blending -Where applicable, co-mingling or blending with other gas sources can be used to manage gas properties and contaminants. The risk assessment process shall determine the suitability and effectiveness of this approach; and
- (e) Infrastructure and appliance resilience -The ability of downstream infrastructure and appliances to manage variations in gas composition shall be assessed. The risk assessment process shall determine the suitability and effectiveness of existing infrastructure and appliances in relation to the proposed gas composition.
