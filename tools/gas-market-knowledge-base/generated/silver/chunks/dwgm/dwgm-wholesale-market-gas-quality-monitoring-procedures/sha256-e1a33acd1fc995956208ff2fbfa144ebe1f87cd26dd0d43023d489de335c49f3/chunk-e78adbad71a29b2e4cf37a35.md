- (iii) AEMO may inform the responsible gas quality monitoring provider in writing of the decision to:
- (A) Conditionally approve the draft gas quality monitoring plan where AEMO provides the conditions in writing to the responsible gas quality monitoring provider .
- (B) Reject the draft gas quality monitoring plan and provide reasons.
- (iv) If a draft gas quality monitoring plan is conditionally approved:
- (A) then condition(s) are not met, AEMO will reject the conditionally approved draft gas quality monitoring plan.
- (B) then condition(s) are met, AEMO will inform the responsible gas quality monitoring provider in writing of the decision to approve it as the gas quality monitoring plan .
- (v) If not approved under clause (b)(iv) or (b)(v) above, the responsible gas quality monitoring provider may submit a plan to AEMO to provide an amended draft gas quality monitoring plan under clause (b)(i) above or a gas quality monitoring plan under clause (a)(i) above.
