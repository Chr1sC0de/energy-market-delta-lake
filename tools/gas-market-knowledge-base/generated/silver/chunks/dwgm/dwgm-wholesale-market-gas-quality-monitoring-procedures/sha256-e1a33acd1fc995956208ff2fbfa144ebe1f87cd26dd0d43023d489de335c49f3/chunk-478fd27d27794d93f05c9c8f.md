The gas quality monitoring systems provide gas quality specification parameters to AEMO to allow the:
(a), 1 = Publication, within day, of the gas quality specification parameters at each system injection point and where provided AEMO may publish data for DDS injection points .. (b), 1 = Publication, on a weekly basis, the gas quality specification parameters' daily average for each heating value zone.. (c), 1 = Operation of AEMO's Heating Value Allocation Model which determines the heating value and gas composition data at each heating value zone.
