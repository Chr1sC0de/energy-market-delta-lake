These Procedures apply to AEMO and each person to whom they are expressed to apply.
Responsible gas quality monitoring providers must comply with the requirements of these Procedures for their gas quality monitoring systems and update their gas quality monitoring plan to reflect the requirements of these Procedures.
Responsible gas quality monitoring providers must comply with the additional requirements added to these Procedures in version 2.0 in response to the publication of AS 4564:2025 (specification for general-purpose natural gas update) as soon as practicable. A new gas quality monitoring plan to reflect these additional requirements must be submitted to AEMO within 90 business days from the date on which version 2.0 of these Procedures becomes effective or such other date as AEMO may agree with the responsible gas quality monitoring provider .
