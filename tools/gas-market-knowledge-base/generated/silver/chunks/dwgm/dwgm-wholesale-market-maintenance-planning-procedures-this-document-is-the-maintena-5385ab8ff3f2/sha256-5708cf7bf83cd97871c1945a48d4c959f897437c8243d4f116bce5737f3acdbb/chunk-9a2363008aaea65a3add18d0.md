- (a) Delay: the maintenance has not obtained a facility release and will be re-scheduled to an alternate date.
- (b) Suspend: AEMO rescind a facility release with the intention that maintenance may recommence within the approved maintenance window. Maintenance must stop for the duration of the suspension.
- (c) Cancelled: AEMO rescind a facility release with the intention that maintenance will not recommence within the approved maintenance window. This work may be re-scheduled to an alternate period.
