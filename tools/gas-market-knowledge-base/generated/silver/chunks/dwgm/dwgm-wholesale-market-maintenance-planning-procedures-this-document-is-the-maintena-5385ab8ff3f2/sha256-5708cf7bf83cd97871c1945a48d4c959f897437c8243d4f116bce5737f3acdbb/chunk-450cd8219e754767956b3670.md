A facility operator must notify AEMO of emergency work as soon as practicably possible. Notification must include relevant details of:
- (a) the breakdown or threatened breakdown; and
- (b) its proposed response to the breakdown or threatened breakdown.
The notification should also include the maintenance information specified in clause 2.3.5, where available.
For the avoidance of doubt, emergency work may commence without AEMO approval. The facility operator must immediately notify AEMO of this emergency work .
