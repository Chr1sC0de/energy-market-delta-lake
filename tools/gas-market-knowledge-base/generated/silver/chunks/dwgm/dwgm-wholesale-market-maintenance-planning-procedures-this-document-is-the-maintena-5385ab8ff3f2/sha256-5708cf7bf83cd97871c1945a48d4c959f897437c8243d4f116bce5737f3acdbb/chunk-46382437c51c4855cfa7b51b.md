Maintenance information is required to be reported to the Gas Bulletin Board as part of the Short Term Capacity Outlook and Medium Term Capacity Outlook data submitted by some BB reporting entities .
AEMO, as the operator of the DTS, is responsible for submitting DTS maintenance forecasts.
These maintenance forecasts will impact the Short Term Capacity Outlook and Medium Term Capacity Outlook data for the DTS that AEMO submits to the Gas Bulletin Board.
