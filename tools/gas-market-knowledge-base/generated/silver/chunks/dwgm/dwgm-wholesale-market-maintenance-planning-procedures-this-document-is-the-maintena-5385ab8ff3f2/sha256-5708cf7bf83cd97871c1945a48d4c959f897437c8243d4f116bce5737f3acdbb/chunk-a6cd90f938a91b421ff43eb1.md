AEMO will consider the impact of any concurrent emergency work in the evaluation of risk from planned maintenance and unplanned maintenance .
AEMO grants maintenance planning review approval to maintenance requests where the aggregated risk of all facility operators' maintenance being performed at the time is assessed as acceptable. Maintenance , that has completed AEMO's maintenance planning review, is still subject to the facility operator requesting a facility release to undertake maintenance as detailed in clause 3.2.
