Failure to approve pricing schedule and operating schedule on time, AEMO will: = If there is a Feasible Operating Schedule and Valid Pricing Schedule available AEMO will: a) approve the operating schedule and pricing schedule b) declare an administered price period and set the administered price flag (manual); c) review the market price of the approved pricing schedule and if greater than the APC, manually cap at the APC d) notify Market Participants of the event and actions undertaken If there is a Valid Pricing Schedule but no Feasible Operating Schedule AEMO will: a) create an operating schedule from the Valid Pricing Schedule b) approve the operating schedule and pricing schedule. Failure to approve pricing schedule and operating schedule on time, if this is not possible, or does not resolve matters: = No further action
