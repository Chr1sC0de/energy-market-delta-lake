- (c) AEMO is not obliged to consider and may reject a proposal submitted under clause 4.10.3(a) without consultation by giving written notice to the proponent in any of the following circumstances:
- (i) in AEMO's reasonable opinion, the proposal is misconceived, lacking in substance, frivolous or vexatious; or
- (ii) in AEMO's reasonable opinion, the proposal is for a change proposed but rejected in the previous 12 months.
