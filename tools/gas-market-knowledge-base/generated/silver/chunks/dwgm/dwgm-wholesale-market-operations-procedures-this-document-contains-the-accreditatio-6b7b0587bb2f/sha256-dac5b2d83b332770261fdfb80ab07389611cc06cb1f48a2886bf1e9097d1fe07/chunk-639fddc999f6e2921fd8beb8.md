Exceeding cumulative price threshold, Declaration of ending of administered price period = AEMO will declare the administered price period to be at an end: • at the end of the gas day following the gas day on which the cumulative price falls below and remains below the cumulative price threshold , provided no other condition for an administered price period is applicable.
