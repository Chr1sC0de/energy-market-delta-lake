- (a) Upon receipt by AEMO of payment in full of the purchase price from a Buyer in accordance with these terms, AEMO will authorise the LNG Storage Provider to transfer the quantity of LNG stock specified in the LNG stock transfer invoice to that Buyer by recording the Buyer as the holder of that quantity of LNG stock in the register of LNG storage capacity entitlement required to be kept by the LNG Storage Provider under rule 281(5) of the NGR.
- (b) The Buyer must ensure that at the time of transfer of the LNG stock from AEMO to the Buyer, the Buyer will have the right to sufficient available LNG storage capacity at the LNG storage facility to store the LNG stock transferred from AEMO to the Buyer.
