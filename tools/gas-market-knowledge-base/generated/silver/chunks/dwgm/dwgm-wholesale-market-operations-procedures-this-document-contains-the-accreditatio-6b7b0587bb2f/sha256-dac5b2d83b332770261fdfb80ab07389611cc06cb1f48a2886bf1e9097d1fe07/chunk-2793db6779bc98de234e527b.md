Example: A schedule at 9:30am with start time 10:00am indicates that an administered price period should apply from 10:00am (e.g. cumulative price will exceed the cumulative price threshold ). AEMO will set the administered price flag for that scheduling horizon prior to approval of the schedule. Then a further re-schedule at 9:40am with start time 10:00am indicates that an administered price period should not apply from 10:00am (e.g. cumulative price now will not, as a result of the re-schedule outcomes, exceed the cumulative price threshold ). In this circumstance, AEMO will rescind the administered price flag prior to approval of the re-schedule with start time 10:00am and the market price will accordingly remain uncapped during that period.
