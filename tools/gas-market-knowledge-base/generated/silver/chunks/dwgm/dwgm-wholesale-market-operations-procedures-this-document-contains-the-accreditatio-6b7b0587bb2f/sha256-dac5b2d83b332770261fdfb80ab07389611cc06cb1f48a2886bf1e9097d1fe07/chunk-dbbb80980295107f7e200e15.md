In the event AEMO is informed of a Market Participant being issued with a suspension notice or being deregistered from the Market then AEMO will rerun the calculation of the Allocation Factor . The suspended or deregistered Market Participants Withdrawal Assignment from the base financial year will be removed from the calculation (including from the total Withdrawal Assignment). The remaining participants will receive a percentage share of the residual Total Withdrawal Assignment to determine their amended Allocation Factor for the remainder of the relevant year .
The following example shows the calculation of the Allocation Factor where a Market Participant (Company D) is deregistered or suspended in the Market during a relevant year .
In this instance the Withdrawal Assignment for Company D is treated as 0 GJ when the Allocation Factor is recalculated after the company is suspended or deregistered.
