Table 21 Condition 7 -Market Participants unable to submit scheduling input data by the required bid submission time

WebExchanger outage/failure for part/entirety of the submission window or other issue preventing Market Participants from submitting scheduling inputs, AEMO will: = a) notify Market Participants as soon as possible of the failure b) produce standard schedules at the next scheduling horizon using the latest Market Participant submissions c) notify Market Participants when WebExchanger returned to service. WebExchanger outage/failure for part/entirety of the submission window or other issue preventing Market Participants from submitting scheduling inputs, if this is not possible, or does not resolve matters: = No further action
