- (a) Auction statements will include GST.
- (b) By 12.00 pm on the 5 th business day after an auction statement is made available under clause 4.12.8 the auction participant must pay to AEMO in cleared funds the total amount stated to be payable to AEMO by that auction participant in its auction statement, whether or not the auction participant disputes the amount payable.
- (c) If an auction participant does not pay the total amount payable under an auction statement in accordance with clause 4.12.9, the capacity certificates referred to in the auction statement will not be allocated to the auction participant and will automatically be made available for allocation by AEMO at subsequent capacity certificates auctions .
