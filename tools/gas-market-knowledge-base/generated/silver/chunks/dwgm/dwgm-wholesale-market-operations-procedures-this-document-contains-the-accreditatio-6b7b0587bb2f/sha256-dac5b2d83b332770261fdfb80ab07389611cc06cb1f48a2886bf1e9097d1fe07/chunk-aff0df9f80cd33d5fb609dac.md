Table 1 Glossary of terms
Accreditation, Definition = Accreditation in accordance with rule 210 of the NGR. The operation of an accreditation in regard to scheduling is defined in clause 7.10.8.. Accreditation Application, Definition = Application made by a Market Participant for Accreditation.. APC, Definition = means the administered price cap applicable to the market pric e for any scheduling horizon when an administered price period applies. Auction Notice, Definition = The notice of a capacity certificates auction published by AEMO under clause 4.8.2.. Auction Platform, Definition = The system established by AEMO for the capacity certificates auction .. Auction Solver, Definition = The program used to determine the results of the capacity certificates auction in accordance with the auction design principles in the NGR. Auction statement, Definition = A statement issue to Market Participants as set out in clause 4.12.8. BoD, Definition = Beginning of gas day. Bid submission time, Definition = The time by which a Market Participant must have submitted data in accordance with rule 211 of the NGR.. Capacity Certificates listing platform, Definition = The system established by AEMO for listing capacity certificates in accordance with rule 330..
