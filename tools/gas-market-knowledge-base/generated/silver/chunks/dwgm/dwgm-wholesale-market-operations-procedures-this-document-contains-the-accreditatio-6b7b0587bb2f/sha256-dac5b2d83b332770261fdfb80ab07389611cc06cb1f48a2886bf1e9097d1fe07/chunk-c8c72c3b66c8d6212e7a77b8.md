A ZFTC is a special case of the NFTC, which is outlined in the previous clause. The purpose of a ZFTC is to enable the net aggregate injections or withdrawals on two or more pipeline systems to be constrained to reflect the physical capacity of the DTS.
The ZFTC parameters are:
Table 13 ZFTC parameters

maximum hourly net injection quantities, Description = the maximum net amount of gas that can be injected in an hour;. maximum hourly net withdrawal quantities, Description = the maximum net amount of gas that can be withdrawn in an hour;. maximum daily net injection quantities, Description = the maximum net amount of gas that can be injected over a day. maximum daily net withdrawal quantities, Description = the maximum net amount of gas that can be withdrawn over a day.
AEMO must apply:
- (a) net injection ZFTCs to operating schedules only; and
- (b) net withdrawal ZFTCs to pricing schedules and operating schedules .
