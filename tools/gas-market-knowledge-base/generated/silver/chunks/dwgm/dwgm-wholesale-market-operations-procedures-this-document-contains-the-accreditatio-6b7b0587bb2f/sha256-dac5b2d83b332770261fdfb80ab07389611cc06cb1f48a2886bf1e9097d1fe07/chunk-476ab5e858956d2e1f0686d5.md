If a Nominated DWGM participant is taken to have issued a Deemed Accreditation Application:
- (a) the requirements of rule 210(7) of the NGR will be taken to have been meet based on the information provided by the DWGM facility operator for the DWGM transfer or DWGM allocation, as applicable; and
- (b) subject to this clause, AEMO will accredit the controllable quantity for each gas day confirmed for the DWGM transfer or the increase confirmed for the DWGM allocation, as applicable by increasing or decreasing the maximum hourly quantity and ramp times for that controllable quantity in accordance with AEMO's standard practices to reflect the corresponding Transportation Capacity increase or decrease.
The increase or decrease of the maximum hourly quantity and ramp times will be for the period of the Transportation Capacity sold or purchased pursuant to the DWGM transfer or the DWGM allocation, as applicable, as confirmed by the DWGM facility operator .
