If AEMO identifies that bid data that has passed validation is causing an operating schedule or pricing schedule to be infeasible, AEMO may amend intraday the accredited quantities of the relevant Market Participant , as described further in clause 7.19. AEMO will notify the affected Market Participant of any such actions undertaken by AEMO.
Accreditation quantities for a Market Participant may include the accreditation parameters detailed in the accreditation procedures .
