AEMO may be required to reduce its LNG storage capacity for a variety of reasons, including, but not limited to:
- (a) If the LNG Storage Provider requests AEMO to relinquish all or part of AEMO's LNG reserve to satisfy a request for services to be provided to a Market Participant by means of the LNG Storage Provider's LNG storage facility , AEMO will relinquish the capacity to the LNG Storage Provider , except to the extent that relinquishing the capacity would breach AEMO's gas safety case or any other applicable legislative or regulatory instrument.
- (b) If AEMO is not required under the Rules to have a contract for use of uncontracted LNG storage capacity and AEMO does not otherwise require the capacity at the LNG storage facility , AEMO will relinquish the capacity to the LNG Storage Provider .
