A DWGM facility operator may apply to register multiple supply sources at a market injection point for the purpose of reflecting an outage of a supply source at that market injection point .
AEMO must register the multiple supply sources if the DWGM facility operator can demonstrate that appropriate arrangements are in place between the DWGM facility operator and the relevant Market Participants to allow the SSC to operate in accordance with clause 7.10.3.
