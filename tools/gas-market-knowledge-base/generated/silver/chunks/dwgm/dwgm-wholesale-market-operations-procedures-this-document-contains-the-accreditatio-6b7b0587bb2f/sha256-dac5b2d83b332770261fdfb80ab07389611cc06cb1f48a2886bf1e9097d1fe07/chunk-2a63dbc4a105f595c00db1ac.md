- (iv) The cumulative price remains less than the cumulative price threshold for an entire gas day following the gas day on which the cumulative price falls below and remains below the cumulative price threshold . For the avoidance of doubt, where cumulative price is below the cumulative price threshold for the first scheduling interval on a gas day , for conclusion of the administered price period , the cumulative price must remain below the cumulative price threshold for all subsequent scheduling intervals on that gas day and the entire gas day following.
