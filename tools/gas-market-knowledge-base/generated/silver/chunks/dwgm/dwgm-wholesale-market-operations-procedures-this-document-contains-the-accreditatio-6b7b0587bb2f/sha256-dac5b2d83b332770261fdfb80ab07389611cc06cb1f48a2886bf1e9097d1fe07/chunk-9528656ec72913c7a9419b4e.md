The six-monthly LNG summary report, required by rule 286C(2), to be published by:
- (a) 1 May will detail the changes in AEMO's LNG Reserve for the previous October to March period; and
- (b) 1 November will detail the changes in AEMO's LNG Reserve for the previous April to September period.
The information to be published in the report is detailed in rule 286C(2) and will be published on AEMO's website .
