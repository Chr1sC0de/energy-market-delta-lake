Purpose, 1 = This report provides daily Wallumbilla benchmark prices for each Gas Supply Hub trading location at Wallumbilla (i.e. RBP, SWQP, and QGP) as well as for 'Wallumbilla' which is a benchmark price across all trading locations.. Access, 1 = Public on the AEMO website, and accessible after subscription to all participants through File Share and Gas Hub Direct. Report period, 1 = The day ahead benchmark prices PLUS the last two years of the daily benchmark prices. Trigger, 1 = Time triggered (Report is generated and overwritten every 5 minutes starting from 8.30am until 5pm, then final report is generated at 5.30pm which will not be overwritten). Gas Hub Direct Category, 1 = GSH Trading. Gas Hub Direct Type, 1 = Wallumbilla Benchmark Price.
