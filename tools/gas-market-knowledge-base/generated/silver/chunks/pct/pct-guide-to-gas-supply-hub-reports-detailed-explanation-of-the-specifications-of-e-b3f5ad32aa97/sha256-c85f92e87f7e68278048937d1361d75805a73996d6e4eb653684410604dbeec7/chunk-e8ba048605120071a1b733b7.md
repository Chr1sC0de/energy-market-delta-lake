-  The report contains Trading and Delivery contact details for every participant in the GSH market.
-  The report is not sent to deregistered participants.
-  The report is sorted in the following order:
- -Ascending order of the 'PARTICIPANT_ID', then within each 'PARTICIPANT_ID',
- -Ascending order of the 'ROLE', then within each 'ROLE',
- -Ascending order of the 'LAST_NAME'.
