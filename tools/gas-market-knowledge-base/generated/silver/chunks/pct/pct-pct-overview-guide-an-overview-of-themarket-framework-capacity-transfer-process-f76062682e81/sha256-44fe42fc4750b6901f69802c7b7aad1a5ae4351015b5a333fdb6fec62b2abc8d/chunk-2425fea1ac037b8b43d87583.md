Both the Exchange Agreement and the Auction Agreement allow an agent to be appointed jointly (such as an unincorporated joint venture) or individually (such as under a corporate group). Appointing members are jointly and severally liable for the acts of the agent. AEMO manages the relationship between appointing and agent participants. The agent trades capacity for contracts held by the appointing participants.
