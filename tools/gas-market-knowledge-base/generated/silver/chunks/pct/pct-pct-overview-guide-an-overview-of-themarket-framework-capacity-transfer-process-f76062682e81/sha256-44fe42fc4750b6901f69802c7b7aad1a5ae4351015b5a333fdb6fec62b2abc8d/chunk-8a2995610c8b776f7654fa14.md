The operators of non-exempt transportation facilities are required to provide AEMO (and keep up to date) a specification of all pipeline, compression, park and backhaul service points. Where a notional point is used in place of a physical point, the Transportation Service Point Register specifies the physical points that are represented by the notional point.
AEMO publishes information provided by facility operators on the transfer of capacity between service points in a zone, which may assist shippers with gaining a better understanding of the deliverability risks associated with each zone.
To ensure that the receipt and delivery point zones can adapt to changes in the market or the operational or technical characteristics of the transportation facility, facility operators and any other person (including AEMO) may propose a change to the zones.
