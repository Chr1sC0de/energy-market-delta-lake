The exposure of GSH trading participants to capacity trades in the CTP is calculated as 25% of trades ahead of the transfer window and 100% of trades within the transfer window.
AEMO calculates an auction participant's exposure on submission of each bid and again prior to the auction run. The Auction Agreement defines the prudential process applicable to the auction, similar to GSH, where
Auction Exposure = Bid Exposure + Indicative Settlement + Invoiced Amounts
Bid Exposure is only measured on bids for the next auction.
Prior to a capacity transfer, default (and suspension) of a buyer or seller results in the close out of the transaction and payment of compensation to the non-defaulting party. The buyer and the seller provide collateral to cover 25% of transaction value.
Following the transfer of capacity, if the buyer or seller defaults in the market, the transaction and transfer stay on foot. The buyer provides collateral to cover 100% of transaction value.
