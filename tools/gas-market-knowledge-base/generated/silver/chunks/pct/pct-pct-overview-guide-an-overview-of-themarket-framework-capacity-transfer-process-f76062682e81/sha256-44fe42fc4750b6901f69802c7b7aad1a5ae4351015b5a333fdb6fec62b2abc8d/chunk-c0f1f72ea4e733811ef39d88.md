Currently, facilities in the eastern states and the NT operate on separate timetables. Gas days in NSW-ACT, Tasmania and South Australia start at 0630 hrs AEST, in Queensland at 0800, and in the Northern Territory at 0830. Similarly, nomination cut-off times also vary across eastern Australia.
The harmonised market timetable will take effect on 1 October 2019. This will establish a common gas day start time of 0600 hrs AEST across the east coast and the Northern Territory , , a common nomination cut-off time of 1500 hrs, and a common auction service nomination cut-off time of 1845 hrs.
