To maximise the pool of prospective buyers and sellers, firm forward haul and compression products are sold on the exchange using a zonal model (Figure 5), such that:
-  Shippers with firm forward haul or compression capacity can sell their point-to-point capacity on a zoneto-zone basis.
-  Secondary shippers can acquire the firm forward haul and/or compression capacity on a zone-to-zone basis and have secondary firm rights 12  at each receipt and delivery point in the relevant zone.
The receipt and delivery points in each zone, which may be physical or notional points, are specified in the Transportation Service Point Register (TSPR).
When a firm forward haul or compression product is traded, the seller notifies AEMO of the pipeline or compression receipt and delivery points it intends to release its capacity from, the buyer notifies AEMO of the pipeline or compression receipt and delivery points it intends to use in the relevant zones, and AEMO then notifies the facility operator of the selected service points in the capacity transfer notice. 13
