When a participant places an order for a product, AEMO systems check that a valid contract reference has been pre-selected. If the trade or bid is successful, the contract information is applied to the information provided to facility operators to execute the transfer in their system (in the capacity transfer notice) and to make adjustments in AEMO's market systems.
For further information on selecting contract references, refer to the relevant guide on the AEMO website 15 .
