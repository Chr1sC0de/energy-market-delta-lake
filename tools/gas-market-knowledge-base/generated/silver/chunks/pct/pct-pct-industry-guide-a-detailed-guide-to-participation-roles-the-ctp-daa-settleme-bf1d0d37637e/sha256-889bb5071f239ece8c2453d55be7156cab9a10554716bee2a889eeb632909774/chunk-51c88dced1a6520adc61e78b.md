Note. Units of TJ may be shown in some examples in this guide; however, auction bids must be expressed in GJ.
In the example shown in Table 9 and Figure 12, a participant bids on 10 TJ @ $1 per GJ on a forward haul capacity product comprising receipt point (Wal HP Trade Point), located in receipt zone RZ-03, and delivery point (MSP Exit), located in delivery zone DZ-01, which are connected by segments FS-04, FS-05, FS-06 and FS-07. As illustrated in Figure 13, the auction system breaks the bid into each component, constrains each component with its AQL, determines prices for each component, and then aggregates the price of all components, giving the participant a single price for the successful bid.
