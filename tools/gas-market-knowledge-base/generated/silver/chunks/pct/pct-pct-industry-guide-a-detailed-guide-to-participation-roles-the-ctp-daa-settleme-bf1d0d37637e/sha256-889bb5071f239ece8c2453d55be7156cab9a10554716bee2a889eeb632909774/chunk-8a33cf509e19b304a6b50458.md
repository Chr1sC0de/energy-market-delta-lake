At the commencement of the CTP and DAA, facilities in the eastern states and the NT operate on independent timetables. Gas days in NSW-ACT, Tasmania and South Australia start at 0630 hrs AEST, in Queensland at 0800, and in the Northern Territory at 0830. Similarly, nomination cut-off times also vary across eastern Australia. The transitional market timetable (Table 15 and Figure 26) will take effect from the date of commencement.
The harmonised market timetable (Table 15 and Figure 27) will take effect on 1 October 2019. This will establish a common gas day start time of 0600 hrs AEST across the east coast and the Northern Territory, a common nomination cut-off time of 1500 hrs, and a common auction service nomination cut-off time of 1845 hrs.
