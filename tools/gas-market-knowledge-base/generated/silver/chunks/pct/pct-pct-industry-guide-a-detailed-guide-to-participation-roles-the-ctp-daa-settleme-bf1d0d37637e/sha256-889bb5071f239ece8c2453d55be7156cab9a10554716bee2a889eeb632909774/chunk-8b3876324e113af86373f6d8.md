Table 13 AQLs for backhaul product components

Backhaul RP also used as a forward haul DP, AQL = Scheduled flow (SF) at the forward haul DP. This is used for demand offset backhaul.. Backhaul RP also used as a forward haul RP, AQL = Unused capacity (UC) at the forward haul RP.. Backhaul DP also used as a forward haul RP, AQL = Scheduled flow (SF) at the forward haul RP. This is used for injection offset backhaul.. Backhaul DP also used as a forward haul DP, AQL = Unused capacity (UC) at the forward haul RP. The delivery point could be to a directly connected user, a distribution system, a park or another pipeline. As such, there needs to be spare physical capacity at the delivery point.
Refer to Appendix A1.3.1, for example AQL calculation for backhaul service points.
