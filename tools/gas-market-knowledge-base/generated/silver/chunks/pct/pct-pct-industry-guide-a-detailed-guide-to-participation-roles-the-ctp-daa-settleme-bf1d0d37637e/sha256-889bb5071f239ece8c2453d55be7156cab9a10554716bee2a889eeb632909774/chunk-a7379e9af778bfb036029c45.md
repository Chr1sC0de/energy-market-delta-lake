Following an auction run, AEMO sends each facility operator the auction results (see auction reports in Section 8.2.3). Auction results provide the information necessary to enable facility operators to make adjustments to shipper contracts:
-  Contract reference
-  Quantity won
-  Nominated receipt point and delivery point (including the point's ids)
-  Clearing price
-  Transaction ID
The facility operator uses the auction results to confirm that the shipper is entitled to use the capacity it has won at the auction. Once facility operators have made the necessary adjustments, auction participants nominate to facility operators to make use of any auction capacity.
