To ensure that the receipt and delivery point zones can adapt to changes in the market or the operational or technical characteristics of the transportation facility, facility operators and any other person (including AEMO) may propose a change to the zones.
When new points are added (or removed) on facilities by a facility operator, the facility operator submits an updated service point list to AEMO, which may result in a change to the register. Changes to the service point, zone and segment specification can be initiated by AEMO proposing a change or by any participant proposing a change to zones or segments to AEMO.
AEMO will consult on proposals or changes to service points by publishing a notice on its website. Consultation on changes will include transitional considerations.
