Table 5 Capacity transfer notice information
Facility, Fields = FacilityId FacilityName. Facility, Notes = As reported in the Transportation Facility Registration data transaction (and report). Participant, Fields = ParticipantId ParticipantName. Participant, Notes = As reported in the Registered Participants data transaction (and report). Contract, Fields = ServiceReference STTMContractReference. Contract, Notes = As provided to AEMO in the Contract Information Details data transaction.. Quantity, Fields = IncrementalQuantityChange OverallQuantityChange. Quantity, Notes = Netted traded quantity to be transferred. For buyer (increase capacity), the quantity fields have a positive sign. For seller (decrease capacity), the quantity fields have a negative sign. Note. For the initial notice issued for each transfer set, the Incremental and Overall Quantity Change are the same. These quantities differ when the facility operator is required to reverse a transfer that has already been processed (see Section 7.2).. Service points, zones and segments, Fields = ReceiptPoint ReceiptZone DeliveryPoint DeliveryZone. Service points, zones and segments, Notes = Forward haul and compression products specify the relevant facility, service points and zones.
