The hybrid model allows CBU capacity from individual points to be moved to other points within the zone if there is unused capacity at those points. When a shipper wins capacity, it knows it has secured capacity at the points it requires and will only be curtailed if firm capacity holders renominate or there is an unplanned, operational issue on the gas day.
