The AQL for forward haul service points is unused capacity. Unused capacity ( UC ) is a measure of the spare physical capacity at the service point at nomination cut-off time. The purpose of the unused capacity auction quantity limit is to ensure that there is sufficient capacity at the service point to facilitate any auction nominations.
