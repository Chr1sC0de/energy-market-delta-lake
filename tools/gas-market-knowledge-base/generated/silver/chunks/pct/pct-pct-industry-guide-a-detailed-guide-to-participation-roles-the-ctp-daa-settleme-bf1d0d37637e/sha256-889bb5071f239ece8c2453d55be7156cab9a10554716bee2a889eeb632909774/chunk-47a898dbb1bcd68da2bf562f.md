Public report providing a summary of curtailed auction volumes for each auction facility for the previous gas day, listing the following items for each facility and service type:
-  initial cleared quantity as determined by the Auction Solver;
-  where there has been a curtailment of the initial cleared quantity, the revised auction quantity which must be the aggregate minimum quantity made available to auction participants on the gas day; and
-  final aggregated scheduled nominations.
Published daily (D+1) for the previous gas day.
