If a shipper fails to pay a market invoice at the due time, AEMO draws down on collateral posted by auction participant. Prudential exposure is measured regularly during trading day (see Section 10.2 and AEMO will reject market transactions if the participant does not have sufficient collateral. The auction participant must provide collateral to meet their ongoing settlement exposure across the GSH, CTP and DAA.
If the collateral funds are not available on the day payments need to be made to facility operators, AEMO will pro rata payments accordingly to all facility operators and trading participants. Pro rata payment amounts are calculated separately for each market. In other words, if the defaulting participant does not owe any money for auction settlement, then auction payments to facility operator will not be impacted.
