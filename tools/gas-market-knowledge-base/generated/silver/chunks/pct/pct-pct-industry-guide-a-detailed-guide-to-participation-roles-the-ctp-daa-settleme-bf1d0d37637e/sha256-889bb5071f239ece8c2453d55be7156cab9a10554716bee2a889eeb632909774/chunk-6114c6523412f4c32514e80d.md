Changes to capacity rights in the STTM or DWGM will only be made following confirmation from the facility operator that capacity has been transferred. If there is a problem transferring the capacity rights in the STTM or DWGM, then AEMO may need to cancel the capacity transfer. This would result in AEMO issuing a reversal of the capacity transfer to the facility operator in a Capacity Transfer Notice.
