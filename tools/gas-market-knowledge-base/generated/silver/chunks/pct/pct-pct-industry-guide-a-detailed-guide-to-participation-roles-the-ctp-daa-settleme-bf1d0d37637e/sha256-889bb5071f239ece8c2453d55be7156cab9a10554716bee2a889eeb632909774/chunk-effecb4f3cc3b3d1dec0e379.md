The terms and conditions on which the buyer can use products purchased on the DAA are set out in the facility operator's standard OTSA. Among other things, the OTSA specifies the hourly flexibility the shipper has and provides for a reasonable endeavours renomination right and a zero-imbalance allowance. If the shipper requires additional flexibility, it can procure it from facility operators or shippers, where operationally and technically feasible.
