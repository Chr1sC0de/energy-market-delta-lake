Trading participants submit orders directly onto the exchange. All orders on the exchange are anonymous. Orders for capacity are validated on submission:
-  Is the submission correctly formatted?
-  Does the participant have sufficient collateral?
-  Has the participant pre-selected a contract to apply to the trade?
Once an order is validated, an order confirmation is sent to the participant. Participants can use the order confirmation report to monitor their activity.
