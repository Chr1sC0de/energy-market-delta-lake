The auction quantity limit for backhaul reflects the physical flow of gas between the backhaul points in the forward haul direction for the segment. The AQL for a backhaul segment is the scheduled net priority forward haul flow for the backhaul pipeline segment for the gas day.
Refer to Appendix A1.3.2, for example AQL calculation for backhaul segments.
