Each auction product type has multiple product components. The product components have auction quantity limits (AQLs) applied against them, which constrain the amount of capacity available in the auction product. A participant's bid must be successful for all product components to be allocated capacity in an auction product.
Table 8 Auction product components

Forward haul, Components = Receipt point Delivery point Receipt zone Delivery zone Forward haul segment. Forward haul, Notes = There may be more than one forward haul segment for a forward haul product. However, there is only ever one receipt zone and one delivery zone.. Backhaul, Components = Backhaul receipt point Backhaul delivery point Backhaul segment. Backhaul, Notes = There may be more than one backhaul segment for a backhaul product.. Compression, Components = Facility compression service Compression receipt point Compression receipt zone Compression delivery point Compression delivery zone. Compression, Notes = The facility compression service component is similar to a forward haul pipeline segment. It reflects the CBU capacity of the compression facility.
