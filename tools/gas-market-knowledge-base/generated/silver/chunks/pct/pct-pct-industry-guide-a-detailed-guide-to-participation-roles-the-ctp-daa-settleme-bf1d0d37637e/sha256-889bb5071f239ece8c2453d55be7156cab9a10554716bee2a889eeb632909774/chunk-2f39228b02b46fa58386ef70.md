Figure 20 Pricing scenario 2: Bids clear the AQL
Where a bid against a product component exactly clears the AQL, the clearing price could be either the bid price or any lower price. The solver sets prices at their highest level without violating the bid prices and AQL constraints. Consistent with the design principles, the price in the following example is set at $2.00, which is the lowest cleared bid.
