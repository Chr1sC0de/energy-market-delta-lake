-  If B purchased capacity on DAA instead of the CTP, the accreditation adjustment would only be applied to B's MHQ.
-  If either party has specified a null MHQ, no adjustment would be made.
-  If the facility operator ramping period is 2 hours, then the ramp rate for the buyer and seller is the transferred MHQ divided by ramping period, i.e. A's ramp rate = 500/2 = 250 GJ/hr, and B's ramp rate = 750/2 = 375 GJ/hr.
Note. Current DWGM bidding and scheduling processes are not affected by the introduction of pipeline capacity trading.
