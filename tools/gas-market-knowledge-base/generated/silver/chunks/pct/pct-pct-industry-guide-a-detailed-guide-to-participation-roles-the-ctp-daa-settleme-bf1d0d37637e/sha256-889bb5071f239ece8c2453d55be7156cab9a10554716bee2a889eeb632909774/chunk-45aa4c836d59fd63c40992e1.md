The contractual arrangements that shippers need to have in place with AEMO and facility operators to trade and transfer capacity are illustrated in Figure 2. Specifically, to participate in secondary trading of capacity, a shipper must have an OTSA with the relevant facility operator and either a GSH Exchange Agreement (for the CTP) or an Auction Agreement (for the DAA) or both. Refer to Section 1.4 for descriptions of these agreements.
