Part 22 of the NGR sets out the rules of conduct for GSH Exchange members, which includes requirements to observe high standards of market conduct and to act with due skill, care and diligence in using the trading system and performing transactions, including submission of orders, providing information to AEMO, compliance and performance of transactions.
The AER is responsible for monitoring auction behaviour to ensure that shippers do not make false or misleading nominations and renominations. The AER maintains a guideline that sets out how facility operators and shippers should record and maintain nomination and renomination data. Facility operators are required to record relevant shipper data and provide the information to the AER on request, and keep a historical record of this information.
