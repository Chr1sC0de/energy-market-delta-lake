If the facility operator identifies a validation failure, the facility operator notifies AEMO in an interim Capacity Transfer Status file (see below), and AEMO reports the information to the trading participant, which provides the trader with an opportunity to rectify the failure with the facility operator.
If the validation failure is not rectified, then the facility operator will need to reduce pro rata the capacity transfers that cannot be completed due to the validation failure.
