Backhaul auction services are sold using a point-to-point model between the backhaul receipt points and backhaul delivery points that AEMO determines should be included in the DAA from time to time as specified in the TSPR (Section 2.3). In contrast to forward haul and compression services, the availability of backhaul services is not constrained by CBU capacity. The availability of backhaul services instead depends on whether there are sufficient firm net forward haul flows that have been scheduled prior to the auction for a gas day between the points used for the backhaul service. When bidding in the auction for a backhaul auction service, the shipper bids for the backhaul receipt and backhaul delivery point pair that they wish to use.
