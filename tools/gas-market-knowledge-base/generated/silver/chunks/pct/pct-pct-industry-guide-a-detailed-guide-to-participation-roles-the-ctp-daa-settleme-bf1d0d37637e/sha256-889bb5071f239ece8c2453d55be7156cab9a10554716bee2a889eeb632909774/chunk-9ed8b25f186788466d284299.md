A sell order (offer) means that a participant is prepared to sell the offered quantity of capacity between forward haul and compression product zones or at the park service point at a price equal to or greater than the offer price.
Buy order (bid) means that a participant is prepared to buy the bid quantity of capacity between forward haul and compression product zones or at the park service point at a price equal to or less the bid price.
