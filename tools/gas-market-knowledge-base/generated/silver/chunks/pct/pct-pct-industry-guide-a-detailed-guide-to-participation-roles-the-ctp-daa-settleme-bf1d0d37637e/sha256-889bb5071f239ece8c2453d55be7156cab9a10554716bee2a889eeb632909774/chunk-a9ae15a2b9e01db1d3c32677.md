The GSH Publishing Direct web application is available in AEMO's Markets Portal. This app is used to:
-  Access and download CSV reports
-  Subscribe to receive an email and/or SMS alert when new versions of reports are published
-  Subscribe to receive CSV reports via email when new versions are published
Facility operators are most likely to use Publishing Direct to access the following reports:
-  Capacity Transfer and Auction Notice
-  Settlement Supporting Data
For further help refer to the Guide to GSH Publishing Direct.
