- (a) AEMO may impose a Trading Halt on the Auction Participant by suspending the Auction Participant's access to or use of the Auction Platform:
- (i) if a Suspension Event has occurred in relation to the Auction Participant;
- (ii) under another provision of this Agreement or the Auction Procedures that allows AEMO to suspend or limit the Auction Participant's access to the Auction Platform;
- (iii) at the request of the AER in connection with any investigation of an alleged breach of the Market Conduct Rules;
- (ii) subject to the recipient created tax invoice agreement between AEMO and the Auction Participant, the supplier must give the recipient a tax invoice for the supply (including when AEMO is treated under GST law as making a supply); and
- (iii) a tax invoice or recipient created tax invoice issued by AEMO may be in the form of a Settlement Statement.
