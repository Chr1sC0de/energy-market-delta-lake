Rule 630(3) requires these Procedures to provide for the arrangements under which, for the purposes of Part 20, a sale through the Gas Trading Exchange of transportation capacity at an STTM interface point will be notified, or will be taken to have been notified, to AEMO and where necessary, confirmed, approved or accepted by AEMO or any other person.
