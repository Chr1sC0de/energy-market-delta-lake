Table 24.1.1:  Bulletin Board reports

Auction Quantity Limits Report, Content = Auction quantity limits for each product component. Auction Quantity Limits Report, Publication Time = On each gas day as soon as practicable after the cut-off time for provision of auction quantity limits by facility operators , as may be extended, under the Capacity Transfer and Auction Timetable. Product Component Price and Volume Report, Content = Cleared quantities, prices and information about the sensitivity of price outcomes to changes in parameters (using parameters determined by AEMO) for each product component. Product Component Price and Volume Report, Publication Time = On each gas day as soon as practicable after the capacity auction has provided cleared quantities and prices for each product component. Revised Auction Quantities Report, Content = For each auction facility and service: (a) initial cleared quantity as determined by the Auction Solver; (b) where there has been a curtailment of the initial cleared quantity, the revised auction quantity which must be the aggregate minimum quantity made available to auction participants on the gas day ; and (c) final aggregated scheduled nominations.. Revised Auction Quantities Report, Publication Time = 14:00 on gas day D+1
