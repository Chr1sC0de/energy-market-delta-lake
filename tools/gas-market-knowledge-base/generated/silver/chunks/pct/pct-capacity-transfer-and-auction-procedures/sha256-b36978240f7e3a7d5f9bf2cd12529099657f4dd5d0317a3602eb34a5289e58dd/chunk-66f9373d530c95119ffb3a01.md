- (a) If a party ( Affected Party ) is prevented or hindered by Force Majeure from complying with its obligations under this Agreement (other than an obligation referred to in clause A.18.2(b)) then the obligations of the Affected Party are suspended during the period and to the extent that those circumstances continue to prevent or hinder that party from complying with its obligations.
- (b) Force Majeure cannot be claimed in respect of, and does not excuse non-performance of, and does not suspend, any obligation with respect to:
- (i) payment of any money; or
- (ii) providing or replacing Credit Support.
