- (a) Each gas day , a facility operator must determine and send to AEMO the a uction quantity limits for the following gas day for each product component associated with an auction service provided by means of any of its auction facilities , as provided for in clause 19.2.
- (b) Where an auction quantity limit for a gas day is determined using nominations , the nominations made at or before the nomination cut-off time for the auction facility for the gas day (as provided in clause 20.3) must be used.
- (c) Where an auction quantity limit for a gas day is determined using physical capacity , the most recent information about physical capacity of the auction facility reasonably available from the facility operator at the time the limits are determined must be used.
- (d) If there is a change to any information used to calculate an auction quantity limit after it has been notified to AEMO and before the final time for submission of auction quantity limits specified in the Capacity Transfer and Auction Procedures, the facility operator must determine and send to AEMO the updated auction quantity limit as soon as practicable.
