- (a) AEMO must establish a document setting out AEMO's proced ures and minimum requirements, to be known as the CTP Interface Protocol , for:
- (i) establishing and maintaining an interface ( CTP Interface ) between the systems of facility operators and AEMO (in its capacity as Operator under Part 22) for the purposes of exchanging information under these Procedures;
- (ii) establishing authorised user identities within the CTP Interface; and
- (iii) providing processes and guides for the use of the CTP Interface and communications.
- (b) AEMO may amend the CTP Interface Protocol after consulting with facility operators and must publish the current CTP Interface Protocol on its website.
