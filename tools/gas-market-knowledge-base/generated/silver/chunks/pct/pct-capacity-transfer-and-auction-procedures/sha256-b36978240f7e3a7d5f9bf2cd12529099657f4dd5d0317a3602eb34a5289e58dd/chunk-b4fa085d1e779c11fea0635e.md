- (i) DSCP is the daily service continuity payment for that gas day and capacity type;
- (ii) TC the traded capacity (in GJ) of that capacity type for which transportation services must be provided during the service continuity period in accordance with rule 639(2), having regard to relevant primary transactions for that capacity type but not including traded capacity held by a terminated primary shipper; and
- (iii) WATP is the weighted average transaction price for the gas day and capacity type, calculated as:
- (A) the sum, for each relevant primary transaction for that capacity type with a service term that includes that gas day of: the transaction price (in $/GJ) multiplied by the transaction quantity (in $/GJ/ gas day ); divided by
- (B) the sum, for each relevant primary transaction for that capacity type with a service term that includes that gas day of the transaction quantity (in GJ/ gas day ).
- (c) To avoid doubt, the daily service continuity payment for a gas day is zero if the gas day falls before the date the termination of the terminated primary contract takes effect.
