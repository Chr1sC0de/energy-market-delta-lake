- (a) Within 24 hours of becoming aware that its existing Credit Support ceases to meet all the requirements of clause A.6.2, the Auction Participant must deliver to AEMO a replacement or additional Credit Support sufficient to ensure that the Auction Participant complies with its obligations under that clause.
- (b) The Auction Participant may deliver a replacement Credit Support to AEMO at any other time.
- (c) AEMO must return any unexpired and undrawn Credit Support to the Credit Support Provider on request, provided that AEMO will continue to hold Credit Support in respect of the Auction Participant that meets the requirements of this clause A.6and takes effect on or before the return date.
- (d) AEMO must return any unexpired and undrawn Credit Support to the relevant Credit Support Provider immediately after the termination of this Agreement takes effect under clause A.2.5 unless this Agreement is continuing in relation to other parties to that Auction Agreement as provided for in clause A.2.6(b).
