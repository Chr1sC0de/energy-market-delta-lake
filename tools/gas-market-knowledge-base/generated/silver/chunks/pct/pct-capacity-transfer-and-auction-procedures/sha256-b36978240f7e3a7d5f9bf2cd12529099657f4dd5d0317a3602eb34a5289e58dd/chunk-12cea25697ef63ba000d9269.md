- (a) To be eligible (and to remain eligible) to access the Auction Platform and participate in the Capacity Auction, the Auction Participant must meet and continue to satisfy the eligibility criteria specified in the Auction Procedures.
- (b) The Auction Participant must give information to AEMO on request to verify that it continues to satisfy the eligibility criteria specified in the Auction Procedures.
- (c) The Auction Participant must notify AEMO immediately if it ceases to satisfy the eligibility criteria specified in the Auction Procedures.
