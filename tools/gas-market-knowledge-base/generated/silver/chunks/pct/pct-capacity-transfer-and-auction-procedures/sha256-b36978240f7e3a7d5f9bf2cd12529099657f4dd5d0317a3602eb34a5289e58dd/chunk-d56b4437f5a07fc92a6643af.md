- (a) Subject to clause A.14.1(b), if any dispute arises between the parties in connection with this Agreement, the party requiring it to be resolved must promptly give the other party written notice giving details of the dispute.
- (b) A dispute subject to determination as a rule dispute (as defined in the National Gas Rules) must be resolved under Part 15C of the National Gas Rules.
