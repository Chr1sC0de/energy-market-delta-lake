A facility operator for a transitional compression facility must nominate and register with AEMO one or more persons to be the authorised users of the facility operator for the purposes of uploading information under this clause 26 to the Natural Gas Services Bulletin Board .
Unless the information is provided on registration of the transitional compression facility , a facility operator for a transitional compression facility must provide information and data to AEMO under this clause 26 in the manner and form specified in the BB Data Submission Procedures published on the Natural Gas Services Bulletin Board .
