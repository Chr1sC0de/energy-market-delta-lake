Item 10(1) of Part 4 of Schedule 5 require a facility operator for an auction facility to provide to AEMO in accordance with these Procedures, information about whether any transportation service for the transportation facility is classified as a transitional firm service.
Item 10(2) of Part 4 of Schedule 5 provides that for the purposes of new rule 653(1), a facility operator for an auction facility must provide to AEMO in accordance with these Procedures, information to identify the transitional firm quantity used in the calculation of the auction quantity limits for the gas day aggregated by service point and the service points to which the quantity relates.
