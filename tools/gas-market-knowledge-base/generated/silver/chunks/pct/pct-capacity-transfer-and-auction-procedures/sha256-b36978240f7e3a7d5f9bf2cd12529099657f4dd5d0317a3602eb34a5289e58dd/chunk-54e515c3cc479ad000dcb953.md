CC or contracted capacity, Quantity to be determined for each gas day (GJ) = The transportation capacity at the compression delivery point reserved for use for gas day D by firm compression services.. CC or contracted capacity, Product component = compression receipt point. CC or contracted capacity, Quantity to be determined for each gas day (GJ) = The transportation capacity at the compression receipt point reserved for use for gas day D by firm compression services.. CC or contracted capacity, Product component = compression service facility. CC or contracted capacity, Quantity to be determined for each gas day (GJ) = The transportation capacity of the compression service facility reserved for use for gas day D by firm compression services.. DC or discretionary capacity, Product component = All service points , pipeline segments and compression service facilities. DC or discretionary capacity, Quantity to be determined for each gas day (GJ) = A positive quantity of transportation capacity at the service point or on the pipeline segment or compression service facility for gas day D determined by the facility operator in its discretion if it wishes to submit additional transportation capacity to the capacity auction that is available but not reserved for use on gas day D .
