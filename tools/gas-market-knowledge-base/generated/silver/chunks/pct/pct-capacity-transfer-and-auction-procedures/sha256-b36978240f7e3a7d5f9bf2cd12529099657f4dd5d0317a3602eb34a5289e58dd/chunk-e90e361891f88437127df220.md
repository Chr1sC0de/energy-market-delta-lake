A Credit Support Provider must have a credit rating of:
- (a) A-1 or higher for short term unsecured counterparty obligations of the entity, as rated by Standard & Poor's (Australia) Pty Ltd;
- (b) P-1 or higher for short term unsecured counterparty obligations of the entity, as rated by Moody's Investor s Service Pty Ltd; or
- (c) some other acceptable credit rating determined by AEMO.
