- (a) For each billing period , each auction participant must pay to AEMO the billing period settlement amount determined for that auction participant and billing period .
- (b) The billing period settlement amount for an auction participant for a billing period is the sum of the daily auction participant settlement amounts for the auction participant for each gas day starting in the billing period .
