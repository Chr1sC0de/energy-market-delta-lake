- (a) Subject to the Auction Procedures and this Agreement, AEMO will give the Auction Participant access to the Auction Platform to the extent required for the purpose of participating in the Capacity Auction.
- (b) AEMO may suspend the Auction Participant's access to the Auction Platform if a Suspension Event or a Default Event occurs in relation to the Auction Participant.
- (c) The Auction Participant's right of access to and use of the Auction Platform terminates on termination of this Agreement.
