- (b) An auction participant may submit separate bids for different combinations of Auction Products. In the Auction Platform, each separate bid is submitted on the same form and if any one of those bids is amended, all bids in the form are overridden by the new form when submitted.
- (c) If a bid is stated to apply to multiple gas days, it will be treated for the purposes of the capacity auction as a separate bid for each gas day in the bid.
- (d) For each bid:
- (i) the bid price must be in $/GJ and must be expressed to up to four decimal places;
- (ii) the bid price must be $0.0001/GJ or higher;
- (iii) the bid price must not be more than $50/GJ; and
- (iv) the bid quantity must be at least 1 GJ and must be expressed as a whole number of GJ.
