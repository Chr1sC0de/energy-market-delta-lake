- (a) An Auction Participant must pay interest on any unpaid moneys due and payable by it under this Agreement at the Interest Rate, calculated as simple interest on a daily basis, for the period commencing on the date payment was due and ending on the date payment is made.
- (b) Any payment received or applied by AEMO in respect of an Auction Participant for a Billing Period is taken to be made, and may be applied by AEMO, in satisfaction of the Auction Fees payable to AEMO by that Auction Participant (as specified in the relevant final or Revised Auction Statement issued to that Auction Participant) before it is applied by AEMO in satisfaction of any other obligation or liability.
