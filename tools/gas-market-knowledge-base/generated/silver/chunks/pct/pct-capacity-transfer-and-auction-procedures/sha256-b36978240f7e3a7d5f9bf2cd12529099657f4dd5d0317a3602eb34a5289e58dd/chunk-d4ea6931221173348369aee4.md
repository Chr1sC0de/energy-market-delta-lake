- (a) This clause 27.1 applies in relation to transitional firm services . A transitional firm service is, in general terms, a transportation service that:
- (i) is currently treated as firm once scheduled (e.g. some as available and authorised overrun services);
- (ii) is used for the supply of gas for consumption by a gas-fired generator that is a market generating unit;
- (iii) for which at least one service point is either a point at which gas is supplied for consumption by a market generating unit, or is on another transportation facility that is used to receive gas for onward transportation to a market generating unit; and
