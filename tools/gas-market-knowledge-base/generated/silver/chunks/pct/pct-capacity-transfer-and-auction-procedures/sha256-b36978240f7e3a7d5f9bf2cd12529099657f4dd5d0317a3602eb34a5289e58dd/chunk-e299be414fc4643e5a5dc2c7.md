- (a) The Auction Platform will create a record of bids, auction results and settlement ( Capacity Auction Records ).
- (b) AEMO must maintain the Capacity Auction Records for a period of seven years after the record was created.
- (c) The auction participant may (at its cost) request copies of Capacity Auction Records that record the relevant activity of the auction participant . AEMO will use reasonable endeavours to provide the information requested if the information is available, subject to:
- (i) the information access restrictions in clause 24.2;
- (ii) payment of AEMO's reasonable cost of retrieving and providing copies of the information; and
- (iii) agreement on any other terms reasonably requested by AEMO, including as to confidentiality of the information.
