- (a) Each of the following is a Default Event under this Agreement:
- (i) the Auction Participant does not pay an amount due for payment by it to AEMO under this Agreement, or fails to comply with a Margin Call in accordance with clause A.6.9(c), by the appointed time on the due date;
- (ii) AEMO does not receive payment in full of any amount claimed by AEMO under any Credit Support in respect of the Auction Participant, within 90 minutes after the due time for payment of that claim;
- (iii) the Auction Participant admits to the AER that it has breached, or is declared by a court to have breached, the Market Conduct Rules, and AEMO reasonably considers that:
- (A) the breach is ongoing and is likely to have a material adverse effect on the Capacity Auction; and
- (B) the Auction Participant is unlikely to remedy or remove the circumstances leading to that breach within a reasonable period of time;
- (iv) the Auction Participant ceases to meet the criteria for registration under the Auction Procedures;
