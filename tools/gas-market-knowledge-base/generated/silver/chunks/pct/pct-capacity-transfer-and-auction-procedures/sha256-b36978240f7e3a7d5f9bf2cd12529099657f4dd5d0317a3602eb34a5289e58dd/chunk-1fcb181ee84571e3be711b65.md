- (a) The National Gas Rules contain Market Conduct Rules applicable to the Auction Participant in relation to its participation in the Capacity Auction.
- (b) If AEMO has reasonable grounds to believe that any act or omission of the Auction Participant on or in relation to the Auction Platform breaches the Market Conduct Rules, AEMO may do any one or more of the following:
- (i) raise the matter with the Auction Participant and, if it is satisfied with the Auction Participant's response, take no further action, or take no further action subject to the Auction Participant complying with conditions agreed between AEMO and the Auction Participant; or
- (ii) refer the conduct to the AER.
