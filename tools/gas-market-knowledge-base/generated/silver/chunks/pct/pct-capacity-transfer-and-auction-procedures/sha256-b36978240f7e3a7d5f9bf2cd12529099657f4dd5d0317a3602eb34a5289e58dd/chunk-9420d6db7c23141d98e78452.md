If the Relevant Market Amount payable by AEMO to a Payee under a Relevant Instrument for the billing period is reduced in accordance with clause 22.1.4 and AEMO subsequently receives payment of any part of the shortfall amount, AEMO must:
- (a) recalculate the Relevant Market Amount payable by AEMO in accordance with clause 22.1.4 taking into account the subsequent payment; and
- (b) pay to the Payee pursuant to the next invoice the difference between the revised Relevant Market Amount calculated under paragraph 22.1.5(a) and the Relevant Market Amount paid by the Payee.
