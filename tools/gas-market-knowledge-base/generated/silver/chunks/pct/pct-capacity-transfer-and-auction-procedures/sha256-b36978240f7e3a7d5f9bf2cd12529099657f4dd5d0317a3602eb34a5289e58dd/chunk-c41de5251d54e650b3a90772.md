This Schedule sets out the Capacity Transfer and Auction Timetable applicable from 6:00 am AEST on 1 October 2019. Table 1 is the timetable for the transfer of capacity purchased on the Gas Trading Exchange as a Day-Ahead Product (and for which the trading window will be D-1 from market open to 12:30 pm). Table 2 is the timetable for the capacity auction . Table 3 is the timetable for the transfer of capacity purchased on the Gas Trading Exchange other than as a Day-Ahead Product (for which the trading window closes by D-2).
Where AEMO must notify an extension of time, it may at the same time notify the extensions to other activities affected by the delay.
