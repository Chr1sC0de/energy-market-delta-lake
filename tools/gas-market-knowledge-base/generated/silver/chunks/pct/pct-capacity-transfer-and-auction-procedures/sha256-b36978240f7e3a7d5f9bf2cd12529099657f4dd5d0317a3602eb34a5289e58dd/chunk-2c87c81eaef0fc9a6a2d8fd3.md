- (a) A forward haul product will have associated with it the following product components:
- (i) forward haul receipt point;
- (ii) receipt zone ;
- (iii) each forward haul pipeline segment ;
- (iv) delivery zone ; and
- (v) forward haul delivery point.
- (b) A compression product will have associated with it the following product components:
- (i) compression receipt point;
- (ii) compression receipt zone ;
- (iii) compression service facility ;
- (iv) compression delivery zone ; and
- (v) compression delivery point.
- (c) A backhaul product will have associated with it the following product components:
- (i) backhaul receipt point;
- (ii) each backhaul pipeline segment ; and
- (iii) backhaul delivery point.
