- (a) An auction participant must register its principal organisational contact, and any other person authorised to submit information on its behalf using the Auction Platform, as its authorised users.
- (b) Authorised users must be registered using the applicable registration form. Each form must be signed by an authorised signatory of the auction participant and where required by the form, the authorised user.
- (c) An auction participant must ensure that its authorised users comply with the terms of use for the Auction Platform in Division 4.
