Rule 656 provides for AEMO to delay or cancel the capacity auction or suspend the participation of an auction facility or part of an auction facility in the capacity auction for a period specified by AEMO in the circumstances provided for in these Procedures.
- (a) AEMO may delay or cancel the running of the capacity auction for a gas day :
- (i) in the circumstances provided for in the Capacity Transfer and Auction Timetable;
- (ii) if the Auction Solver is unavailable to run the capacity auction for any reason; or
- (iii) if, in AEMO's opinion, the Auction Solver is producing manifestly erroneous auction results.
