- (a) Information about nominated and forecast use of a transitional compression facility must be in TJ/day.
- (b) Aggregate nominations of natural gas to be compressed for a gas day must be provided by no later than one hour after the start of the gas day for the relevant transitional compression facility .
- (c) The forecast quantity of natural gas to be compressed by the transitional compression facility must be provided no later than 7 pm on each day or as soon as practicable after the facility operator has been provided with the required information by shippers, whichever is the later and may be provided to AEMO as soon as practicable after being received.
- (d) AEMO will publish on the Natural Gas Services Bulletin Board the aggregate nominations and the forecast quantities of natural gas to be compressed for up to the next 7 gas day s. A facility operator may provide that information to the Natural Gas Services Bulletin Board for a longer period if available.
- (e) If any of the information changes for the transitional compression facility for a gas day by 10% of nameplate rating , the facility operator must provide updated information to AEMO as soon as practicable after it is received by the facility operator .
