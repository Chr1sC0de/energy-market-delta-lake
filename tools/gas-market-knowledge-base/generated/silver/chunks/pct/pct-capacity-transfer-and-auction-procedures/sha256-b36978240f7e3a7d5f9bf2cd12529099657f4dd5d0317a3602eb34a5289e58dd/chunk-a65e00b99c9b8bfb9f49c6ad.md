- (a) Subject to this clause A.7.3, the Auction Participant may notify AEMO of a query concerning the amount payable in an Auction Settlement Statement before 9:00am on the 1st Business Day of the 4th Billing Period after the end of the Billing Period for the Auction Settlement Statement and AEMO and the Auction Participant must each use reasonable endeavours to resolve that query before that cut-off time.
- (b) If the Auction Participant's query is about an error (including an omission) or discrepancy in settlement information provided by a Facility Operator to AEMO and used by AEMO to determine the auction amount (as defined in Part 25 of the National Gas Rules) payable by the Auction Participant, the Auction Participant must comply with the Auction Procedures and National Gas Rules in relation to the query and any dispute about that query must be resolved as a rule dispute under Part 15C of the National Gas Rules.
