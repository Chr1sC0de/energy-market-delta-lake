- (a) During a Trading Halt, AEMO may suspend access to and use of the Auction Platform by the Auction Participant that is subject to the Trading Halt in whole or in part, and AEMO may vary the extent of the suspension at any time having regard to subsequent events or circumstances.
- (b) AEMO may withdraw or cancel any bid submitted by the Auction Participant while it is subject to a Trading Halt, whether the bid was submitted before or after the Trading Halt commenced.
- (c) The Auction Participant must while it is subject to a Trading Halt continue to perform its obligations under this Agreement (including its obligations with respect to payment).
