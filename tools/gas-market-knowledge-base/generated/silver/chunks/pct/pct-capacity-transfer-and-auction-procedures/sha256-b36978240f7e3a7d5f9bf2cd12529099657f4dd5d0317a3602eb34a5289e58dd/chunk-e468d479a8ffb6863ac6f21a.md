- (a) The Auction Participant must pay the Auction Fees specified and published by AEMO from time to time.
- (b) The Auction Fees may include:
- (i) an application fee;
- (ii) a fee payable upon the acceptance of an application;
- (iii) annual fees for participation in the Capacity Auction;
- (iv) a fee for purchases in the Capacity Auction; and
- (v) ad hoc fees for services provided by AEMO to the Auction Participant from time to time, including training, assistance or advice in relation to access to and use of the Auction Platform.
