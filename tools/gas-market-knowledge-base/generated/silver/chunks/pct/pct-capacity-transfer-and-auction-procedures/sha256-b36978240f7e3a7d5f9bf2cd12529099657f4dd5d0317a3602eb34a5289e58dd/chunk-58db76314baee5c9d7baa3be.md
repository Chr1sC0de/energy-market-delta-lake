- (a) If the Auction Participant is eligible to be a Credit Support Provider, the Auction Participant will not have a Trading Limit or a Trading Margin.
- (b) AEMO must calculate the Trading Limit, Prudential Exposure and Trading Margin for the Auction Participant at least once each Business Day and make the calculations available to the Auction Participant.
