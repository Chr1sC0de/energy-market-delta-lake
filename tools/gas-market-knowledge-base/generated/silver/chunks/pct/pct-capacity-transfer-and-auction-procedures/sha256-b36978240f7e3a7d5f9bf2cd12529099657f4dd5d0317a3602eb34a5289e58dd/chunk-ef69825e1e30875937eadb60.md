Rule 626(1) requires the facility operator for a Part 24 facility to provide to AEMO and keep up to date a specification of each service point at or between which transportation services are or may be provided by means of a Part 24 facility and each park service point, in a form that complies with the Procedures and is suitable for publication in the transportation service point register.
Rule 626(2) allows the transportation service provider to amend the specification of a service point or a park service point in accordance with these Procedures.
