Rule 639(3) requires AEMO to determine an amount for the provision of transportation services during the service continuity period and, in its capacity as Operator, pay that amount in accordance with these Procedures.
- (a) If the date the termination of a terminated primary contract takes effect is after the end of the service continuity period, or there are no relevant primary transactions in relation to that terminated primary contract, AEMO is not required to make a payment to the facility operator under this clause.
- (b) If the date the termination of a terminated primary contract takes effect is before the end of the service continuity period, for each gas day in the service continuity period, AEMO must pay to the relevant facility operator the amount determined under clause 10.4.
- (c) AEMO will include a payment under paragraph (b) in the statement and invoice issued to the facility operator for capacity auction settlement under Part 25 of the Rules and will pay those amounts in accordance with the auction settlement timetable under that Part, subject to adjustment in accordance with clause 22.
