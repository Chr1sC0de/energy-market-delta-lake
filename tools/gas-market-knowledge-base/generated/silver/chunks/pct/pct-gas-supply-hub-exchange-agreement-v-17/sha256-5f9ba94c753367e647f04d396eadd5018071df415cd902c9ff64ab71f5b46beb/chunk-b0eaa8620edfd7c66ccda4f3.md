The Custom Delivery Period is for consecutive gas days from the start date (inclusive) to the end date (inclusive) as submitted and confirmed for the Pre-matched Trade or Broker Pre-matched Trade. The end date for the Custom Delivery Period must not be greater than 364 calendar days from the date on which the Trading Participant or Broker Participant (as applicable) confirm the details of the of the Pre-matched Trade or Broker Pre-matched Trade in the Trading System.
