- (a) In this clause 20.5, the Defaulting Participant is the Trading Participant in relation to which the Default Event occurred.
- (b) At the time of giving a termination notice to a Defaulting Participant under clause 20.4(a), or as soon as reasonably practicable afterwards, the Operator must give a Close Out Notice to the Defaulting Participant that close out and offset will apply to that Trading Participant with effect on and from a Gas Day specified in the Close Out Notice (Close Out Effective Date), which must be a Gas Day commencing at or after the time of the Close Out Notice and within 5 Business Days after the termination notice.
