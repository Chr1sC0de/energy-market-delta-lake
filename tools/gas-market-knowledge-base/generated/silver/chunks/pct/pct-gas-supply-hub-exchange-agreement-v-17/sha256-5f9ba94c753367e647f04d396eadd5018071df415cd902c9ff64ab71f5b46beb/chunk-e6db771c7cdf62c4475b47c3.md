If the Buyer or Seller for a Location Swap Transaction is the Affected Party and claims Force Majeure relief under this clause 25 with respect to its obligation to receive or deliver a quantity of gas in respect of that Transaction, the other party to the Location Swap Transaction is not 'responsible'
within the meaning of clause 15.2(c) for a Delivery Variance Quantity arising in respect of the corresponding obligation to deliver or receive up to that same quantity of gas.
