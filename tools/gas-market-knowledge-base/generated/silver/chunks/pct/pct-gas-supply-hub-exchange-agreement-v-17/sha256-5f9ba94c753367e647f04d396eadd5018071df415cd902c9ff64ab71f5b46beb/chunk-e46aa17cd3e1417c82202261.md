- (a) As soon as reasonably practicable after receipt of a valid capacity listing request, the Operator must post a corresponding capacity listing on the Trading System (available for viewing by Trading Participants and Viewing Participants) containing the following details in accordance with the request (as applicable):
- (i) the gas transportation pipeline or other facility;
- (ii) the receipt and delivery points on the pipeline between which the capacity is available or required;
- (iii) the dates between which the capacity is available or required;
- (iv) whether the listing is for a sale or purchase;
- (v) the contact details of the listing party; and
- (vi) any other details reasonably determined by the Operator, other than Confidential Information.
- (b) The Operator must remove or close a capacity listing on any expiry date specified in the capacity listing request.
- (c) To avoid doubt, neither a capacity listing request nor a listing of capacity on the Trading System is an offer capable of acceptance by another person.
