- (a) The Operator will determine and make available to all Members a trading timetable specifying:
- (i) the Trading Hours for the Exchange; and
- (ii) for each Product, the Trading Window in respect of each specified Delivery Period, consistent with the Product Specification.
- (b) The Operator may only amend the trading timetable after consultation with Market Participants.
