On-The-Day (OTD), Trading Window = For Gas Day D, Trading Hours on that same Gas Day.. Day-Ahead (DA), Trading Window = For DA where Delivery Netting applies to the Premium Product and Base Product: • For Gas Day D, from 9:00 am to 1:00 pm on D-1. For DA where Delivery Netting does not apply to the Premium Product and Base Product: • For Gas Day D, from 1:00 pm to 7:00 pm on D-1.. Daily (D), Trading Window = For Gas Day D, Trading Hours on each of Gas Day D-7 to Gas Day D-2.. Weekly (W), Trading Window = For a Delivery Period commencing on Gas Day D, Trading Hours on each Gas Day commencing 4 weeks prior to Gas Day D and ending on D-2.
Monthly (M), Trading Window = For a Delivery Period commencing on Gas Day D, Trading Hours on each Gas Day commencing 3 calendar months and 1 calendar day prior to the calendar month in which Gas Day D falls and ending on D-2 prior the commencement of the calendar month in which Gas Day D falls.
