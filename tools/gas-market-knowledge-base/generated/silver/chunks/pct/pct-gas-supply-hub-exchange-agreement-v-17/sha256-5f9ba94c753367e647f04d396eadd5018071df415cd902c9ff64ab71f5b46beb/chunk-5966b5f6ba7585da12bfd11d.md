Definition = An amount representing the Dollar Reallocation Amounts and the amounts payable for Energy Reallocation Quantities debited to a Market Participant (in which case the amount is payable by the Market Participant to the Operator) or credited to a Market Participant (in which case the amount is payable by the Operator to the Market Participant) in respect of a Gas Day.. Settlement Amount, Definition = A net aggregate amount for a Market Participant in respect of a Billing Period - see clause 17.3.3(b).
